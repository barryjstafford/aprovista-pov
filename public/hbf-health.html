Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Member Claims Adjudication Cycle-Time & Rework

Member and provider claims flow through intake, adjudication, exception handling, and payment. Manual holds, rework loops, and inconsistent exception routing drive cycle-time variance and directly feed member NPS drag. AFPI mines the claim ID as case key across the claims engine and workflow tool, quantifies rework loops by variant, and pinpoints the top 3 controllable drivers — then hands the top automatable steps to the HBF Agentforce backlog. Metric: median claim cycle time and first-pass-yield.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Claims engine, Financial Services Cloud

Key Contact: David Beer (GM, Member Connect)

2. Provider Onboarding & Contract-to-Payment

Onboarding hospitals, ancillary providers, and Life Ready physio partners spans credentialing, contracting, fee-schedule loading, and first payment. Delays compound provider dissatisfaction and complaint volume to PHIO. AFPI mines the provider ID as case key across CRM + provider master + finance, exposes hand-off delays and rework, and shows where SLAs slip. Metric: onboarding-to-first-payment days and complaint volume.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Provider master, Financial Services Cloud

3. Member Onboarding & May-Round Retention Journey

HBF competes with Bupa, Medibank and nib for switchers each May premium round. New-member onboarding — quote, join, first-claim, retention save — is a multi-system funnel on FSC + policy admin where drop-off drives churn. AFPI maps the actual member journey (not the designed one), identifies the variants that lead to retention vs churn, and surfaces the earliest intervention point that the Member Agent can act on. Metric: 90-day retention and cost-to-acquire recovery.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial Services Cloud, policy admin
