Topline POV: Apromore can help HBF Health Limited by mining member-claims lifecycle, provider onboarding, and prior-authorisation workflows to compress claims cycle-time, cut avoidable rework, and give APRA/PHIO-grade evidence of process conformance — all through a no-code path that lets a single business analyst do the work of a SQL+PQL pair.

Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Claims Adjudication Cycle-Time & Rework Reduction

Member and provider claims flow through intake, adjudication, exception handling, and payment. Manual holds, rework loops, and inconsistent exception routing drive cycle-time variance and member NPS drag. AFPI mines the claim ID as case key across the claims engine + workflow tool, quantifies rework loops by variant, and pinpoints the top 3 controllable drivers. Metric moved: median claim cycle-time and first-pass-yield.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Claims engine, workflow/BPM

Key Contact: COO / Chief Claims Officer (Claims Operations)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Provider Onboarding & Contract-to-Payment

Onboarding hospitals, ancillary providers, and Life Ready physio partners spans credentialing, contracting, fee-schedule loading, and first payment. Delays compound provider dissatisfaction and complaint volume to PHIO. AFPI mines the provider ID as case key across CRM + provider master + finance, exposes hand-off delays and rework, and shows where SLAs slip. Metric moved: onboarding-to-first-payment days and complaint volume.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Provider master, Salesforce

Key Contact: Head of Provider Network (Provider Relations)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Member Onboarding & Retention Journey

HBF competes with Bupa, Medibank and nib for switchers each May premium-round. New-member onboarding — quote, join, first-claim, retention save — is a multi-system funnel where drop-off drives churn. AFPI maps the actual member journey (not the designed one), identifies the variants that lead to retention vs churn, and surfaces the earliest intervention point. Metric moved: 90-day retention rate and cost-to-acquire recovery.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce, policy admin

Key Contact: CMO / Head of Member Experience (Member Growth)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
4. Prior-Authorisation & Utilisation Management

Prior-auth for high-cost procedures (surgery, imaging, ancillary bundles) is a compliance-heavy workflow with clinical review hand-offs. Variance in approval time exposes HBF to member complaints and reputational risk under PHIO. AFPI mines the auth-request ID across intake, clinical review, and decision, quantifies conformance to the documented policy, and highlights bottleneck reviewers. Metric moved: median approval time and PHIO-relevant complaint rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Utilisation management, claims engine

Key Contact: Chief Medical Officer (Clinical Governance)

5. Complaints & Dispute-Resolution Conformance

Complaints to PHIO and internal disputes are a compliance-graded workflow — every step and hand-off must be evidenced. Manual RCA today is retrospective and expensive. AFPI turns the complaint case ID into a conformance-checked process, showing where the actual path deviates from the documented SLA-bound process. Metric moved: PHIO-referred complaint rate and time-to-resolution.
