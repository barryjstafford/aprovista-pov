Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ISO 9001 Quality Management System Controls

As a regulated water-filtration manufacturer supplying plumbers and OEMs, Puretec runs under ISO 9001-style quality controls covering incoming inspection, batch release, and non-conformance handling. Compliance Center codifies each control as a controlled workflow with segregation of duties and continuous evidence, replacing spreadsheet-and-attestation practice. It also links non-conformance events back to the process map so root cause is visible, not guessed. Metric moved: audit findings per surveillance visit and time-to-close on non-conformance reports.

2. Product Warranty & Recall Traceability across 4,000+ SKUs

Water-filter and cartridge products carry warranty and consumer-protection obligations under Australian Consumer Law, and any batch-level defect triggers traceability requirements back to lot.
