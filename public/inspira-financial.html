Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. IRA / HSA Account Opening & Funding

Inspira administers millions of custodial IRA, HSA, and health & benefits accounts — the new-account-to-first-funding cycle spans partner intake, KYC/CIP, custody setup, and ACH funding through MuleSoft integrations. Delays and rework here directly slow AUC growth and drive inbound service tickets. Mining the FSC + MuleSoft + custody event streams exposes rework loops, manual KYC re-checks, and funding-latency variants, then feeds the winning happy-path into the Agentforce for Service rollout. Metric: reduce open-to-funded cycle time 30-40% and cut abandoned-open rework.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial Services Cloud, MuleSoft Anypoint

Key Contact: Linda O’Boyle (Managing Director, Application Development)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Distributions, RMDs & Rollovers

Distribution processing — required minimum distributions, in-service withdrawals, and 401(k)/IRA rollovers to and from other custodians — is governed by ERISA and IRS timing rules, so any process variance surfaces as compliance risk and CX pain during tax season. Apromore Phase 2 is positioned around workflow discovery, and this is the natural first target: clear regulatory deadlines, heavy manual case handling, and a proven Regrello/Apromore stack already in play. AFPI would surface off-happy-path cases, aging inventory, and hand-off delays across call center, ops, and tax reporting. Metric: cut aged-distribution inventory by 50% and eliminate late-RMD penalty exposure.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Apromore, Regrello

Key Contact: Karyn DeFlaco (COO)

3. Post-Merger Custody Platform Integration (Millennium Trust + Inspira)

Inspira has explicitly named fragmented systems from the Millennium Trust + Inspira/Alegeus/PayFlex combinations as a top integration challenge (Chicago Agentforce dinner, June 2025). Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ERISA / IRS Distribution & RMD Controls

Every RMD, in-service withdrawal, and rollover carries hard IRS/ERISA deadlines and prescribed control paths — today most of the assurance is spreadsheet-plus-signoff. Compliance Center layers conformance checking on the mined distribution process to prove, with evidence, that every event followed the required control path. Directly extends the Phase 1 Apromore footprint into an audit-ready controls story. Metric: audit-prep hours cut 40-60% and late-distribution penalty exposure eliminated.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Apromore, custody record-keeping platform

Key Contact: Karyn DeFlaco (COO)
