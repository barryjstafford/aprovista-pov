Topline POV: Apromore can help Inspira Financial Trust, LLC by mining its IRA/HSA account-opening, distribution, and case-handling event streams to cut cycle time and lift Agentforce for Service containment — extending the Phase 1 Apromore footprint into a documented, audit-ready target state before FSC and Agentforce lock in.

Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. IRA / HSA Account Opening & Funding

Inspira administers millions of custodial IRA, HSA, and health & benefit accounts; the new-account-to-first-funding cycle spans partner intake, KYC/CIP, account setup, and ACH/check funding — one of the highest-volume, most-touched processes in trust custody. Delays or rework here directly slow AUC growth and generate service tickets. AFPI would mine the SFA + FSC + MuleSoft event streams to expose rework loops, manual KYC re-checks, and funding-latency variants, then feed the winning happy-path into an Agentforce for Service agent. Metric moved: reduce account-open-to-funded cycle time by 30–40% and cut abandoned-open rework.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, Financial Services Cloud

Key Contact: John Balevic (Executive Sponsor)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Distributions, RMDs & Rollovers

Distribution processing (required minimum distributions, in-service withdrawals, rollovers to/from other custodians) is regulated by ERISA and IRS timing rules, and any process variance surfaces as compliance risk and CX pain during tax season. Apromore Phase 2 is already positioned around workflow discovery — this is the natural first mining target because it has clear regulatory deadlines and heavy manual case-handling. AFPI would surface off-happy-path cases, aging inventory, and hand-off delays between call center, ops, and tax reporting. Metric moved: cut aged-distribution inventory by 50% and eliminate late-RMD penalty exposure.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Apromore, Regrello

Key Contact: Meg Zwick (Head of Distributions Operations)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Case & Service Ticket Resolution (FSC + Agentforce for Service)

The FSC PxE > AF1 for Service opp indicates Inspira is standing up Agentforce for Service on top of Financial Services Cloud — but agent throughput and containment only improve if the underlying case-handling process is well-understood. AFPI mines the Service Cloud case history to find repeat contact drivers, escalation loops, and knowledge gaps, then hands the discovered patterns to the Agentforce team so the agent is trained on real variants, not a whiteboard workflow. Metric moved: raise Agentforce case containment 15–25 points and cut average handle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial Services Cloud, Agentforce for Service

Key Contact: David Vogt (Head of Service Operations)

4. Partner / Advisor Onboarding & Data Handoff

Inspira's model depends on advisor and employer-plan channel partners feeding accounts and contributions in; the onboarding-to-active-flow process runs across MuleSoft integrations, contracts, and operations teams. Rework or delays here directly slow the growth engine and are almost never measured end-to-end. AFPI would mine the MuleSoft transaction logs and CRM stage history to expose where partner activations stall and quantify revenue-at-risk. Metric moved: shorten partner-to-first-contribution cycle by 25% and lift activation conversion.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft, Sales Cloud

5. Compliance Reporting & Custody Controls (Compliance Center wedge)

As a trust company Inspira has continuous ERISA, SEC, and IRS reporting obligations plus internal custody controls; today most of that assurance is spreadsheet-and-signoff. AFPI's Compliance Center wedge would layer conformance checking on the mined processes to prove — with evidence — that every distribution, transfer, and reporting event followed the required control path. This is the "why AFPI vs. generic BPM" story: same event data, but conformance and audit-ready evidence for free. Metric moved: cut audit-prep hours 40–60% and reduce control-exception rework.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Apromore, Financial Services Cloud
