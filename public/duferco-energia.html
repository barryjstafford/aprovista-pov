Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Meter-to-Cash for Retail Electricity & Gas (Luce & Gas)

Duferco Energia sells electricity and gas to ~700k households, condominiums, SMEs and large corporates on PUN/PSV-indexed plans — every new fornitura, plan change, and re-indexing touches MuleSoft, Vlocity Utilities CPQ and Billing Plus. Billing exceptions, delayed switching and payment-plan rework are chronic and drive both inbound call volume and credit-note issuance. AFPI mines the end-to-end meter-to-cash event log to quantify DSO, first-bill-right rate and self-service leakage, then feeds a concrete target-state into the ongoing MC Next migration and the $369k MuleSoft renewal. Metric moved: DSO, first-bill-right %, cost-to-serve per customer.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft, Billing Plus

2. Customer Switching & Contract Onboarding (Voltura / Subentro / Cambio Fornitore)

Italian retail energy is fiercely churn-driven — Duferco Energia must onboard new customers from incumbents (voltura, subentro, cambio fornitore) within ARERA-mandated windows and hand off cleanly between commercial, distributor (SII) and billing systems. Every stall between contract signature and first bill is a churn risk and an ARERA exposure. AFPI mines the CPQ-to-activation event log across Sales Cloud, Vlocity Utilities and the SII integration mediated by MuleSoft to expose which variants blow the switching SLA and why (documentation loops, POD/PDR mismatches, credit-check bounces). Metric moved: ARERA-compliant switching %, contract-to-first-bill cycle time, early-life churn.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Vlocity Utilities CPQ, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Head of Retail Sales — TBD (Direttore Vendite, Duferco Energia)

3. Energy Trading Front-to-Back Settlement

Duferco Energia operates a dedicated wholesale trading desk on Italian and European power / gas markets — every trade must flow cleanly from front-office execution through risk, back-office confirmation, settlement and regulatory reporting (REMIT, EMIR). Failed confirmations, break-fixes and manual reconciliations are where P&L bleeds and audit findings emerge. AFPI mines the trade-lifecycle event log to expose which counterparties, product types and desk workflows generate the most rework, and how long confirmations really sit before matching. Metric moved: STP rate, break resolution time, REMIT reporting cycle time, operational-risk loss events.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Trading & Risk platform, MuleSoft

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ARERA Complaint SLA & Indennizzo Controls (Reclami Scritti)

ARERA enforces strict SLAs on Duferco Energia's handling of written complaints (reclami scritti), billing disputes and quality-of-service events — miss the deadline and the retailer pays an automatic indennizzo. Compliance Center codifies the complaint-to-resolution workflow with controlled SLAs, sign-offs and continuous evidence for the ARERA regulator. Every reopened complaint or missed deadline is captured as a control event, not a spreadsheet line. Metric moved: ARERA SLA-compliant %, indennizzo payout, complaint reopen rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Billing Plus
