Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Meter-to-Cash for Retail Electricity & Gas (Luce & Gas)

Duferco Energia sells electricity and gas to households, condominiums, SMEs and large corporates on PUN/PSV-indexed plans — every new fornitura, plan change, and price re-indexing touches MuleSoft, Vlocity Utilities CPQ and Billing Plus. Billing exceptions, delayed switching (voltura/subentro), and payment-plan rework are chronic and drive both inbound call volume and credit-note issuance. AFPI mines the end-to-end meter-to-cash event log to quantify DSO, first-bill-right rate and self-service leakage, then feeds a concrete target-state into the ongoing MC Next migration and the MuleSoft renewal. Metric moved: DSO, first-bill-right %, cost-to-serve per customer, first-call resolution.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft, Vlocity Utilities CPQ (Billing Plus)

2. Customer Switching & Contract Onboarding (Voltura / Subentro / Cambio Fornitore)

Italian retail energy is fiercely churn-driven — Duferco Energia must onboard new customers from incumbents (voltura, subentro, cambio fornitore) within ARERA-mandated windows and hand-off cleanly between commercial, distributor (SII) and billing systems. Every stall between contract signature and first bill is a churn risk and an ARERA exposure. AFPI mines the CPQ-to-activation event log across Sales Cloud, Vlocity Utilities and the SII integration mediated by MuleSoft to expose which variants blow the switching SLA and why (documentation loops, POD/PDR mismatches, credit-check bounces). Metric moved: ARERA-compliant switching %, contract-to-first-bill cycle time, early-life churn rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Vlocity Utilities CPQ, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Head of Retail Sales — TBD (Direttore Vendite, Duferco Energia)

3. Energy Trading Front-to-Back Settlement

Duferco Energia operates a dedicated wholesale trading desk on Italian and European power/gas markets — every trade must flow cleanly from front-office execution through risk, back-office confirmation, settlement and regulatory reporting (REMIT, EMIR). Failed confirmations, break-fixes and manual reconciliations are where P&L bleeds and audit findings emerge. AFPI mines the trade-lifecycle event log to expose which counterparties, product types and desk workflows generate the most rework, and how long confirmations really sit before matching. Directly reinforces the audit-grade posture that regulators and internal risk demand. Metric moved: STP rate, break resolution time, REMIT reporting cycle time, operational-risk loss events.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft, Trading & Risk platform (TBD)

4. Customer Service & Complaint Handling (ARERA Reclami)

ARERA enforces strict SLAs on Duferco Energia's handling of written complaints (reclami scritti), billing disputes and quality-of-service events — miss the deadline and the customer gets an automatic indennizzo (compensation) that the retailer eats. Complaints ricochet across Service Cloud, MuleSoft, the billing stack and back-office analysts, with plenty of dead time between hops. AFPI mines the reclamo-to-resolution event log to expose which complaint categories bust the ARERA clock, where handoffs stall, and which agents/BUs are outliers. Feeds a real ROI case for Agentforce and self-service investment. Metric moved: ARERA SLA-compliant %, indennizzo payout, complaint reopen rate, cost-per-complaint.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Head of Customer Operations — TBD (Responsabile Customer Care, Duferco Energia)

5. E-Mobility Operations (Duferco Mobility & Elettra Car Sharing)

Duferco Mobility and the Elettra electric car-sharing platform are the group's fastest-growing bet on the energy transition — vehicle onboarding, session activation, charging authorisation, and post-trip billing span the mobility app, the OCPP charging network, MuleSoft and the retail-energy billing stack. Every failed charge session, disputed trip fee, or asset-availability gap is a direct hit on customer experience and unit economics. AFPI mines the session-to-invoice event log to expose fallout patterns, authorisation failures and vehicle-utilisation blockers — a clean fit with the active Mobile Publisher and MC Next investments. Metric moved: session success rate, revenue per vehicle-day, dispute rate, activation cycle time.
