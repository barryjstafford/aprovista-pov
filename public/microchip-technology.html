This drives variant explosion in how a customer PO becomes revenue — the exact scenario Apromore's variant explorer was built for. Mining the SAP sales-order and billing tables against the Sales Cloud pipeline exposes the divergent paths that delay cash conversion. Metric moved: DSO reduction and quote-to-order cycle time.

2. Fab & Assembly/Test Supply Chain Cycle Time (Gresham OR + Colorado Springs + Chachoengsao + Calamba)

Microchip runs Fab 4 (Oregon), Fab 5 (Colorado Springs), and assembly/test in Thailand and the Philippines — with wafer starts flowing to global test then to distribution. Cycle-time slippage between these nodes is the single biggest lever on inventory and lead-times, and semi customers are hyper-sensitive to lead-time promises. Apromore mines the MES + SAP transactional logs to reveal actual node-to-node cycle time versus planned and pinpoints where blueprints stall (the same "20-min becoming 6-second" and "120-min print job" pain Juma flagged in the weekly leadership sync). Metric moved: wafer-to-ship cycle-time variance.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP ECC (MM/PP), MES
