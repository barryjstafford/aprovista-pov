Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Order-to-Delivery for Custom Trucks

Daimler Truck's build-to-order model spans thousands of chassis, powertrain, and body-builder configurations across Mercedes-Benz Trucks, Freightliner, Western Star, FUSO, and BharatBenz — and long lead times, missed customer promise dates, and finished-goods write-downs are chronic CFO-level pain post-IPO. AFPI mines the SAP order and MES event logs to expose where variants stall, which options trigger rework, and which plants beat vs. miss cycle time. The wedge is a 3–4 week diagnostic that pairs directly with the in-flight Customer 360 Data Cloud opp to unify order status across SAP, dealer DMS, and CRM. Metric moved: on-time delivery to promise date +8–12pp, WIP working capital -5%.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP (Order/MES), Dealer DMS

Key Contact: Karin Rådström (CEO)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Aftersales & Warranty Claims Handling

Warranty is the single largest controllable cost lever in commercial vehicles — every day a claim sits in dealer queues drags NPS and cash. Today the process spans dealer portals, ServiceNow tickets, and SAP warranty modules with heavy manual triage across Mercedes-Benz Trucks, Freightliner, Western Star, FUSO and BharatBenz. AFPI process-mines the claim event log to find rework loops, dealer outliers, and codes that consistently escalate to engineering, then feeds an Agentforce Service Agent (see the Agentic Pilot and ServiceNow Integration opps) to auto-triage and route. Metric moved: warranty cycle time -30–40%, warranty leakage -3–5%.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP (Warranty), ServiceNow

Key Contact: Achim Puchert (CEO, Mercedes-Benz Trucks)

3. Procure-to-Pay & Supplier Onboarding

With 20,000+ direct suppliers across four brands and a full BEV/H2 supplier stand-up underway, PO exception rates, invoice mismatches, and supplier-onboarding lead time bleed working capital and delay new programs. AFPI mines the SAP P2P event log to pinpoint the 15–20% of vendors driving 80% of exceptions and the maverick-buying paths bypassing framework agreements — a Regrello-adjacent wedge that expands the in-flight Regrello (Upside) Shadow Oppty. Metric moved: touchless invoice rate 60% → 80%+, supplier onboarding cycle -40%.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP (P2P), Regrello

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Vehicle Safety Recall & Field-Action Governance

Every commercial-vehicle recall (NHTSA, KBA, ADR) has to be tracked from defect identification through VIN-level closure with auditable evidence — a heavy manual burden across four brands and multiple regulators. Compliance Center codifies the recall/field-action workflow with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and continuous evidence for regulators. Metric moved: recall closure rate at deadline, days-to-audit-ready evidence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP (Quality/Warranty), Dealer DMS
