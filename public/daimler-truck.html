Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Order-to-Delivery for Custom Trucks

Daimler Truck's build-to-order model spans thousands of chassis, powertrain, and body-builder configurations across Mercedes-Benz Trucks, Freightliner, Western Star, FUSO, and BharatBenz. Long lead times, missed customer promise dates, and finished-goods write-downs are chronic pain — and CFO scrutiny is intense post-IPO. AFPI mines the SAP order and MES event logs to expose where variants stall, which options trigger rework, and which plants consistently beat vs. miss cycle time; the wedge is a 3-4 week diagnostic that pairs with the Customer 360 Data Cloud opp to unify order status across SAP, dealer DMS, and CRM. Metric moved: on-time delivery to promise date +8-12pp, working-capital tied up in WIP -5%.

Key System(s): SAP (Order/MES), Dealer DMS, Sales Cloud

2. Aftersales & Warranty Claims Handling

Warranty is the single largest controllable cost lever in commercial vehicles — every day a claim sits in dealer queues drags NPS and cash. Today the process spans dealer portals, ServiceNow tickets, and SAP warranty modules with heavy manual triage. AFPI process-mines the claim event log to find rework loops, dealer outliers, and codes that consistently escalate to engineering, then feeds an Agentforce Service Agent (see the Agentic Pilot and ServiceNow Integration opps) to auto-triage and route. Metric moved: warranty cycle time -30-40%, warranty leakage -3-5%.

Key System(s): SAP (Warranty), ServiceNow, Service Cloud

Key Contact: Achim Puchert (CEO, Mercedes-Benz Trucks)
