Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Booking-to-Boarding Journey & Revenue Management

GNV sells passenger and freight tickets through gnv.it, call center, travel agencies, and B2B corporate channels, then hands the passenger off to check-in, port boarding, and onboard revenue capture. Manual price overrides, cross-channel double bookings, and abandoned-cart drop-offs today cost measurable revenue per sailing and blur the yield picture that Revenue Management Manager Maurizio Ammendolia is expected to defend. AFPI mines the full booking-to-boarding event log across MC Engagement, Loyalty, and Service Cloud, exposes conversion leaks by channel, and lets the yield team re-simulate policy changes without a SQL specialist — targeting 2-4 pt conversion uplift and 3-6% ancillary revenue lift. This is the natural expansion path for the in-cycle Apromore opp ($68k) into a durable Wave 2 anchor.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, MC Engagement

Key Contact: Maurizio Ammendolia (Revenue Management & Pricing Manager)

2. Port Turnaround & Vessel Operations

Every minute a cruiseferry is berthed at Genoa, Palermo, Barcelona, or Tangier is a minute not selling cabins and not moving freight — turnaround performance is the single biggest lever on fleet utilization as GNV scales from 13 to 16+ vessels through 2027. Today, port coordination across the Chief Port Operations Officer, Italian Agency Port Coordinator, and local port agents runs on tribal knowledge and disconnected checklists. AFPI mines the entire disembark-clean-provision-embark event flow, quantifies the delay causes, and gives Andrea Balabani a fact-based ranking of where the next hour of turnaround savings sits — 5-10% turnaround improvement compounds directly into revenue capacity.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Port Ops System (TBD)

Key Contact: Andrea Balabani (Chief Port Operations Officer)

3. Contact Center & Customer Service Case Resolution

Customer Service Manager Virginia Rivano runs a high-volume, seasonally-spiky contact center handling booking changes, refunds, disruption re-accommodation, and freight escalations in Italian, English, Spanish, and French. Slack signals confirm strong interest in AI-generated knowledge articles from historical case data ([Slack] Olga Eberini, May 2026) — a classic downstream Agentforce Service play, but only useful if the underlying case-resolution process is measured. AFPI mines every case path, identifies rework loops (transfers, re-opens, escalation to CCO), and feeds an Agentforce Service rollout that is grounded in the real process, not the documented one. Target: 15-25% deflection lift and 20% AHT reduction on repeat intents.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, MC Engagement

4. Freight Sales & B2B Order-to-Cash

Freight is GNV's stickier, higher-margin revenue leg — hauliers, logistics operators, and MSC Group intercompany bookings across Sicily, Sardinia, Spain, Morocco, Albania, and Tunisia routes. Sales Freight Manager Alessandro Feniello and Cost & Contract Manager Lorenzo Cammarata today wrestle with contract exceptions, credit blocks, and manual pricing tiers that slow quote-to-loaded-vehicle cycle time and inflate DSO. AFPI mines the freight quote-to-cash event log inside the newly-live Sales & Service platform, exposes the exception paths that eat margin, and lets Finance re-simulate credit and pricing rules without IT — 3-5 days DSO reduction plus visible margin recovery on non-standard tariffs.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, Service Cloud
