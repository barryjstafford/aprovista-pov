Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Booking-to-Boarding Journey & Revenue Management

GNV sells passenger and freight tickets through gnv.it, call center, travel agencies, and B2B channels, then hands the passenger off to check-in, port boarding, and onboard revenue capture. Manual price overrides, cross-channel double bookings, and abandoned-cart drop-offs today blur the yield picture that Revenue Management is expected to defend. AFPI mines the full booking-to-boarding event log across MC Engagement, Loyalty, and Service Cloud, exposes conversion leaks by channel, and lets the yield team re-simulate policy changes without a SQL specialist — targeting 2-4 pt conversion uplift and 3-6% ancillary revenue lift.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, MC Engagement

Key Contact: Maurizio Ammendolia (Revenue Management & Pricing Manager)

2. Port Turnaround & Vessel Operations

Every minute a cruiseferry is berthed at Genoa, Palermo, Barcelona, or Tangier is a minute not selling cabins and not moving freight — turnaround performance is the single biggest lever on fleet utilization as GNV scales from 13 to 16+ vessels through 2027. Today, port coordination across the Chief Port Operations Officer and local port agents runs on tribal knowledge and disconnected checklists. AFPI mines the entire disembark-clean-provision-embark event flow, quantifies delay causes, and gives leadership a fact-based ranking of where the next hour of turnaround savings sits — 5-10% turnaround improvement compounds directly into revenue capacity.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Port Ops System (TBD)

Key Contact: Andrea Balabani (Chief Port Operations Officer)

3. Freight Sales & B2B Order-to-Cash

Freight is GNV's stickier, higher-margin revenue leg — hauliers, logistics operators, and MSC Group intercompany bookings across Sicily, Sardinia, Spain, Morocco, Albania, and Tunisia routes. Sales Freight and Cost & Contract managers today wrestle with contract exceptions, credit blocks, and manual pricing tiers that slow quote-to-loaded-vehicle cycle time and inflate DSO. AFPI mines the freight quote-to-cash event log inside the newly-live Sales & Service platform, exposes the exception paths that eat margin, and lets Finance re-simulate credit and pricing rules without IT — 3-5 days DSO reduction plus margin recovery on non-standard tariffs.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, Service Cloud
