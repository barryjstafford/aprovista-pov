Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Order-to-Cash across ERP + Service Cloud. Alfa Laval books orders across pumps, heat exchangers, decanters, and aftermarket parts through a multi-ERP landscape feeding a Service Cloud rollout now in Stage 02. Manual order-clearing, credit blocks, and delivery-date renegotiation stretch quote-to-cash cycle times and hurt on-time delivery in a capex-sensitive industrial buyer market. AFPI mines the full O2C event log without a PQL specialist, exposes rework loops, and lets the business analyst re-simulate policy changes in weeks, not quarters — targeting DSO reduction of 4-6 days and OTIF improvement of 3-5 pts. Champion: Anders Wilhjelm (President, Marine Division) or the Service Cloud program lead named on the rollout opp.

2. Field Service & Aftermarket Case-to-Resolution. The two open Service opps ($126k pilot + $210k Phase I rollout) signal a live investment in service transformation across ~100 service centers globally. Aftermarket is Alfa Laval's highest-margin business; every hour a decanter or heat exchanger is down costs a customer real revenue, so first-time-fix and MTTR are board-level metrics. AFPI process-mines every case path, spare-parts dispatch, and technician handoff, then feeds Agentforce Service to auto-route and auto-diagnose — a 10-15% MTTR reduction and 5-8 pt FCR lift compound directly into service margin. Champion: Sameer Kalra (President, Business Unit Service).

3. Procure-to-Pay & Supplier On-Boarding. Alfa Laval sources specialty alloys, castings, and titanium plates from a global supplier base under tightening EU CSRD, CBAM, and dual-use export controls. Manual PO exceptions, three-way match failures, and supplier onboarding backlogs today burn CFO-office FTE and slow new product introductions. AFPI mines the P2P event log, quantifies rework hotspots, and lets Finance re-simulate policy changes with no SQL — reclaiming 8-12% of P2P touchless-rate uplift and cutting supplier onboarding from weeks to days. Champion: Fredrik Ekström (CFO).

4. New Product Introduction (NPI) & Engineering Change Order. Alfa Laval ships thousands of engineered-to-order configurations across the Marine, Energy, and Food & Water divisions; ECO cycle time is the hidden tax on innovation speed. AFPI mines the PLM-to-ERP change-request flow, exposes bottlenecks between engineering, sourcing, and manufacturing, and pairs Compliance Center with the AFPI wedge to keep CE/ATEX/marine-class documentation always audit-ready. Target: 20-30% reduction in engineering-change lead time and zero missed classification reviews. Champion: Susanne Pahlén Åklundh (President, Business Unit Energy).

5. Marine Decarbonization & Compliance Reporting. Alfa Laval's PureBallast, PureSOx, and boiler business sit at the center of the IMO 2030/2050 decarbonization mandate and EU ETS-Maritime rollout — the customer's reporting obligation IS Alfa Laval's revenue engine. AFPI process-mines the certification-to-installation-to-service flow for marine retrofits and layers Compliance Center to auto-evidence each installation against MARPOL and EU-ETS schedules. This turns a compliance line item into a durable renewal moat and shortens retrofit cycle time by 15-25%. Champion: Anders Wilhjelm (President, Marine Division).
