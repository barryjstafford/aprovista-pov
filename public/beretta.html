2. Export-Control & Dual-Use Licensing Workflow (ITAR / EU 821)

Every civilian and defense shipment out of Gardone or Accokeek touches an ITAR / EU dual-use license workflow with heavy rework when destination, end-user, or product classification changes late. Mining the license-approval → export-doc → shipment-release trail exposes rework loops, expired-license risk, and shipments that sat in customs waiting on a re-classify. AFPI adds a Compliance Center overlay so audit evidence and control effectiveness are byproducts of the mining, not a separate project. Metric moved: percentage of shipments held for compliance rework and time-to-license-approval.
