2. Export-Control & Dual-Use Licensing Workflow (ITAR / EU 821)

Every civilian and defense shipment out of Gardone or Accokeek touches an ITAR / EU dual-use license workflow with heavy rework when destination, end-user, or product classification changes late in the cycle. Mining the license-approval → export-doc → shipment-release trail exposes rework loops, expired-license risk, and shipments held in customs waiting on a re-classify. AFPI targets the exact steps where compliance rework crushes on-time-ship. Metric moved: percent of shipments held for compliance rework and time-to-license-approval.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ITAR & EU Dual-Use Export-License Control Effectiveness

ITAR (US), EU Regulation 821/2021 dual-use, and Italian AMA licensing put every civil and defense shipment under formal license controls with heavy penalties for violation. Compliance Center codifies the license-issue → end-use-check → shipment-release control set and produces continuous audit evidence tied to the actual mined process, not a hand-built binder. That closes the loop between the operations team and the compliance team on the same event log. Metric moved: audit-ready shipments covered and mean-time-to-evidence per license type.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: ERP, Trade-Compliance module

2. Firearm Serialization, ATF Recordkeeping & Product-Recall Traceability

US ATF Form 4473 recordkeeping and EU firearms directive traceability require Beretta USA and Gardone to prove every serialized unit's chain-of-custody from manufacture to dealer to end-user. Compliance Center runs serialization + dealer-transfer + recall-notification as a controlled workflow with sign-offs and evidence retention. That converts a periodic ATF inspection scramble into an always-on control. Metric moved: percent of serialized units with complete chain-of-custody and time-to-execute a targeted recall.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: ERP serialization, Salesforce Sales Cloud dealer-network
