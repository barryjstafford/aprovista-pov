Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Client Onboarding & KYC Refresh for Institutional Custody

BNY onboards asset managers, sovereign wealth funds, and corporate treasuries into custody, fund accounting, and collateral services — a multi-week workflow spanning legal, tax, AML, and account-provisioning teams. The pain is a stubborn 30-90 day onboarding cycle that delays revenue recognition and drives client complaints during periodic KYC refresh cycles. AFPI mines the actual case-management event log to expose hand-off delays, rework loops, and exception paths, then Agentforce Operations automates document chase and status updates. Metric that moves: time-to-first-transaction and analyst throughput per FTE.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: KYC/AML platform, onboarding case-management

Key Contact: Akash Shah (Chief Commercial Officer)

2. Corporate Actions Processing at Scale

BNY processes millions of corporate actions annually across mergers, dividends, tenders, and rights offerings — each with narrow deadlines and enormous downstream P&L consequence if missed. A single missed election or misapplied entitlement can cost seven figures and trigger client escalations, and the process mixes SWIFT feeds, custody platforms, and manual reconciliation. AFPI mines the end-to-end event log to surface stuck cases, exception clusters, and SLA breaches, then Agentforce triages by risk. Metric moved: exception rate and SLA adherence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SWIFT feeds, custody platform

Key Contact: Bridget Engle (Chief Operations & Technology Officer)

3. Collateral Management & Margin Call Workflow

As one of the world's largest tri-party collateral agents, BNY moves trillions in collateral daily between dealers, clearing houses, and buy-side clients, all under Basel III and UMR margin rules. Intraday margin calls and disputes still require analyst intervention, and root-cause analysis of failed pledges is slow. AFPI mines the margin-call and settlement event log to identify recurring dispute patterns and slow counterparties, then Agentforce drafts responses and escalates by exposure. Metric moved: dispute resolution time and capital efficiency.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Tri-party collateral platform, settlement engine
