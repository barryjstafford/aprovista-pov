The pain is a stubborn 30-90 day onboarding cycle that delays revenue recognition and drives client-satisfaction complaints during periodic KYC refresh cycles. 2. Corporate Actions Processing at Scale

BNY processes millions of corporate actions annually across mergers, dividends, tenders, and rights offerings — each with narrow deadlines and enormous downstream P&L consequence if missed. The pain is that a single missed election or misapplied entitlement can cost seven figures and trigger client escalations, and the process today mixes SWIFT feeds, custody platforms, and manual reconciliation. AFPI mines the end-to-end event log to surface stuck cases, exception clusters, and SLA breaches, then Agentforce triages by risk while a Compliance-Center wedge audits every override. The metric moved is exception rate and SLA adherence.

3. Collateral Management & Margin Call Workflow

As one of the world's largest tri-party collateral agents, BNY moves trillions in collateral daily between dealers, clearing houses, and buy-side clients, all under Basel III and UMR margin rules. The pain is that intraday margin calls and disputes still require analyst intervention, and root-cause analysis of failed pledges is slow. AFPI mines the margin-call and settlement event log to identify recurring dispute patterns and slow counterparties, then Agentforce drafts responses and escalates by exposure. The metric moved is dispute resolution time and capital efficiency.

4. Fund Accounting NAV Production & Exception Handling

BNY strikes daily NAVs for tens of thousands of funds across Investment Services — a tightly-scheduled pipeline where any pricing, corporate-action, or reconciliation break can delay strike and breach client SLAs. The pain is a growing exception queue driven by increasingly complex private-market funds, plus rising audit and DORA operational-resilience obligations. AFPI mines the NAV-production pipeline (Eagle, InForm, Nexen event logs) to spotlight the recurring 20% of breaks causing 80% of delays, then Agentforce auto-routes and pre-drafts break commentary.
