1. Health-Insurance Claims Processing & Adjudication

Bupa's private-health claims funnel spans OCR intake, provider validation, benefit-rule adjudication, and payment — with rework and manual exceptions concentrated in dental and extras. AFPI plus Agentforce Operations can automate the deterministic branches while flagging outliers to human adjudicators. Target metric: claims cycle time and straight-through-processing rate.
