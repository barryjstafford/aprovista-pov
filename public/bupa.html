Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Health-Insurance Claims Adjudication

Bupa's private-health claims funnel spans OCR intake, provider validation, benefit-rule adjudication, and payment — with rework and manual exceptions concentrated in dental and extras. Mining the end-to-end log surfaces the ~15-20% of claims that touch three or more human queues and quantifies the CSAT + cycle-time cost. Apromore plus Agentforce Operations can automate the deterministic branches while flagging outliers to human adjudicators. Metric: claims cycle time and straight-through-processing rate.

Feeds an Agentforce Operations business case directly and pairs with the Pega-replacement opp. Metric: adjudications per FTE per day and touch-time per claim.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. APRA Private-Health Insurer Prudential Reporting

Bupa HI is one of Australia's largest private health insurers and must produce APRA HRS-series prudential returns plus PHIAC-legacy statistical reporting on a locked cadence. Compliance Center codifies data-lineage, reviewer sign-off, and evidence retention for every return, and continuously monitors whether the underlying claims + membership processes drift from certified behaviour. Especially important as Data Cloud becomes the source of record. Metric: return cycle time and audit findings per quarter.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Data Cloud, GL / actuarial reporting platform

Key Contact: Chief Risk Officer (Chief Risk Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Aged-Care Quality Standards & Serious Incident Response

Post-royal-commission, the Aged Care Quality & Safety Commission enforces the eight Quality Standards and Serious Incident Response Scheme (SIRS) with continuous evidence. Compliance Center runs incident triage, notification, investigation, and remediation as a controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties and always-on evidence, replacing the current spreadsheet + email trail. Directly de-risks the sector's number-one reputational exposure. Metric: SIRS notification SLA hit-rate and Quality Standards audit close-out time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Aged-care incident-management platform, case management

Key Contact: Head of Aged-Care Compliance (Head of Compliance)

Bupa handles millions of sensitive health records across insurance, dental, and aged care, all in scope for the Privacy Act, the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme, and the incoming Privacy Act reforms.
