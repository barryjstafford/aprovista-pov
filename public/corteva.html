The live Grower 360 Transformation opp signals Corteva is rebuilding how growers and retailers order seed, crop protection, and traits — a process notoriously fragmented across retailer channels, distributor rebates, and season-tied pricing. AFPI mines the current O2C event log to expose rework loops (rebate disputes, credit-hold escalations, mis-priced orders) that erode margin at planting-season peak. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. EPA / EU REACH Regulatory Submission Control Tower

Every crop-protection molecule Corteva sells lives inside a 3-7 year submission lifecycle across EPA, EU REACH, and dozens of national authorities — today tracked in spreadsheets and email. Compliance Center codifies the submission process as a controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and audit-ready evidence at every stage-gate. This is the natural landing wedge for the R&D Compliance Project opp. Metric moved: on-time-approval rate and audit-finding count per submission.
