AFPI mines Service Cloud case events plus billing to reveal the exact steps where customers stall or churn calls. 2. ESC Price-Submission & Regulatory Reporting Workflow

Victorian water corporations submit five-yearly price determinations to the Essential Services Commission — a heavyweight, cross-functional cycle covering forecasting, PREMO customer outcomes, capex justification and evidence packs. Mining the end-to-end submission workflow surfaces where iterations stall between finance, engineering and executive review. AFPI shortens the next Water Plan cycle and creates an auditable trail for ESC scrutiny. Target: 15-25% reduction in cycle time to submission-ready.

Water and sewer infrastructure renewal across 39 localities is a multi-year capital program with hand-offs between planning, procurement, contractors and asset registers.
