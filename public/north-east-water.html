Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Meter-to-Cash Cycle Time (Move-in → First Bill → Payment)

NEW serves ~120k customers across 39 north-east Victorian localities on a Service Cloud + billing stack that generates thousands of daily events. Rework loops between call-centre agents, meter reads and billing exceptions inflate first-bill lag and drive avoidable inbound contacts. Process mining Service Cloud case events joined to billing reveals exactly where customers stall or repeat-call. Target: 20-30% reduction in cost-to-serve per active connection and measurable NPS lift.

2. ESC Price-Submission (Water Plan) Workflow

Victorian water corporations submit five-yearly price determinations to the Essential Services Commission — a heavyweight cycle covering forecasting, PREMO customer outcomes, capex justification and evidence packs. Mining the end-to-end submission workflow surfaces where iterations stall between finance, engineering and executive review. AFPI shortens the next Water Plan cycle and creates an auditable trail for ESC scrutiny. Target: 15-25% reduction in cycle time to submission-ready.

Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. Call-Centre Agent Handle-Time & After-Call Wrap

NEW's Service Cloud call centre handles billing enquiries, faults and hardship intake across a small team — AHT and wrap variability drive both cost and NPS. Task mining captures each agent's on-screen actions to isolate app-switching, copy/paste and the tabs that eat wrap time. Feeds directly into an Agentforce for Service business case that lifts self-service deflection. Metric: AHT and after-call-work minutes per contact type.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud console, Billing lookup screens

2. Billing-Exception & Meter-Read Rework

Estimated reads, transposed digits and account-mismatch exceptions push work back into billing analysts who re-key between the meter-data platform and the customer master. Task mining the analyst desktop quantifies re-keying time, exposes which exception codes eat the most human touch, and names the exact automations Agentforce or Flow can replace them with. Metric: exceptions resolved per analyst hour and rework rate on billing runs.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Billing System desktop, Service Cloud

3. Field-Service Dispatch & SCADA Exception Response

Water-main breaks, sewer overflows and treatment-plant alarms generate SCADA events that dispatchers translate into field-crew jobs against SLA. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ESC PREMO Customer-Outcome Evidence

The ESC's PREMO framework demands ongoing evidence that customer commitments (price, reliability, environment, market, outcomes) are being met — not just at submission time. Compliance Center runs each PREMO commitment as a controlled workflow with owners, KPIs, sign-offs and always-on evidence. Ends the pre-submission scramble that consumes finance and customer teams. Metric: audit-ready commitments covered and mean-time-to-evidence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Salesforce Platform

2. Safe Drinking Water Act & EPA Notifiable-Incident Controls

NEW is bound by the Safe Drinking Water Act 2003 (Vic) and EPA Victoria reporting duties — every notifiable water-quality or environmental incident has statutory reporting timeframes. Compliance Center codifies incident-to-notification as a controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties and produces continuous evidence for Department of Health and EPA audits. Metric: notification SLA hit-rate and audit findings per reporting cycle.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SCADA / Lab LIMS (TBD), Service Cloud
