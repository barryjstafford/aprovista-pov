Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Customer Billing & Dispute Resolution Cycle

Water Corporation bills ~1.1M WA customers on a metered/quarterly cadence, and billing complaints plus tariff adjustments consume meaningful Head of Customer Billing capacity. Mining the SAP billing-to-cash log against Service Cloud case data exposes rework loops (re-reads, estimate reversals, hardship escalations) and the true dispute cycle time by customer segment. AFPI pinpoints the 3–5 case reasons that drive 60%+ of rework and guides targeted process fixes. Metric moved: dispute cycle time (target −25%) and cost-to-serve per billing exception.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, Salesforce Service Cloud

Key Contact: Head of Customer Billing (Water Corporation)

2. Asset Maintenance Work-Order-to-Completion (Maximo)

Managing ~35,000 km of water and wastewater mains plus dams, pumps, and treatment plants, Water Corporation runs a large IBM Maximo work-order backlog. Mining the create-plan-dispatch-complete flow surfaces where jobs stall (waiting on parts, permit approvals, crew reassignment) and quantifies the true wrench-time-to-total-time ratio. AFPI feeds fact-based prioritisation into the General Manager Operations team. Metric moved: mean time-to-complete on priority-1 asset jobs (target −20%) and reduced emergency-shift overtime.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: IBM Maximo, SAP

Key Contact: Head of Operational Technology (Water Corporation)

3. New Connection Application & Developer Onboarding

New connections (residential, developer, industrial) span planning approvals, engineering assessment, quote issue, payment, and physical connection — crossing Service Cloud, SAP, and field-crew scheduling. Mining reveals hand-off delays (typically at engineering assessment and permit) that inflate wait times and put Water Corp under Economic Regulation Authority scrutiny. AFPI produces a variant analysis showing which application archetypes deserve a fast lane. Metric moved: new-connection lead time (target −30% for standard residential) and application backlog.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, SAP

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Drinking Water Quality Management Plan (DWQMP) Conformance

The WA Department of Health mandates a documented Drinking Water Quality Management Plan for each supply scheme, with routine sampling, non-conformance reporting, and 12-monthly audits. Compliance Center runs the whole sampling-to-notification chain as a controlled workflow — sample scheduled, result recorded, exceedance escalated, Health Dept notified — with segregation-of-duties and evidence attached to each step. Especially timely given the ongoing Alcoa bauxite-mining catchment contamination scrutiny. Metric moved: incident-to-notification cycle time and Health Dept finding closure rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: LIMS / lab platform, Salesforce Service Cloud

Key Contact: Head of Risk & Assurance (Water Corporation)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Economic Regulation Authority (ERA) Price Submission Controls

Every price-submission cycle to the WA Economic Regulation Authority requires auditable evidence for cost build-ups, capex justification, and service-standard performance. Compliance Center codifies the submission workflow — data pulls from SAP and asset systems, actuarial-style modelling sign-offs, executive attestation, ministerial briefing — producing a defensible audit trail on demand. Reduces the manual scramble at each Determination window. Metric moved: submission preparation cycle time and audit-adjustment rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, corporate performance / EPM platform

Key Contact: Chief Financial Officer (Water Corporation)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Environmental Licence & Wastewater Discharge Reporting

Wastewater treatment plants operate under DWER environmental licences with prescribed discharge limits, monthly reporting, and non-compliance notifications. Compliance Center runs the sample-to-report cycle as a controlled workflow, capturing every threshold breach, corrective action, and licence-condition attestation with named accountability. Removes the risk of a missed 24-hour notification. Metric moved: on-time regulatory reporting rate and licence-condition exception count.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SCADA/OT historian, LIMS
