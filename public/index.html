Welcome!

AproVista is an AI sales-development agent that turns a single company name into a one-page Point-of-View brief for the Agentforce Process Intelligence (AFPI) play.

Given an account, AproVista researches it across Org62, Slack, and the open web — pulls in the latest 10-K, buyer titles, install base, competitor mentions, and Slack deal-support threads — and scores its propensity to buy process mining across 100 value points, against an 8-category rubric (the AproScore). It then names the top process-intelligence use cases to mine, the likely champion and economic buyer, and the recommended sales motion (standalone / co-sell with Agentforce Operations / integrated with Salesforce).

The purpose of AproVista is to provide Apromore AEs with an account-specific Point of View (POV) to help make their interactions with Core Salesforce and Customers more compelling and more impactful in generating interest in process mining.
