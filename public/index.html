Welcome!

AproVista is an AI SDR agent that turns a company name into an account brief for selling Agentforce Process Intelligence (AFPI). It researches all Salesforce Org62, Slack, LinkedIn, + the open web to surface deal threads, account insights, installed software, and competitor signals. Then the account is "AproScored" across over 100 points of real propensity to buy value metrics.

AproVista then recommends and ranks the top company-specific process-mining use cases, helping seller have sharper, more compelling conversations with Core Salesforce and customers — ultimately creating new Apromore opportunities.

Soon available in Slack as @aprovista. Until then, Slack Barry Stafford any account names, and AproVista will return updated account insights in about five minutes.
