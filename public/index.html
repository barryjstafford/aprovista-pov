Welcome!

AproVista is an AI SDR agent that turns a company name into an account brief for selling Agentforce Process Intelligence (AFPI). It researches all Salesforce Org62, Slack, LinkedIn, Company websites, Annual Reports, 10-K's & the open web to surface account insights, deal threads, installed software, and competitor signals. Then the account is "AproScore" across over 100 points of real propensity-to-buy value metrics.

AproVista then recommends and ranks the top company-specific process-mining use cases, helping sellers have sharper, more compelling conversations with core Salesforce and customers — ultimately creating new Apromore opportunities.

Soon available in Slack as @aprovista. Until then, Slack Barry Stafford any account names, and AproVista will return updated account insights in about five minutes.
