Topline POV: Apromore can help Westernport Region Water Corporation by mining meter-to-cash, field work-order execution, and regulator reporting cycles so a lean 62-person team can shrink billing exceptions, cut truck-rolls per fault, and evidence Safe-Drinking-Water & ESC compliance without adding headcount.

Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Meter-to-Cash & Revenue Leakage

Reads, estimates, billing exceptions, and dunning span the billing system, the meter data platform, and manual customer-service touches. For a small retailer any un-billed volume or write-off is material to the bottom line. AFPI would mine the full read-to-invoice-to-payment path to size revenue leakage and rework, then automate the top 3 exception patterns with Agentforce Operations. Metric moved: revenue recovered per 1,000 accounts and days-sales-outstanding.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Customer Billing System, Meter Data Management

2. Field Work-Order & Fault Response

Mains bursts, sewer chokes, water-quality callouts and planned maintenance flow across the works management system, GIS, and mobile crews. Repeat truck-rolls and unclosed jobs inflate opex on a stretched crew that covers Phillip Island plus the mainland district. Mining the dispatch-to-close path exposes rework loops and idle time by asset class. Metric moved: first-time-fix rate and average job cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Works/Asset Management, GIS

Key Contact: David Girdwood (General Manager, Assets and Operations)

3. Capital Program & Renewals Delivery

The 2025-30 corporate plan includes water-meter replacement, sewer pump-station upgrades, water-quality improvement, and renewable energy generation — a heavy capital slate for a small utility. Mining the plan-award-deliver-commission path across finance, procurement, and asset systems shows where schedule slip and change-orders originate. Metric moved: capex delivered on plan, and change-order rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial/ERP, Procurement

4. Regulator & Safe-Drinking-Water Compliance Evidence

ESC price-submission monitoring, EPA reporting, and Safe Drinking Water Act audit responses require assembling event evidence from lab, SCADA, works, and customer systems. Today this is a manual, per-request scramble. Compliance-Center is the wedge: a re-usable mined process view of every regulated workflow, ready for on-demand audit. Metric moved: audit response time and open non-conformance count.
