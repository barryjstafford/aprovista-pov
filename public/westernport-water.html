Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Meter-to-Cash & Revenue Leakage

Reads, estimates, billing exceptions, and dunning span the customer billing system, the meter data platform, and manual customer-service touches. For a small retailer any un-billed volume or write-off is material to the bottom line. AFPI mines the full read-to-invoice-to-payment path to size revenue leakage and rework, then targets the top 3 exception patterns. Metric moved: revenue recovered per 1,000 accounts and days-sales-outstanding.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Customer Billing System, Meter Data Management

Key Contact: Gareth Kennedy (General Manager, Corporate and Customer)

2. Field Work-Order & Fault Response

Mains bursts, sewer chokes, water-quality callouts and planned maintenance flow across the works management system, GIS, and mobile crews covering Phillip Island plus the mainland district. Repeat truck-rolls and unclosed jobs inflate opex on a stretched crew. Mining the dispatch-to-close path exposes rework loops and idle time by asset class. Metric moved: first-time-fix rate and average job cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Works/Asset Management, GIS

Key Contact: David Girdwood (General Manager, Assets and Operations)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. New Connections & Developer Servicing

Phillip Island growth and district development drive applications for new connections, servicing agreements, and account setup. Handoffs between customer, engineering, and billing are prone to stall — particularly around approval SLAs. Mining the end-to-end path exposes where applications age and which handoffs cause resubmits. Metric moved: application-to-connection cycle time and % straight-through applications.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Customer Billing System, Works/Asset Management

Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. Customer Service Rep Desktop Swivel-Chair

A tiny customer-service team fields billing, connections, and fault calls on Sales Cloud while jumping to the billing system, meter data, and email. Task mining the CSR desktop reveals the exact copy/paste and lookup steps that eat handle-time, and feeds the business case for Agentforce-assisted responses. Metric moved: average handle time and after-call-work minutes per contact.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, Customer Billing System

Key Contact: Gareth Kennedy (General Manager, Corporate and Customer)

2. Field Crew Mobile & Job-Close Capture

Field techs record job outcomes on mobile devices, then admins re-enter into the works system and GIS. Task mining the mobile-plus-back-office activity exposes double-keying and delayed job-closure that skew asset data and delay invoicing. Directly informs a mobile-first workflow rebuild. Metric moved: job-close latency and share of same-day closures.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Mobile Works App, GIS

Key Contact: David Girdwood (General Manager, Assets and Operations)

3. Finance & AR Cash-Application Desktop

Finance staff at a lean utility spend time reconciling bank feeds, applying receipts to customer accounts, and clearing unallocated cash across the ERP and billing platform. Task mining exposes the manual keystrokes and screen-hops per receipt, sizing the automation opportunity and freeing time for capital-program financial control. Metric moved: unallocated cash days and receipts processed per FTE-hour.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Safe Drinking Water Act Audit Evidence

The Victorian Safe Drinking Water Act requires documented water-quality management from source to tap, with rapid response to any non-conformance. Today, audit evidence is assembled by hand from lab, SCADA, works, and customer complaint systems. Compliance Center runs each regulated workflow as a controlled, always-audit-ready process. Metric moved: audit response time and open non-conformance count.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: LIMS, SCADA

Key Contact: Dona Tantirimudalige (Managing Director)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. ESC Price Submission & Regulator Reporting

Every five years the Essential Services Commission price submission consumes months of finance and operations effort; annual performance reporting is continuous. Compliance Center codifies the data-collection and sign-off workflow so evidence is captured as work happens, not reassembled retroactively. Metric moved: cycle time to prepare each ESC pack and internal audit hours consumed.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial/ERP, Customer Billing System

Key Contact: Kathy Hawke (Executive Manager Finance / CFO)

3. EPA Environmental & Net-Zero Reporting

Trade-waste, biosolids, discharge-quality and greenhouse-gas reporting for the EPA and the Corporate Plan 2025-30 Net-Zero targets require pulling data from SCADA, environmental monitoring, and finance. Compliance Center controls the workflow, evidences segregation-of-duties, and delivers a repeatable Net-Zero disclosure trail. Metric moved: reporting cycle time and exception-count per submission.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SCADA, Environmental Monitoring
