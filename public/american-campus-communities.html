Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Annual Turn: Move-Out to Move-In Across 200+ Properties

ACC concentrates ~90% of lease commencements into a two-week August window across 200+ properties and 167k beds — the "turn." Inspections, cleanings, damage assessments, punch lists, and move-in scheduling collide with billing, deposits, and customer-service load. Apromore mines the full turn lifecycle across Yardi and Service Cloud to expose the properties, roles, and steps that miss commencement SLA — the single biggest operating risk of the year. Metric: percentage of units ready at day-zero and turn labor cost per bed.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Yardi Voyager, Salesforce Service Cloud

Key Contact: Dan Barry (SVP Facilities Operations)

2. Touchless Resident Renewal Automation

Two open Org62 opps ("ARY Touchless Renewal Automation Only") signal an active automation push on lease renewals — the highest-margin retention lever in student housing. Renewal decisions blend Marketing Cloud journeys, Sales Cloud follow-up, and Yardi lease creation, with parent/guarantor co-signer flows layered on top. Apromore mines the current renewal path to find handoff drops and pricing-approval loops before Agentforce agents get bolted on. Metric: renewal conversion rate and days-to-lease-signature.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Yardi Voyager

Key Contact: Kimberly Kelley (EVP & Chief Marketing Officer)

3. Maintenance Work-Order Lifecycle (167k-Bed Portfolio)

Maintenance ticket volume at 167k beds is enormous and highly seasonal; first-time-fix, technician routing, parts availability, and resident SLA drive resident satisfaction and retention. Work orders originate in Service Cloud and complete in Yardi's facilities module, with a persistent gap in visibility across the two. Apromore mines the full ticket-to-resolution path, spotlighting re-visit drivers, aging categories, and property-level variants. Metric: mean-time-to-resolve and first-time-fix rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Yardi Voyager

This directly feeds the touchless-renewal and touchless-application investments already funded. Metric: minutes-per-application processed and share of leasing time spent in swivel-chair.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Fair Housing Act & Application Screening Consistency

ACC processes tens of thousands of student applications annually and must apply screening, roommate-matching, and pricing rules consistently to avoid Fair Housing Act exposure. Compliance Center continuously audits Yardi lease workflows and Sales Cloud application records to detect deviations, missing steps, and property-level variants that could constitute disparate treatment. This is proactive control monitoring rather than sample-based audit. Metric: exception rate against defined screening playbook and time-to-detect a variant.

Key System(s) to be Audited: Yardi Voyager, Salesforce Sales Cloud

Key Contact: Kim Voss (EVP Human Resources, Chief Compliance Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Security Deposit Return & State Statute Compliance

Every state ACC operates in imposes strict deadlines (14-30 days typical) and itemization rules on security-deposit returns after move-out. Missed deadlines trigger statutory penalties and reputational risk with parent guarantors. Compliance Center audits the Yardi + Service Cloud move-out-to-refund flow against per-state deadline rules and flags at-risk records in real time. Metric: percent of deposit returns processed within statutory window, by state.

Key System(s) to be Audited: Yardi Voyager, Salesforce Service Cloud

Key Contact: Kimberly Kelley (EVP & Chief Marketing Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Title IX / Clery Incident Handling on Managed Properties

Because ACC operates on-campus and public-private partnership housing tied to universities, incident reports (Title IX referrals, Clery-reportable events, protective-order enforcement) must be routed and documented against strict university and federal requirements. Compliance Center audits the Service Cloud incident-handling flow to prove that every reportable event was escalated, timestamped, and acknowledged per policy. This protects both ACC and its university partners. Metric: percent of incidents with complete Clery/Title IX handling trail.

Key System(s) to be Audited: Salesforce Service Cloud, Yardi Voyager

Key Contact: Kim Voss (EVP HR & Chief Compliance Officer)
