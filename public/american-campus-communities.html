Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Annual Turn: Move-Out to Move-In Across 200+ Properties

ACC's student-housing model concentrates ~90% of lease commencements into a two-week August window across 200+ properties and 167k beds — the "turn." Inspections, cleanings, damage assessments, punch lists, and move-in scheduling collide with billing, deposits, and customer-service load. Apromore can mine the turn-lifecycle across Yardi and Service Cloud to expose the properties, roles, and steps that miss commencement SLA — the single biggest operating risk of the year. Metric: percentage of units ready at day-zero and turn-labor cost per bed.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Yardi Voyager, Salesforce Service Cloud

2. Touchless Resident Renewal Automation

Two open Org62 opps ("ARY Touchless Renewal Automation Only") signal an active automation push on lease renewals — the highest-margin retention lever in student housing. Renewal decisions blend Marketing Cloud journeys, Sales Cloud follow-up, and Yardi lease creation, with parent/guarantor co-signer flows layered on top. Apromore can mine the current renewal path to find handoff drops and pricing-approval loops before Agentforce agents get bolted on. Metric: renewal conversion rate and days-to-lease-signature.

3. Maintenance Work-Order Lifecycle (167k-Bed Portfolio)

Maintenance ticket volume at 167k beds is enormous and highly variable seasonally; first-time-fix, technician routing, parts availability, and resident SLA drive resident-satisfaction and retention. Work orders originate in Service Cloud and complete in Yardi's facilities module, with a persistent gap in visibility across the two. Apromore mines the full ticket-to-resolution path, spotlighting re-visit drivers, aging categories, and property-level variants. Metric: mean-time-to-resolve and first-time-fix rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Yardi Voyager

Key Contact: Thomas Hickey (SVP Operations)

4. Lead-to-Lease Marketing Funnel

ACC runs Marketing Cloud (ExactTarget) plus SMS across university-partnered campaigns to drive tours, applications, and lease signatures. The $331k MC ExactTarget renewal plus a live SMS opp show the channel is core, but the conversion funnel spans MC, Sales Cloud, and Yardi with fractured attribution. Apromore mines the full journey to expose where prospects stall, which sources convert, and how much marketing spend is being wasted on properties already at target occupancy. Metric: cost-per-signed-lease and top-of-funnel-to-lease conversion.
