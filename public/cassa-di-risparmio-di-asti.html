Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Retail Mortgage & Small-Business Loan Origination

Banca di Asti is a Piedmont/Lombardy retail lender where mortgage and small-business loan files traverse relationship managers, credit, underwriting, doc prep, and closing — a hand-off-heavy process that erodes NIM in the current ECB rate environment. AFPI mines the origination event log across the LOS and Salesforce Financial Services Cloud to quantify stage-level cycle times, exception paths, and re-work loops, then hands the evidence to the branch-network heads to prioritize fixes. Metric moved: application-to-fund cycle time, pull-through rate, and per-branch variance.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial Services Cloud, Core Banking / LOS

Key Contact: Carlo Demartini (Amministratore Delegato e Direttore Generale)

2. Post-Merger Process Harmonization (Biverbanca Legacy) & MuleSoft Integration

Biverbanca was fully absorbed into Banca di Asti in 2021, but dual-stack processes and duplicated party/account records still persist in the branch network and back office. With MuleSoft actively deployed and a live $100K MuleSoft IDP opportunity in-flight, AFPI mines the reconciliation, master-data steward, and cross-stack integration events to expose where harmonization is stalling — a natural revenue-per-customer story the ICT and Organizzazione teams can take to the board. Metric moved: duplicate-record rate, integration throughput, steward hours per 1,000 records.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft, Financial Services Cloud

Key Contact: Stefano Vaccaneo (Office Manager - ICT)

3. Complaint (Reclami) Handling & PSD2 Dispute Management

Italian retail banks face strict Bank of Italy timelines on complaint handling (Reclami) and PSD2 dispute resolution, with escalation to the ABF (Arbitro Bancario Finanziario) if SLAs slip. AFPI mines the reclamo-to-resolution event log across Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud outreach, and the back-office to expose SLA breaches, escalation loops, and evidence-gathering delays. Compliance Center produces the audit dossier when the ABF or CONSOB asks. Metric moved: complaint-SLA compliance, resolution cycle time, escalation-to-ABF rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Financial Services Cloud

Key Contact: Alessio Vessoso (Responsabile Ufficio Organizzazione)

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. DORA ICT Risk & Third-Party Register Controls

The EU Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) now requires Italian credit institutions to maintain an evidenced ICT risk framework, an incident-classification workflow, and a Registro dei Fornitori ICT (third-party register) that Bank of Italy examiners can audit on demand. Compliance Center codifies the ICT incident, third-party onboarding, and resilience-testing workflows on top of Salesforce Platform and MuleSoft integration logs. Especially timely as Agentforce for Service goes live — every new AI-touched process needs DORA-grade lineage. Metric moved: incident-classification cycle time, third-party register completeness, audit-ready evidence coverage.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Platform, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Leonardo Prunotto (Data Governance e ICT Risk Manager)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. AML / SOS Filing & 6AMLD Alert Adjudication

As an EU credit institution supervised by Bank of Italy and subject to 5AMLD/6AMLD, Banca di Asti runs transaction monitoring, sanctions screening, and Segnalazioni Operazioni Sospette (SOS) filing to UIF. Compliance Center gives UIF and Bank of Italy examiners an evidenced control story on why alerts were closed or filed, with codified SLAs across L1, L2, and Antiriciclaggio escalation. Metric moved: alert-clearing SLA hit-rate, time-to-SOS, false-positive rate.
