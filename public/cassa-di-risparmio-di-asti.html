Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Loan Origination & Underwriting (Retail Mortgage + Small Business)

Banca di Asti is a Piedmont/Lombardy retail lender where mortgage and small-business loan files traverse relationship managers, credit, underwriting, doc prep, and closing — a hand-off-heavy process that erodes NIM in the current ECB rate environment. AFPI mines the origination event log across the LOS and Salesforce Sales Cloud (in the $1.34M renewal) to quantify stage-level cycle times, exception paths, and re-work loops, then hands the evidence to the branch-network heads to prioritize fixes. Compliance Center provides Bank of Italy examiners a defensible view of covenant reviews and disclosure timing. Metric moved: application-to-fund cycle time, pull-through rate, and per-branch variance.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Sales Cloud, Core Banking / LOS (TBD)

2. Contact Center & Digital-Channel Case Handling (Service Cloud + Filiale OnLine)

The bank runs Service Cloud as its Call Center platform and operates a "Filiale OnLine" digital branch under Stefano Salvi. AFPI mines the case-handling event log — Service Cloud statuses, agent actions, escalations, and transfers to branch — to isolate the top intents by AHT and re-open rate. That evidence turns any future Agentforce agent (the prior generic Agentforce pilots went Dead-Lost) into a targeted, ROI-defensible deployment aimed at complaint handling, PSD2 dispute flows, and internet-banking support. Metric moved: AHT, first-contact resolution, complaint-SLA adherence.

3. KYC / AML Alert Investigation & SOS Filing (Segnalazioni Operazioni Sospette)

As an EU credit institution supervised by Bank of Italy and subject to EBA / DORA / 5th–6th AML directives, Banca di Asti runs transaction monitoring, sanctions screening, and SOS (Suspicious Transaction Reporting) workflows on top of legacy AML tooling. AFPI mines the alert-to-disposition process — enrichment, L1/L2 hand-offs, escalation to Antiriciclaggio, SOS narrative preparation — to expose bottlenecks and disparate reviewer paths. Compliance Center gives UIF and Bank of Italy examiners an evidenced control story on why alerts were closed or filed. Metric moved: alerts per investigator per day, SOS filing timeliness, false-positive rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: AML / Transaction Monitoring (TBD), Salesforce Platform

4. Post-Merger Process Harmonization (Biverbanca Legacy) & MuleSoft Integration

Biverbanca was fully absorbed into Banca di Asti in 2021, but dual-stack processes and duplicated party/account records persist in the branch network and back office. With MuleSoft actively deployed and a live $100K MuleSoft IDP opportunity in-flight, AFPI mines the reconciliation, master-data steward, and cross-stack integration events to show where harmonization is stalling — a natural revenue-per-customer story the ICT / Organizzazione teams can take to the board. 5. Complaint Handling & PSD2 / MiFID II Dispute Management

Italian retail banks face strict Bank of Italy timelines on complaint handling (Reclami) and PSD2 dispute resolution, and MiFID II adds appropriateness/suitability audit demands on the investment side (Paolo Canale runs Finanza; Valentina Argenta runs asset management). AFPI mines the reclamo-to-resolution event log across Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud outreach, and the back-office to expose SLA breaches, escalation loops, and evidence-gathering delays. Compliance Center produces the audit dossier when the ABF (Arbitro Bancario Finanziario) or CONSOB asks. Metric moved: complaint-SLA compliance, resolution cycle time, escalation-to-ABF rate.
