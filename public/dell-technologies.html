Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Quote-to-Cash cycle time in CSG configure-price-quote. Dell CSG runs one of the largest CPQ deployments on the platform (70,000 partner-community CPQ seats, 3,750 CPQ Plus internal), and quote velocity directly caps commercial-PC and server bookings. AFPI mines the Salesforce CPQ event log to expose approval bounce-backs, discount-tier re-work, and configuration-rule dead-ends that Regrello workflows have been papering over. The play stacks natively on the in-flight Regrello-to-AFO Migration — every rework loop AFPI surfaces becomes a targetable AFO agent — and moves quote-to-order cycle time and discount leakage. Engage John Byrne (President, CSG) as the executive sponsor.

2. Field Service dispatch and technician utilization (ProSupport). With 12,000+ Field Service Unlimited seats and legacy ClickSoftware forecasting still on the account, Dell's ProSupport dispatch process is a prime AFPI target: mine the Service Cloud + Field Service event stream to find travel waste, appointment rescheduling loops, and parts-mismatch truck-rolls that AFO's autonomous scheduling can eliminate. Metric moved: first-time-fix rate and technician revenue-hours per week — a 3-point lift on 12k techs is real EBITDA. This is the highest-ROI wedge tied to the $1.8M Agentforce Operations deal in stage 5. Champion: Doug Schmitt (President, Services & Digital).

3. Renewal & entitlement process for the $68.3M SFA re-up. Dell's own $68M SFA renewal running in stage 01 is the internal analog of exactly what AFPI mines for its customers — Dell's Deal Desk and Renewals org runs on Salesforce, and internal renewal-cycle friction (approvals, entitlement checks, product-family remaps) is the tell-tale signal that a process-intelligence layer is missing. Position AFPI as the instrumentation for Dell's own SF-on-SF renewal motion, then productize the same mining logic against Dell customer renewals. Metric: forecast-to-close variance and renewal cycle time. Engage Bill Scannell (President, Global Sales & Customer Ops).

4. Order orchestration and MuleSoft-migration process transparency. The active $1.99M MuleSoft Migration project rewires Dell's order and fulfillment integration fabric; AFPI reads the MuleSoft + Salesforce event log to surface where orders actually stall (credit-hold, config-hold, backorder, entitlement-lookup) instead of relying on tribal knowledge. Pain today is that Dell's Order Management team can tell you a truck rolled but not why a $200k server config sat 9 days between "Booked" and "Manufactured." AFPI moves order-to-manufacture cycle time and on-time shipment. Engage the Global Operations lead.

5. Marketing Cloud + Data Cloud lead-to-opportunity conversion (ISG demand-gen). The $1.4M Data Cloud for Marketing Cloud opp signals Dell is unifying customer data across ExactTarget Enterprise, Account Engagement Ultimate, and Sales Cloud. AFPI mines the lead-to-opp handoff to expose where MQLs die (SDR-to-AE handoff SLAs, dedupe re-work, campaign-attribution loops) — the exact process gap Marketing Ops teams struggle to see with dashboards alone. Metric moved: MQL-to-SQL conversion and lead-response time. Champion: the CMO / ISG marketing ops lead.
