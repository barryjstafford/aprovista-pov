Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. APRA CPS 230 Operational Resilience & Critical-Operations Controls

CPS 230 came into force in July 2025 and requires ADIs to identify critical operations, map end-to-end dependencies, and continuously test tolerances. TMBL's post-merger operating model — four brands on shared UltraData plumbing — makes manual attestation of those dependencies fragile. Compliance Center codifies the CPS 230 control set against mined event data from UltraData and Genesys so tolerance breaches, third-party dependency drift, and single-points-of-failure are surfaced as they happen. Moves regulator confidence and time spent on manual attestation.
