Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Retail Client Onboarding & KYC Refresh (Macquarie Bank / BFS)

Onboarding a new retail or private-wealth client at Macquarie touches origination, identity verification, AUSTRAC AML/CTF checks, sanctions screening, and periodic KYC refresh — a process routinely audited by APRA and ASIC. AFPI mines the FSC + core banking event log to expose rework loops, aged cases waiting on document upload, and screening false-positive queues; the pain is 10-15 day median TAT vs. a fintech benchmark of <48 hours. The metric moved is straight-through-processing rate on new accounts and days-to-first-transaction.

2. Mortgage Origination & Broker Workflow (Macquarie Home Loans)

Macquarie is the fastest-growing mortgage lender in Australia (13.1% market share, FY25) and 100% broker-distributed, which makes origination cycle time a direct competitive weapon. AFPI mines the loan-origination event log across broker submission → credit assessment → valuation → formal approval → settlement, exposing the exact hand-offs and rework causing lost deals; today Macquarie touts ~1 business day unconditional approval and needs to defend that. The metric moved is time-to-formal-approval and application withdrawal rate.
