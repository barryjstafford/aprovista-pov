Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. RG 271 Complaints Handling & Remediation (Group-wide)

Post-Royal-Commission, Australian FS players operate under continuous scrutiny; ASIC's RG 271 mandates strict complaints handling SLAs and Macquarie has publicly-disclosed remediation programs in-flight. Compliance Center runs complaints intake → triage → root-cause → remediation payout as a controlled workflow with codified SLAs, sign-offs, and always-on evidence. This is the "audit-ready process" APRA and ASIC ask for and lines up with the Salesforce Shield footprint already deployed. Metric moved: % complaints resolved inside RG 271 timeframe and remediation cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial Services Cloud, complaints case management

Key Contact: Fiona Herbert (Chief Risk Officer, Macquarie Bank)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. AUSTRAC AML/CTF Alert Adjudication

Macquarie Bank and MAM operate under AUSTRAC AML/CTF obligations plus cross-border AML requirements from FCA, MAS, HKMA, and FinCEN in overseas books. Compliance Center gives a single controlled workflow for alert triage, escalation, SAR/SMR filing and periodic KYC refresh — with codified SLAs and audit evidence. Especially valuable as CGM institutional onboarding scales and the mortgage book grows. Metric moved: alert-clearing SLA hit-rate and time-to-report.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: AML monitoring platform, case management

Key Contact: Fiona Herbert (Chief Risk Officer, Macquarie Bank)

3. CPS 230 Operational Risk & Third-Party Controls

APRA's CPS 230 (in force from July 2025) requires banks to identify critical operations, map them end-to-end, define tolerance levels, and monitor third-party service providers with formal evidence. Compliance Center couples the process-mining view of "critical operations" with a controlled workflow for tolerance breaches, incidents, and third-party attestations — producing continuous evidence for APRA supervision. Metric moved: critical-operation coverage and mean-time-to-evidence on tolerance breaches.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Operational risk / GRC platform, vendor management
