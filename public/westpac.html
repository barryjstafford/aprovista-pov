Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Mortgage Origination Cycle Time

Home lending is Westpac's largest earning asset and the origination journey spans broker submission, credit decisioning, valuation, and settlement across legacy platforms. Post-remediation, turnaround-time consistency has been a repeated APRA and broker-channel pain point. AFPI mines the event log across the loan-origination stack to expose rework loops, verification bottlenecks, and broker abandonment triggers, moving the median time-to-unconditional-approval and reducing broker churn.

3. Complaints Handling & AFCA-Reportable Disputes

APRA CPS 230 (effective July 2025) requires demonstrable operational-resilience controls over customer-facing critical operations, and complaints handling is one of the most scrutinised. AFPI mines the complaint lifecycle from first contact through resolution and AFCA escalation, exposing which channels, products, and handoffs are driving rework and breach risk. The result is a Compliance Center wedge with process evidence auditors can consume directly, plus a measurable drop in AFCA-escalated volume.

4. Business & Institutional Client Onboarding

Onboarding a business or institutional client at Westpac touches KYB, AML, credit, product setup, and multiple divisional handoffs across Business & Wealth and WIB. Deal desks report weeks of cycle time and repeated document requests that erode NPS with corporate treasurers. AFPI mines the end-to-end onboarding process, quantifies the cost of rework loops, and pinpoints where Agentforce or straight-through-processing can compress cycle time. Metric moved: time-to-first-transaction and onboarding cost per client.

5. Technology Modernisation & Simplification (CORE / UNITE)

Westpac's multi-year CORE program is consolidating retail brands onto a single technology stack — a rare, board-sponsored process-redesign event. Without a process-mining ground truth, "to-be" designs are drawn from interviews and PowerPoint rather than actual event data, which drives rework once code hits production. AFPI provides the as-is object-centric process model that CORE work-streams can use to prioritise which processes to simplify first and prove benefit realisation post-migration.
