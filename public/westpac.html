Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Mortgage Origination Cycle-Time (Core Service — Loan Applications)

Home lending is Westpac's largest earning asset and the $400k Core Service — Loan Applications opp is already scoped around origination cycle-time. The end-to-end journey hops from broker submission through credit decisioning, valuation, and settlement across legacy platforms — broker channel churn tracks directly to TAT variance. AFPI mines the loan-origination event log to expose rework loops, verification bottlenecks, and where broker cases abandon, then feeds those hotspots into the Agentforce automation backlog. Metric moved: median time-to-unconditional-approval and broker channel NPS.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Loan origination platform, broker portal

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. APRA CPS 230 Operational Resilience (Live Opp)

The open $200k Operational Resilience CPS 230 opp is the direct wedge: CPS 230 (effective July 2025) requires demonstrable controls over customer-facing critical operations with mapped dependencies, tested tolerances, and evidence auditors can consume. Compliance Center codifies the critical-operation process, runs the resilience-tolerance checks as a controlled workflow, and produces always-on evidence. Beats attestation-plus-spreadsheet approaches on time-to-audit. Metric moved: audit-ready critical operations covered and mean-time-to-evidence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Critical-operations mapping tool, GRC platform

Key Contact: Scott Collary (Group Executive, Customer Services & Technology)
