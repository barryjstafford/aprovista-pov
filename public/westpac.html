Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Mortgage Origination Cycle Time. Home lending is Westpac's largest earning asset and the origination journey spans broker submission, credit decisioning, valuation, and settlement across legacy platforms. Post-remediation, turnaround-time consistency has been a repeated APRA and broker-channel pain point. AFPI mines the event log across the loan-origination stack to expose rework loops, verification bottlenecks, and broker abandonment triggers, moving the median time-to-unconditional-approval and reducing broker churn. Engage Damien MacRae, Managing Director Mortgages.

2. AML/KYC Transaction Monitoring Alert Triage. Post-AUSTRAC settlement, Westpac has invested heavily in financial-crime operations but analysts still face high false-positive rates and inconsistent case-handling paths. AFPI mines the alert-to-disposition process to surface the variants that drive re-work and SLA breaches, then targets automation and Agentforce-assist opportunities on the highest-volume paths. Metric moved: cost per alert closed and productive-alert ratio. Engage Les Vance, Group Executive Financial Crime, Compliance & Conduct.

3. Complaints Handling & AFCA-Reportable Disputes. APRA CPS 230 (effective July 2025) requires demonstrable operational-resilience controls over customer-facing critical operations, and complaints handling is one of the most scrutinised. AFPI mines the complaint lifecycle from first contact through resolution and AFCA escalation, exposing which channels, products, and handoffs are driving rework and breach risk. The result is a Compliance Center wedge with process evidence auditors can consume directly, plus a measurable drop in AFCA-escalated volume. Engage Jason Yetton, Chief Executive Consumer.

4. Business & Institutional Client Onboarding. Onboarding a business or institutional client at Westpac touches KYB, AML, credit, product setup, and multiple divisional handoffs across Business & Wealth and WIB. Deal desks report weeks of cycle time and repeated document requests that erode NPS with corporate treasurers. AFPI mines the end-to-end onboarding process, quantifies the cost of rework loops, and pinpoints where Agentforce or straight-through-processing can compress cycle time. Metric moved: time-to-first-transaction and onboarding cost per client. Engage Anthony Miller, Chief Executive Business & Wealth.

5. Technology Modernisation & Simplification (CORE / UNITE). Westpac's multi-year CORE program is consolidating retail brands onto a single technology stack — a rare, board-sponsored process-redesign event. Without a process-mining ground truth, "to-be" designs are drawn from interviews and PowerPoint rather than actual event data, which drives rework once code hits production. AFPI provides the as-is object-centric process model that CORE work-streams can use to prioritise which processes to simplify first and prove benefit realisation post-migration. Metric moved: rework in transformation delivery and time-to-benefit. Engage Scott Collary, Group Executive Customer Services & Technology.
