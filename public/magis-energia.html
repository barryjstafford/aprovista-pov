Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Post-Merger Meter-to-Cash Harmonisation (AGSM + AIM)

Following the December 2025 birth of MAGIS S.p.A., two legacy meter-to-cash stacks — one from AGSM Verona, one from AIM Vicenza — still coexist across 890,000 electricity and gas customers. Bill exceptions, credit-note rework, and dunning variants inflate DSO and drive contact-centre volume every cycle. AFPI mines the end-to-end read-to-bill-to-cash event log to expose which legacy variants can be retired first and which customer segments to migrate to the target platform, giving the harmonisation programme a defensible as-is baseline. Metric moved: billing-exception rate, DSO, cost-to-bill per customer.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP IS-U / billing stack (TBD), Sales Cloud CRM

Key Contact: Davide Casarin (Responsabile Reporting e Transformation Office)

2. Field-Service Dispatch & First-Time-Fix (V-Reti + Magis Calore)

V-Reti (electricity/gas distribution) and Magis Calore (district heating, 8 cogeneration plants) share a field force across Verona, Vicenza and the wider Veneto — with EIB funding accelerating grid modernisation work orders. AFPI mines the dispatch-to-close event log in Salesforce Field Service alongside V-Reti's SCADA/OMS interfaces to expose repeat-visit patterns, parts-truck-roll misalignment, and SLA-breach clusters by asset class. The result is measurable first-time-fix improvement and lower cost-to-serve on regulated distribution and district-heating jobs alike. Metric moved: first-time-fix rate, mean-time-to-restore, cost per work order.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, SCADA/OMS (TBD)

Key Contact: Michele Bendinelli (Responsabile Innovazione, Gruppo Magis)

3. Customer Switch-In & Contract Activation on the Free Market

Italy's electricity free-market transition (fine tutela) has made customer acquisition and switch-in cycle time a direct competitive lever — Magis Energia sells power, gas and heat on the mercato libero to households, condominiums, businesses and PA. Today the switch-in journey traverses web/agent channels, credit checks, ARERA-mandated data exchanges with Acquirente Unico, and back-office activation with variants that lengthen time-to-first-bill. AFPI mines the quote-to-activation event log across Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud journeys, and back-office queues to compress cycle time and lift agent-network conversion. Metric moved: time-to-activation, switch-in abandonment rate, cost per acquisition.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud (ExactTarget)

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ARERA Data Exchange & Free-Market Switch-In Controls

ARERA (Italy's energy regulator) mandates strict SLAs and data-exchange formats for supplier switching, meter reads, and customer-consent handling with Acquirente Unico — enforced with monetary penalties. Compliance Center codifies the switch-in workflow as a controlled process with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and always-on evidence for ARERA and regional authority inquiries. Especially important as Magis expands the free-market book across the Veneto and beyond. Metric moved: ARERA-SLA breach rate, audit-ready cases, mean-time-to-evidence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, Acquirente Unico integration logs

Key Contact: Head of Regulatory Affairs, Magis Energia (TBD — field verify)

2. NIS2 Critical-Infrastructure Cyber-Resilience Evidence

NIS2 designates energy suppliers and distribution operators as essential entities with binding incident reporting, risk-management, and executive-accountability duties across the group — AGSM Verona, V-Reti, Magis Power, and Magis Calore all in scope. Compliance Center runs the NIS2 control set (24h early-warning, 72h incident notification, executive attestation) as a controlled workflow with codified SLAs and continuous evidence. That collapses today's spreadsheet + email trail into a defensible audit posture for the Italian NIS2 competent authority. Metric moved: 24h/72h notification hit-rate, controls with continuous evidence, executive sign-off cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SIEM / SOC platform, GRC tooling (TBD)
