Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Post-Merger Meter-to-Cash Harmonisation (AGSM + AIM)

Following the December 2025 birth of MAGIS S.p.A., two legacy meter-to-cash stacks — one from AGSM Verona, one from AIM Vicenza — still coexist across 890,000 electricity and gas customers. Bill exceptions, credit-note rework and dunning variants inflate DSO and drive contact-centre volume every cycle. AFPI mines the end-to-end read-to-bill-to-cash event log to expose which legacy variants can be retired first and which customer segments to migrate to the target platform, providing a defensible as-is baseline for the harmonisation programme. Metric moved: billing-exception rate, DSO, and cost-to-bill per customer.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP IS-U / billing stack (TBD), Sales Cloud CRM

Key Contact: Davide Casarin (Responsabile Reporting e Transformation Office)

2. Field-Service Dispatch & First-Time-Fix (V-Reti + Magis Calore)

V-Reti (electricity/gas distribution) and Magis Calore (district heating, 8 cogeneration plants) run a shared field force across Verona, Vicenza and the wider Veneto — with EIB funding accelerating grid modernisation work orders. AFPI mines the dispatch-to-close event log in Salesforce Field Service alongside V-Reti's SCADA/OMS interfaces to expose repeat-visit patterns, parts-truck-roll misalignment, and SLA-breach clusters by asset class. The result is measurable first-time-fix improvement and lower cost-to-serve on both regulated distribution and district-heating jobs. Metric moved: first-time-fix rate, mean-time-to-restore, cost per work order.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, SCADA/OMS (TBD)

Key Contact: Michele Bendinelli (Responsabile Innovazione, Gruppo Magis)

3. Customer Switch-In / Contract Activation on the Free Market

Italy's electricity free-market transition (fine tutela) has made customer acquisition and switch-in cycle time a direct competitive lever — Magis Energia sells power, gas and heat on the mercato libero to households, condominiums, businesses and PA. Today the switch-in journey traverses web/agent channels, credit checks, ARERA-mandated data exchanges with Acquirente Unico, and back-office activation with variants that lengthen time-to-first-bill and inflate abandonment. AFPI mines the quote-to-activation event log across Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud journeys and back-office queues to compress cycle time and lift agent-network conversion. Metric moved: time-to-activation, switch-in abandonment rate, cost per acquisition.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud (ExactTarget)

Key Contact: Donata Dallavo (Direzione Vendite e Processi Commerciali)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
4. Credit & Collections on the Regulated + Free Market Book

Italian multi-utility bad-debt exposure has stayed elevated post-2022 energy-price spike and the mix of regulated (maggior tutela residual) and free-market receivables complicates dunning strategy. AFPI mines the invoice-to-cash and dunning event log to reveal which customer segments respond to which reminder sequence, where manual credit-note rework compounds, and where legally-required steps (e.g. constituzione in mora) are delayed. The output is a data-grounded segmentation for Agentforce-assisted collections and a defensible reduction in provisions. Metric moved: DSO, bad-debt provision as % of revenue, contact-centre volume on billing disputes.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, SAP FI/Billing (TBD)

Key Contact: Stefania Demenego (Gestione del credito)

5. Renewables Project & Grid-Connection Onboarding (Magis Power + V-Reti)

Gruppo Magis is investing €137M/year in the energy transition — 22 recently-acquired PV plants (~85 MWp), the largest group PV park in Trissino, and continued hydro/wind/geothermal capacity. Each project journey — permitting, EPC handoff, grid-connection request via V-Reti, GSE incentive filing, commissioning — spans multiple companies and regulators, and today lives in email plus point tools. AFPI mines the project-and-connection lifecycle event log to expose which permitting gates and internal handoffs slip most, so the programme office can pull them onto the critical path first. Metric moved: time from permit filing to first kWh, and internal handoff delays per MW commissioned.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Project/PMO system (TBD), V-Reti connection-request platform (TBD)
