1. Field Service Dispatch & Contractor Handoff

The $199k FS swap adds 150 dispatchers and 175 contractor logins to Field Service — day one, AGI has a rich Service Appointment event stream covering scheduling, in-house tech dispatch, and the contractor broadcast handoff. Today ~33 internal Field Service techs cover local jobs while everything else flows to contractors through AGI's in-house portal, so mining exposes where dispatch bounces between the two channels, where SLAs slip, and where re-visits cluster. AFPI feeds those exceptions to Agentforce Operations for auto-remediation. Metric moved: first-time-fix rate and technician / contractor utilization.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ADA & Building-Code Signage Compliance Attestation

Architectural signage must comply with ADA, IBC, and jurisdiction-specific wayfinding codes on every install — today the attestations sit in project files and email threads. Compliance Center codifies the design-review, install-verification, and customer sign-off steps as a controlled workflow with evidence attached to each work order. Cuts audit risk and speeds up commercial customer close-out. Metric moved: attestations captured on time and audit-ready installs percentage.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, AGI project system

Key Contact: Head of Quality & Compliance (TBD — field verify)

2. Contractor Onboarding & Credential Controls

175+ contractor logins in the pipeline means AGI must run a repeatable process for insurance, W-9, background, safety-training, and portal-access provisioning — today that's a manual checklist.
