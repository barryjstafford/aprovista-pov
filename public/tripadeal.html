1. Booking-to-Fulfillment Cycle Time

TripADeal's core product is packaged travel deals combining flights, tours, and accommodation — every booking touches multiple suppliers, GDS lookups, and finance confirmations. Mining the end-to-end lead-to-booked-trip flow will expose where manual supplier confirmations and payment holds add days to fulfillment. AFPI can quantify variants that quietly hit escalation queues and cost margin. Target metric: reduce median booking-to-confirmation cycle by 30%.
