Mining the end-to-end lead-to-booked-trip flow will expose where manual supplier confirmations and payment holds add days to fulfillment. AFPI can quantify variants that quietly hit escalation queues and cost margin. Target metric: reduce median booking-to-confirmation cycle by 30%.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Australian Consumer Law (ACL) Refund & Dispute Controls

ACCC's enforcement of Australian Consumer Law on travel refunds — especially post-cancellation and involuntary schedule changes — puts consumer-remedy timelines under scrutiny. Compliance Center codifies the refund-eligibility, approval, and payout workflow with segregation-of-duties and evidence per case, so every refund decision is defensible. Especially important given Qantas Group's ACCC history and TripADeal's consumer-facing brand exposure. Target metric: refund-SLA hit-rate and time-to-decision on disputed cases.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud case management, payment gateway

Key Contact: Shannon Knapp (Customer Operations & Logistics Manager)

2. PCI-DSS Payment & Cardholder-Data Controls

TripADeal takes tens of thousands of card-not-present transactions across web, contact-centre, and payment-plan channels — PCI-DSS 4.0 requires evidenced controls on every path where cardholder data flows. Compliance Center runs the merchant-side control workflow (access reviews, tokenisation checks, dispute chargeback handling) as an auditable process rather than annual attestation. Metric: number of PCI-scope controls with continuous evidence and mean-time-to-remediation on findings.
