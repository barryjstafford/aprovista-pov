2. Post-Acquisition Client Onboarding Harmonization

MPM has consolidated Assist Plan Managers and NDSP Plan Managers and is onboarding 1,000+ new NDIS participants per month. Each acquired book brought its own onboarding variant — document capture, plan validation, service agreement issuance — and CTO Richard Hilliard has publicly flagged consolidation as the primary growth lever. Mining across the merged tenants exposes the divergent paths, hand-offs, and SLA breach hotspots so a single canonical onboarding can be enforced. Metric moved: onboarding cycle time -30%, first-invoice-processed lag -40%.

Mining the end-to-end journey (claim raised → validated → funded → paid) across Service Cloud, MuleSoft integrations, and Heroku-powered provider portals surfaces the bottleneck steps and lets ops target the highest-friction provider cohorts first.
