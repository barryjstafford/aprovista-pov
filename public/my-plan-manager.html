Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Invoice-to-Pay Exception Mining (the Last 30%)

MPM has publicly reported 70% touchless invoice processing and ~100,000 hours saved via Service Cloud automation, but the remaining 30% consumes disproportionate operator time and carries duplicate-payment risk across ~60,000 clients. Mining the actual invoice event log will surface why exceptions loop, which provider types drive rework, and where the automation guardrails misfire. AFPI overlays Service Cloud + MuleSoft events to isolate the specific steps that leak time. Metric moved: touchless invoice rate from 70% → 85%+, cost-per-invoice down ~25%.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, MuleSoft

2. Post-Acquisition Client Onboarding Harmonization

MPM has consolidated Assist Plan Managers and NDSP Plan Managers and is onboarding 1,000+ new NDIS participants per month. Each acquired book brought its own onboarding variant — document capture, plan validation, service agreement issuance — and consolidation is now the primary growth lever. Mining across the merged tenants exposes the divergent paths, hand-offs, and SLA breach hotspots so a single canonical onboarding can be enforced. Metric moved: onboarding cycle time -30%, first-invoice-processed lag -40%.

3. Participant Service-to-Cash Journey (Provider Payments)

MPM sits between NDIS participants and thousands of service providers, mediating the pay-provider cycle. Mining the end-to-end journey (claim raised → validated → funded → paid) across Service Cloud, MuleSoft integrations, and Heroku-powered provider portals surfaces the bottleneck steps and lets ops target the highest-friction provider cohorts first. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. NDIS / NDIA Audit-Trail & Conformance Evidence

NDIS plan managers operate under NDIA oversight with hard rules on funding allocation, provider vetting, and participant safeguards. Compliance today is largely policy plus manual sampling. Compliance Center runs the compliance-relevant event streams — plan changes, budget movements, provider onboarding — as a controlled workflow with continuous, audit-ready conformance evidence and flags deviation patterns before an NDIA review surfaces them. Metric moved: audit prep time -50%, compliance findings caught pre-audit rather than post.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Donna Radway (Quality Assurance & Continuous Improvement Manager)

2. Provider Vetting, Fraud & Duplicate-Payment Controls

Duplicate invoices, ghost providers, and misaligned service categories are the classic fraud vectors in NDIS plan management — and they're exactly the kind of control that regulators expect to see codified after each sector inquiry. Compliance Center wraps provider vetting, duplicate detection, and payment approvals into a single controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and an evidence chain. Sits directly on the Heroku + MuleSoft data spine already in place. Metric moved: duplicate-payment leakage detected pre-payment, control breach evidence produced automatically.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft, Heroku
