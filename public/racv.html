Topline POV: Apromore can help Royal Automobile Club of Victoria (RACV) Ltd by mining the roadside-assistance dispatch, insurance claims, and membership renewal journeys across their live Salesforce, MuleSoft, and Field Service stack — collapsing discovery from weeks to days, exposing the exact bottlenecks driving cycle-time and NPS erosion, and building the event-log foundation that will let Agentforce Operations act on those processes without a costly Celonis-style two-specialist model.

Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Roadside Assistance Dispatch-to-Resolution

RACV's flagship service — over 2 million call-outs per year — runs through Field Service dispatch, contractor networks, and member callback loops. Variability in triage, tow vs on-site repair decisions, and contractor SLAs drives cycle-time and satisfaction swings that today are only visible in aggregate dashboards. Apromore surfaces the exact deviations (retriage, callback, missed-ETA) as process variants and quantifies their cost in minutes and $ per member. That directly informs the RACV Field Service #DF2U project already in flight.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, MuleSoft (contractor integrations)

Key Contact: General Manager, Motoring & Mobility (RACV)

2. Insurance Claims Lifecycle (IMA JV)

Through Insurance Manufacturers of Australia (JV with IAG), RACV handles home and motor claims for Victorian members. Claims processes are notorious for rework loops (assessor callbacks, disputed excesses, repair-network handoffs) that inflate cycle time and drive complaint volume. Apromore's variant analysis on the claims event log will pinpoint which handoffs cause the top 20% of duration outliers — the classic input for STP (straight-through processing) uplift. Every 10% cycle-time reduction on ~$1B claims volume is material.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, IAG core claims platform

Key Contact: Head of Claims Operations (RACV Insurance)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Membership Onboarding, Renewal & Churn Prevention

RACV's mutual model depends on member retention across ~2.1M memberships — motoring, home, insurance cross-hold. The renewal journey (touchless renewal automation is already an in-flight SF opp) blends Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud/ExactTarget, and Customer Portal, with hand-offs to call centre for exceptions. Apromore will map the actual renewal path per cohort and expose where members drop out or downgrade — feeding both the ARY Touchless Renewal opp and the Marketing Cloud journey design.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud / ExactTarget

Key Contact: Head of Membership & Retention (RACV)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
4. RACV Solar Order-to-Install

RACV Solar (formerly Gippsland Solar) runs a quote → survey → install → activation process that touches sales, subcontracted electricians, DNSP grid-connect approvals, and finance. Long tail delays in DNSP approvals and installer scheduling erode margin and NPS. Apromore can benchmark median vs 90th-percentile install cycle time, identify the specific approval and rework loops that stretch the tail, and give ops a live dashboard rather than a monthly rear-view. Direct input to any RACV energy expansion.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, RACV Solar quoting/CRM
