Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Roadside Assistance Dispatch-to-Resolution

RACV's flagship service — more than 2 million roadside call-outs per year — runs across Field Service dispatch, a contractor network, and member-callback loops. Variability in triage, tow-vs-onsite decisions, and contractor SLAs drives cycle-time and NPS swings that today are only visible in aggregate dashboards. Apromore mines the dispatch event log to surface exactly which variants (retriage, callback, missed-ETA) blow out response time and quantify their cost per member. Metric: median dispatch-to-resolution time and % of jobs within SLA.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, MuleSoft (contractor integrations)

Key Contact: General Manager, Motoring & Mobility (RACV)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Insurance Claims Lifecycle (IMA JV)

Through Insurance Manufacturers of Australia (30% JV with IAG), RACV underwrites home and motor claims for Victorian members. Claims processes are notorious for rework loops — assessor callbacks, disputed excesses, repair-network handoffs — that inflate cycle time and drive complaint volume. Apromore's variant analysis on the claims event log will pinpoint which handoffs cause the top 20% of duration outliers, the classic input for straight-through-processing uplift. Metric: median claim cycle time and rework rate per claim type.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, IAG core claims platform

Key Contact: Head of Claims Operations (RACV Insurance / IMA)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Membership Onboarding, Renewal & Churn Prevention

RACV's mutual model rests on member retention across 2.29M memberships spanning motoring, home, and insurance cross-hold. The renewal journey — the subject of the live ARY Touchless Renewal Automation opp — blends Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud/ExactTarget, and the customer portal, with hand-offs to the call centre for exceptions. Apromore will map the actual renewal path per cohort and expose where members drop out or downgrade, directly feeding the touchless-renewal design. Metric: renewal completion rate and touchless-renewal conversion per cohort.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud / ExactTarget

Key Contact: Head of Membership & Retention (RACV)

RACV's Emergency Roadside contact centre agents juggle the Field Service console, member CRM, contractor dispatch, and internal knowledge tools on every call. Task mining captures each agent's on-screen actions to isolate the after-call-work steps and application-switches that eat wrap time. This feeds an Agentforce Operations business case for automated case creation and dispatch. Metric: average handle time and after-call-work minutes per call type.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud console, Field Service dispatch console

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

The IMA insurance joint venture is regulated by APRA, which requires evidenced controls over underwriting, claims decisions, and complaint handling. Compliance Center runs these as controlled workflows with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and continuous evidence for APRA examiners. Especially important as Agentforce lands in claims and service. Metric: audit-ready control coverage and mean-time-to-evidence per examination.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Underwriting/claims platform, GRC / audit register

2. Australian Consumer Law & Financial Services Complaint Handling (AFCA)

ASIC RG 271 and the AFCA scheme mandate 30-day resolution windows and full traceability on member complaints across insurance, finance, and roadside. Compliance Center codifies the intake-triage-resolve-escalate workflow with automatic SLA controls and evidence — today handled through a patchwork of Service Cloud cases and spreadsheets. Metric: RG 271 SLA hit-rate and % of complaints closed with full evidence chain.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, complaints case-management system

Key Contact: Head of Customer Advocacy & Complaints (RACV)
