Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Trade Settlement Exception Handling (T+1 & Beyond)

DTCC clears and settles the vast majority of US equities and fixed-income trades daily through NSCC, FICC, and DTC — with T+1 now live and pressure building for T+0, every settlement exception is expensive latency risk. Apromore mines the trade lifecycle across NSCC/CTM/ITP event logs to expose exception patterns, hand-off delays, and manual rework loops that erode straight-through-processing. The play is to co-sell alongside the open Agentforce AELA opportunity: Apromore supplies the process evidence and Agentforce Operations executes remediation agents on the exceptions surfaced. Metric moved: STP rate lift and exception cycle-time reduction.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: NSCC/FICC clearing platform, CTM/ITP

Key Contact: Lynn Bishop (CIO / Managing Director)

2. Regulatory Reporting & SDR / GTR Workflows

DTCC operates the Global Trade Repository and Swap Data Repository — simultaneously a regulated entity and the regulator's data plumbing for CFTC, ESMA, MAS, and JFSA reporting. Apromore mines the reporting workflows to prove control effectiveness, spot late or failed submissions, and produce audit-grade process evidence for CAT, EMIR REFIT, and DORA obligations. It collapses control-testing from quarters to weeks and gives the CRO a live conformance dashboard auditors can consume directly. Metric moved: reporting-error rate and control-testing cost.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Global Trade Repository (GTR), Swap Data Repository (SDR)

3. Corporate Actions Lifecycle Standardization

Corporate actions — dividends, mergers, tenders, redemptions — remain one of the most error-prone processes in post-trade, and DTCC's ISO 20022 CA transformation is a multi-year program to industrialize it. Apromore mines the CA event lifecycle across CA Web, GCA VS, and downstream member workflows to identify where announcements, elections, and allocations diverge from the target model, and quantifies the cost of each break. This gives the transformation office an evidence base to prioritize automation and rework targets rather than a swag on where money is bleeding. Metric moved: CA break rate and indemnification claims paid.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: CA Web, GCA VS
