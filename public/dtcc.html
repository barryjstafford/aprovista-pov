Topline POV: Apromore can help DTCC (Depository Trust & Clearing) by mining the end-to-end trade settlement, regulatory reporting, and corporate actions event logs to lift straight-through-processing rates, shorten control-testing cycles, and give the open Agentforce AELA program a documented process baseline it can automate against.

Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Trade Settlement Exception Handling (T+1 & Beyond)

DTCC clears and settles the vast majority of US equities and fixed-income trades daily through NSCC, FICC, and DTC — with T+1 now live in the US and pressure building for T+0, every settlement exception is expensive latency risk. AFPI mines the end-to-end trade lifecycle across NSCC/CTM/ITP event logs to expose the exception patterns, hand-off delays, and manual rework loops that erode straight-through-processing rates. The play is to co-sell alongside the open Agentforce AELA opportunity: AFPI supplies the process evidence, Agentforce Operations executes the remediation agents on the exceptions AFPI surfaces. Metric moved: STP rate lift, exception cycle-time reduction, reduction in manual break resolution FTE.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: NSCC, CTM/ITP

Key Contact: Lynn Bishop (CIO / Managing Director)

2. Regulatory Reporting & SDR / GTR Workflows

DTCC operates the Global Trade Repository and Swap Data Repository — meaning it is simultaneously a regulated entity and the regulator's data plumbing for CFTC, ESMA, MAS, and JFSA reporting. AFPI mines the reporting workflows to prove control effectiveness, spot late/failed submissions, and produce audit-grade process evidence — the Compliance-Center wedge is a direct fit here for CAT, EMIR REFIT, and DORA obligations. AFPI shortens control-testing cycles from quarters to weeks and gives the CRO a live conformance dashboard the auditors can consume. Metric moved: reporting-error rate, control-testing cost, regulator remediation cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: GTR, SDR

Key Contact: Timothy Cuddihy (Group Chief Risk Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Client Onboarding & KYC/AML for Institutional Members

Onboarding a new clearing member, fund, or omnibus account at DTCC touches legal, credit, KYC/AML, connectivity, and testing — a multi-week journey with dozens of hand-offs across teams. AFPI mines the onboarding case flow (which can be surfaced through Service Cloud plus supporting systems) to expose bottlenecks, rework loops, and SLA breaches; Agentforce Ops then executes the standard steps AFPI proves are safe to automate. This is the fastest-time-to-value co-sell against Agentforce because the process is bounded, the data is captured, and the pain is quantified. Metric moved: onboarding cycle time (days), first-time-right rate, ops cost per new member.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, KYC/AML platform (TBD)

Key Contact: Nikki Poulos (General Counsel & Managing Director)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
4. Corporate Actions Lifecycle Standardization

Corporate actions — dividends, mergers, tenders, redemptions — remain one of the most error-prone processes in post-trade, and DTCC's ISO 20022 CA transformation is a multi-year program to industrialize it. AFPI mines the CA event lifecycle across CA Web, GCA VS, and downstream member workflows to identify where announcements, elections, and allocations diverge from the target model, and quantifies the cost of each break. This gives the transformation office an evidence base to prioritize automation and re-work targets, rather than a swag on where the money is bleeding. Metric moved: CA break rate, indemnification claims paid, transformation ROI proof.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: CA Web, GCA VS

Key Contact: Nadine Chakar (Managing Director, Digital Assets)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
5. Cyber & Operational Resilience Evidence (DORA-Ready)

As a Systemically Important Financial Market Utility (SIFMU) DTCC is examined by the Fed, SEC, and CFTC on operational resilience; DORA extends the same regime to European counterparties DTCC serves. AFPI mines incident, change, and vendor-management workflows to produce continuous, auditable evidence of control operation — collapsing the reactive spreadsheet drill into a live process view. The Compliance Center is the wedge: it takes the same event data AFPI is already mining and turns it into regulator-consumable attestations. Metric moved: attestation prep time, control-gap detection speed, resilience-test coverage.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Compliance Center, ITSM/change-mgmt platform (TBD)
