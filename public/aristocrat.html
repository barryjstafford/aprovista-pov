2. EGM Order-to-Cash: Sydney & Las Vegas Manufacturing to Casino Floor

Aristocrat runs a dual-hub electronic gaming machine manufacturing footprint (Sydney design/build for ANZ/EMEA; Las Vegas assembly and distribution for the Americas) delivering into a heavily regulated casino-operator customer base. The O2C process — quote, jurisdictional certification, build, ship, install, revenue recognition — has notorious variance because every state/tribal jurisdiction adds its own certification wait state. Apromore can quantify the certification bottleneck and simulate parallel-track approvals; the metric that moves is days from order to installed-and-revenue-recognized.
