Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. HSA/FSA Claim Substantiation & Reimbursement Cycle

Optum Financial administers ~9M HSAs and processes millions of card swipes and manual claims that must be substantiated against IRS Section 213(d) rules. Manual substantiation, receipt requests, and denial appeals create long cycle times, member abrasion, and IRS/CFPB audit exposure. AFPI mines the end-to-end claim flow across Optum Bank core, the mobile app, and back-office ops to expose auto-substantiation bypass rates, appeal loop-backs, and rework hot-spots. Metric moved: substantiation auto-rate, average days-to-resolve appeal, cost-per-claim.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Optum Bank core, HSA mobile app

Key Contact: Bill Miller (CEO, Optum Financial)
