1. HSA/FSA Claim Substantiation & Reimbursement Cycle

Optum Financial administers ~9M HSAs and processes millions of card swipes and manual claims that must be substantiated against IRS Section 213(d) rules. Manual substantiation, receipt requests, and denial appeals create long cycle times, member abrasion, and CFPB/IRS audit risk. AFPI mines the end-to-end claim flow across Optum Bank, the mobile app, and back-office ops to expose auto-substantiation bypass rates, appeal loop-backs, and rework hot-spots.
