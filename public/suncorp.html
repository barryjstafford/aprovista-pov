Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Multi-Brand Claims Lifecycle Harmonisation (AAMI · GIO · Vero)

Suncorp runs three flagship personal-lines brands on shared claims infrastructure but with divergent workflows, SLA definitions and rework loops — FNOL to settlement can vary by weeks across brands for the same peril. With Omni Gateway (MAF) now live for Claims orchestration [Slack], Apromore mines the Guidewire ClaimCenter + MuleSoft event stream to expose the true variant tree, quantify rework cost by brand, and produce a data-backed harmonisation roadmap. Especially load-bearing for the Home Claims Assessor & Repair RFP covering 4,000 internal staff and 3,500 supply-chain partners. Metric: average claim cycle time ↓ 20-30%, straight-through-processing ↑ 15pp.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. APRA CPS 230 Operational Resilience & Control Attestation

APRA CPS 230 (effective July 2025) forces Suncorp to identify its critical operations, map end-to-end process dependencies, define tolerance levels, and evidence controls to the regulator annually. Today this is largely Visio + interview-based across 19 orgs. Apromore's Compliance Center auto-derives the as-is process map from MuleSoft + ServiceNow event logs and continuously monitors conformance against declared tolerances — turning CPS 230 from a 4-6 month project into a live control. Metric: attestation prep cycle ↓ from months to weeks, audit-defensibility ↑.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft (transactional spine), ServiceNow (ITSM)

Key Contact: Priyanka Paranagama (CAIO & Group CTO)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. ASIC RG271 Internal Dispute Resolution & Claims Handling

ASIC RG271 requires financial firms to acknowledge complaints in 24 hours and resolve most in 30 days with a documented decision, and the Insurance Code of Practice layers SLA obligations across every claim. Today complaint and claim decisions are stitched across ClaimCenter, Service Cloud and email — hard to prove regulator-ready timeliness at scale. Compliance Center codifies IDR/claims workflows with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs and continuous conformance monitoring against RG271 timeframes. Metric: RG271 SLA hit-rate ↑, breach-remediation cycle time ↓.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Guidewire ClaimCenter

Key Contact: Lisa Harrison (CEO, Consumer Insurance)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. General Insurance Code of Practice & Financial Hardship Controls

The 2020 GI Code of Practice and vulnerable-customer/financial-hardship rules require Suncorp to identify at-risk customers early, offer tailored support, and evidence every touchpoint back to the regulator. Home Claims (Service Cloud + Data Cloud) is the largest surface for this because storm and flood claimants disproportionately need hardship pathways. Compliance Center runs hardship as a controlled workflow with vulnerability triggers, escalation SLAs and audit trail across every channel. Metric: vulnerable-customer identification lead time ↓, Code-compliance evidence gaps ↓.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Data Cloud
