Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Personal & Corporate Customer Engagement Redesign

The pilot Elena Voli and Emanuele Baruffa already scoped is redesigning how BPER engages personal and corporate customers across multichannel CRM, branch, and digital — a classic case where the "designed" journey and the actual event log diverge sharply. AFPI mines the lead-to-serve process across FSC and Marketing Cloud Engagement to expose where campaigns leak, where advisor hand-offs stall, and which segments never receive next-best-action follow-up. This is the exact scope of the two $50k opps already in Org62, so AFPI is not sold cold — it needs execution to convert. Metric moved: campaign-to-appointment conversion and advisor next-best-action adherence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial Services Cloud, Marketing Cloud Engagement

2. Post-Merger Integration: Carige & Monte Paschi Retail Harmonization

BPER absorbed Banca Carige and is now integrating Monte Paschi retail flows — the "+xx FSC UE for Monte Paschi Integration" opp is live in Org62. 3. Credit Origination & Underwriting (Retail + Commercial + Large Corporate)

BPER's Divisione Retail, Corporate, and Large Corporate teams each run distinct origination flows, and Credit Risk Management has to prove consistent decisions across all three. AFPI mines the application-to-decision-to-fund process across FSC and the LOS to quantify stage-level cycle times, exception paths, and covenant-review rework — the same evidence base that unlocks a targeted Agentforce underwriter assistant later. In a rising-NPL environment for Italian banks, this directly protects the cost-of-risk line. Metric moved: application-to-close cycle time, decision consistency across divisions, exception rate.

Branch relationship managers hop between FSC, core banking, and legacy Carige screens to complete client requests, adjust products, or resolve service issues. Task mining the advisor desktop reveals how much time is lost to app-switching, re-keying customer data, and repeat lookups — the exact steps Agentforce can eliminate. This feeds a direct expansion of the current Apromore pilot into an Agentforce-for-branch business case. Metric moved: touch-time per advisor task and rekey-per-transaction count.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. DORA Operational Resilience & ICT Third-Party Register

DORA is landing on ECB-supervised banks in 2026-27 and demands documented ICT risk management, incident classification, and a maintained register of ICT third-party arrangements. Compliance Center codifies the incident and third-party attestation processes as controlled workflows with evidence and SLA gates, and produces an always-on register auditors can read directly. Especially valuable given BPER's ongoing multi-brand integration where third-party inventory is genuinely hard. Metric moved: DORA register accuracy, incident classification timeliness, exam-ready evidence coverage.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: ITSM, Salesforce Platform

Key Contact: Chief Risk Officer, BPER Banca (TBD — field verify)

2. AML Alert Investigation & Suspicious-Activity Reporting

As an ECB-supervised universal bank, BPER files a large volume of Segnalazioni Antiriciclaggio (Italy's SAR equivalent) and answers to Bank of Italy inspectors. Compliance Center runs alert-to-disposition as a controlled workflow across L1/L2 investigation, escalation, and SAR filing with codified SLAs and evidence — the audit trail Auditing has to produce today by hand.
