Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Personal & Corporate Customer Engagement Redesign (Pilot Use Case)

The pilot Elena Voli and Emanuele Baruffa have already scoped is redesigning how BPER engages personal and corporate customers across multichannel CRM, branch, and digital — a classic case where the "designed" journey and the actual event log diverge sharply. AFPI mines the lead-to-serve process across FSC, Marketing Cloud Engagement, and the multichannel CRM Elena runs to expose where campaigns leak, where advisor hand-offs stall, and which segments never receive next-best-action follow-up. This is the exact scope of the two $50K opps already in Org62, so AFPI is not sold cold — it just needs execution to convert. Metric moved: campaign-to-appointment conversion and advisor next-best-action adherence.

3. AML Alert Investigation & Suspicious-Activity Reporting

As an ECB-supervised universal bank, BPER files a large volume of Segnalazioni Antiriciclaggio (Italy's SAR equivalent) and is directly exposed to DORA operational-resilience requirements landing in 2026-2027. AFPI mines the alert-to-disposition process across transaction monitoring, L1/L2 investigation, and Auditing to expose reviewer variance, escalation rework, and SLA breaches. Compliance Center then produces the evidenced control trail Vincenzo Boldrini's audit function and Bank of Italy inspectors expect. Metric moved: alerts per investigator per day, SAR filing timeliness, false-positive rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Transaction Monitoring (TBD), Salesforce Platform

4. Credit Origination & Underwriting (Retail + Commercial + Large Corporate)

BPER's Divisione Retail, Corporate, and Large Corporate teams each run distinct origination flows, and Livio Tempesta's Credit Risk Management function has to prove consistent risk decisions across all three.
