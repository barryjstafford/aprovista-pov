Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Post-Merger Order-to-Cash Harmonization (DNOW + MRC Global) — DNOW's combination with MRC Global creates two parallel OTC stacks with duplicative branch, credit, and billing steps. Metric moved: quote-to-cash cycle time and DSO. Champion: Ramy Matta (Process Optimization Manager, Projects Team) [Org62].

2. Procure-to-Pay & Supplier Onboarding (MDM adjacency) — The active Informatica MDM Product 360 + Supplier expansion opps confirm master-data pain across a fragmented supplier base. AFPI mines the actual P2P event log — PR → PO → GR → invoice — to prove where duplicate suppliers, off-catalog buys, and manual approvals leak margin, feeding directly into the MDM cleanup program rather than competing with it. Metric moved: PO cycle time, off-catalog spend %. Champion: Jessie Wang (Director, Data and Business Technology) [Org62].

3. Warehouse-to-Ship Fulfillment for PVF, Pumps & Artificial Lift — Distribution of PVF, pumps, and artificial-lift equipment across dozens of branches is a same-day-turn business where pick/pack/ship deviations hit customer NPS immediately. AFPI mines WMS + ERP events to show branches with hidden re-work loops and stockout patterns, then routes the improvement work into Agentforce Operations. Metric moved: perfect-order rate, branch fill rate. Champion: Timm Bohnert (VP Supply Chain) [Org62].

4. Quote-to-Order via eSpec & DigitalNOW eCommerce — DigitalNOW and the eSpec configurator are the digital front door, but the handoff from configured quote to fulfilled order runs through Sales Cloud and the underlying ERP. AFPI reveals where digital orders drop into manual rework — pricing overrides, credit holds, spec exceptions — collapsing the SQL+PQL Celonis model into a single no-code business-analyst view. Metric moved: digital order straight-through rate. Champion: Chris Daniels (Director of Integrated Solutions) [Org62].

5. Credit Approval & Collections Operations — The DNOW Credit Department runs its own Service Cloud Call Center org (open renewal in flight). Credit-app turnaround and collections dunning are event-log-rich processes where AFPI directly quantifies where credit reviews stall and which delinquent-account paths recover cash fastest. Metric moved: credit-app SLA, cash conversion cycle. Champion: Terrie Williams (Director of CSM Business Process) [Org62].
