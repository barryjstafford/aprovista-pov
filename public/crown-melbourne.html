Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. AML/KYC Surveillance & Suspicious Matter Reporting

Crown Melbourne operates under Victorian Special Manager oversight and a 2023 A$450M AUSTRAC undertaking that requires continuous evidence of AML/KYC uplift. The current flow — alert generation, EDD triage, SMR filing, Board Risk Committee reporting — spans multiple surveillance tools with high rework and analyst discretion. Mining the alert-to-SMR lifecycle exposes where alerts stall, where false positives dominate, and where investigator handling diverges from policy. Target metric: alert-to-SMR cycle time and Board-reportable variance against control policy.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SYCO/Bright Path AML surveillance, Salesforce Data Cloud

Key Contact: Chief Risk & Compliance Officer (Chief Risk & Compliance Officer, Crown Resorts)

2. VIP Host & Premium Guest Lifecycle

Since the exit of junket operators post-Royal-Commission, Crown's premium-play model has shifted to direct VIP host management with tightened source-of-funds checks — the very workflow the open $750k Agentforce VIP Experience opp is targeting. Identification, KYC clearance, credit setup, complimentary services, gaming activity, and review are fragmented across CMS, PMS, and Marketing Cloud. Mining the process shows where high-value guests wait for approvals, where hosts freelance around policy, and where recognition breaks between properties. Target metric: VIP onboarding cycle time and per-guest responsible-gambling touchpoint compliance.

3. Integrated Hotel-to-Casino Guest Journey

Crown Melbourne is an integrated resort — three hotels (Towers, Metropol, Promenade), 40+ restaurants and bars, retail, and the casino floor share the same guest. Today the journey is stitched together by Crown Rewards but the operational handoffs — check-in, dining reservation, gaming session, cage transaction, check-out — live in different systems. Mining the multi-touchpoint journey shows which sequences drive repeat premium spend and where friction (cage queues, dining conflicts, upgrades never delivered) costs top-decile guests. Apromore quantifies the operational payoff of the Data Cloud unified profile Mav3rick is standing up. Target metric: multi-touchpoint conversion and top-decile guest retention.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Opera PMS, Salesforce Data Cloud
