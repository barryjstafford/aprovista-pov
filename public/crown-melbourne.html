Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. AML/KYC Surveillance & Suspicious Matter Reporting

Crown Melbourne operates under Special Manager oversight and a 2023 A$450M AUSTRAC undertaking that requires continuous evidence of AML/KYC uplift. The current flow — alert generation, EDD triage, SMR filing, Board Risk Committee reporting — spans multiple surveillance tools and manual case queues with high rework. Mining the alert-to-SMR lifecycle exposes where alerts stall, where false positives dominate, and where analyst discretion diverges from policy. AFPI plus Agentforce Operations orchestrate the deterministic branches and hand exceptions to human analysts with the full evidence pack. Target metric: alert-to-SMR cycle time and Board-reportable variance to policy.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SYCO/Bright Path AML surveillance, Salesforce Data Cloud

2. VIP Host & Premium Guest Lifecycle

Since the exit of junket operators post-Royal-Commission, Crown's premium-play model has shifted to direct VIP host management with tightened source-of-funds checks. The lifecycle — identification, KYC clearance, credit setup, complimentary services, gaming activity, review — is fragmented across CMS, PMS, and Marketing Cloud. Mining the process shows where high-value guests wait for approvals, where hosts free-lance around policy, and where recognition breaks between properties. AFPI plus Marketing Cloud can prove that responsible-gambling controls are working without degrading guest experience. Target metric: VIP onboarding cycle time and per-guest RG-touchpoint compliance.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Bally's/IGT casino management system, Salesforce Marketing Cloud

3. Integrated Hotel-to-Casino Guest Journey (Crown Towers, Metropol, Promenade)

Crown Melbourne is an integrated resort — three hotels, 40+ restaurants and bars, retail, and the casino floor share the same guest. Today the guest journey is stitched together by loyalty (Crown Rewards) but the operational handoffs — check-in, restaurant reservation, gaming session, cage transaction, check-out — live in different systems. Mining the multi-touchpoint journey shows which sequences drive repeat premium spend and which cause friction (queueing at the cage, dining conflicts, room upgrades that never arrive). Apromore quantifies the guest-experience payoff of a Data Cloud unified profile. Target metric: multi-touchpoint conversion and top-decile guest retention.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Opera PMS, Salesforce Data Cloud

Key Contact: Chief Operating Officer (Chief Operating Officer, Crown Melbourne)

4. Cash-Cage & Gaming Machine Reconciliation

Crown operates ~2,600 electronic gaming machines and hundreds of table games — cash and TITO tickets flow between machines, cage, count room, and treasury under strict VGCCC and AUSTRAC controls. Reconciliation exceptions are investigated manually by cage operations and internal audit. Mining the cash-lifecycle event log identifies where variances repeat, which machines drive the most exceptions, and where controls can be automated versus require human review. AFPI plus Agentforce Operations turn each variance into an evidenced case. Target metric: reconciliation cycle time and audit-reportable exception rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Bally's/IGT casino management system, TITO ticket system

Key Contact: Chief Financial Officer (Chief Financial Officer, Crown Resorts)

5. Employee Vetting, Licensing & Ongoing Suitability

Every Crown Melbourne employee touching the gaming floor requires a Special Employee Licence from the VGCCC, plus ongoing suitability monitoring — a workload multiplied by ~11,700 staff and continuous churn. The vetting process spans HR, People & Culture, Legal, and the regulator, with document rework and re-submission a common drag. Mining the license lifecycle shows where submissions get bounced, which document types cause the most rework, and where compliant staff sit idle awaiting clearance. AFPI plus Agentforce Operations pre-flight submissions and coordinate handoffs. Target metric: time-to-badge and license-review pass-rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: HR / Workday, Salesforce Platform
