Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Servizi Ambientali Field Dispatch & Route Optimization

Herambiente and Hera Servizi Ambientali run one of Italy's largest waste-collection and environmental-services operations, with an active $581K flotte replacement modernizing dispatch on Salesforce Field Service. AFPI mines the dispatch-to-completion event log — work-order creation, crew assignment, route deviation, on-site duration, exception codes — to expose where routes get re-planned, where crews idle, and where the same address gets re-serviced. That evidence turns a like-for-like fleet replacement into a measurable route- and crew-productivity story, directly reinforcing the $50K SWE-funded discovery already approved for Hera Waste Management. Metric moved: jobs per crew-day, route deviation minutes, first-visit completion.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, Service Cloud

2. Meter-to-Cash & Billing Exception Handling

A €13B multi-utility runs meter-to-cash across gas, electric, water, and district-heating meters — with the classic reading → billing → collection loop that ARERA scrutinizes and that generates a long tail of billing-exception cases in Service Cloud. With SAP confirmed as the ERP core (two active SAP-replacement opps), AFPI mines the meter-read-to-cash event log across SAP and Salesforce to expose stuck bills, estimated-reading loops, and manual re-billing rework. That evidence funds the Agentforce 4Serv billing-exception agent already sized at $803K in pipeline. Metric moved: unbilled days, exception rate, DSO.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, Salesforce Service Cloud

3. Grid Connection & New-Service Activation (Inrete Distribuzione Energia)

Inrete Distribuzione Energia handles new gas and electricity connection requests across Emilia-Romagna and Veneto — a regulated, hand-off-heavy process (survey → design → works order → commissioning → activation) where ARERA measures elapsed-time compliance and applies penalties for slippage. AFPI mines the connection-request event log across Salesforce and the works-management system to expose which stage is eating the SLA, where surveys are being redone, and where the field crew is waiting on design. Delivered as an integrated Field Service play, this de-risks the FY28 platform renewal already in pipeline. Metric moved: elapsed connection time, ARERA SLA compliance, re-visit rate.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ARERA Quality-of-Service & SLA Reporting

ARERA (the Italian utilities regulator) sets binding quality-of-service standards on call-response time, complaint resolution, connection SLAs, and billing accuracy — and levies penalties for slippage. Compliance Center codifies those standards as continuous, event-driven controls on top of Service Cloud and Field Service, replacing the current end-of-period spreadsheet attestation with always-on evidence. Especially critical as Agentforce lands in call center and Servizi Ambientali. Metric moved: ARERA SLA compliance %, penalty exposure, mean-time-to-evidence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Field Service
