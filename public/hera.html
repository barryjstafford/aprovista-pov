Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Environmental Services (Servizi Ambientali) Field Dispatch & Route Optimization

Herambiente and Hera Servizi Ambientali run one of Italy's largest waste-collection and environmental-services operations, with an active $581K flotte replacement project modernizing the fleet-dispatch stack on Salesforce Field Service. AFPI mines the dispatch-to-completion event log — work-order creation, crew assignment, route deviation, on-site duration, exception codes — to expose where routes are being re-planned, where crews idle, and where the same address gets re-serviced. That evidence turns the flotte project from a like-for-like replacement into a measurable route- and crew-productivity story. Metric moved: jobs per crew-day, route deviation minutes, first-visit completion.

3. Meter-to-Cash & Billing Exception Handling

A €15B Italian multi-utility runs meter-to-cash across gas, electric, water, and district-heating meters — with the classic reading → billing → collection loop that ARERA scrutinizes and that generates a long tail of billing-exception cases in Service Cloud. AFPI mines the meter-read-to-cash event log across the ERP (suspected SAP) and Salesforce to expose stuck bills, estimated-reading loops, and manual re-billing rework. That evidence directly funds an Agentforce Operations agent for billing-exception triage — the natural next Salesforce buy on top of the current renewal. Metric moved: unbilled days, exception rate, DSO.

4. Grid Connection & New-Service Activation (Inrete Distribuzione Energia)

Inrete Distribuzione Energia handles new gas and electricity connection requests across Emilia-Romagna and Veneto — a regulated, hand-off-heavy process (survey → design → works order → commissioning → activation) where ARERA measures elapsed-time compliance.
