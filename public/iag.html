Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Contact-Centre First-Contact Resolution & Verint→Agentforce Transition

IAG's Retail Insurance AU division is scoping a ~$5M ACV displacement of Verint Kana across 3,000+ users, with a Professional Services-led pilot positioning the shift from a telephony-centric CC to a customer-centric agentic model. Apromore mines call routing, hold/transfer/repeat-contact patterns, and case handling in Service Cloud to expose exactly which journeys are candidates for autonomous agents vs. augmented humans — grounding the Agentforce business case in evidence, not hunch. It moves first-contact resolution, average handle time, and repeat-contact rate — the same KPIs the RFI will be judged on.

Pairs directly with Agentforce Operations for autonomous triage of straight-through claims. Moves cycle time, straight-through processing rate, and customer NPS during CAT events.

5. Regulatory & AFCA Complaints Handling

Australian general insurers face heightened APRA prudential scrutiny and AFCA (Australian Financial Complaints Authority) timeliness reporting, with complaint mishandling driving both fines and remediation programs. Apromore mines the complaint lifecycle end-to-end — intake channel, triage, internal dispute resolution, AFCA escalation, remediation payment — surfacing which categories of complaint routinely miss the 30/45-day thresholds and where the delays actually sit. The output feeds automated case orchestration in Service Cloud and provides an auditable process-map artefact regulators accept. Moves complaint-resolution SLA compliance, remediation cost, and AFCA determination win-rate.
