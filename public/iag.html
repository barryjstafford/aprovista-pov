Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. AFCA Complaints Handling & 30/45-Day SLAs

Australian general insurers face heightened AFCA (Australian Financial Complaints Authority) timeliness reporting, where complaint mishandling drives fines and remediation programs. Compliance Center codifies the complaint lifecycle end-to-end — intake, triage, internal dispute resolution, AFCA escalation, remediation payment — with segregation of duties and continuous evidence. Feeds automated case orchestration in Service Cloud and produces an auditable artefact regulators accept. Metric: complaint-resolution SLA compliance and AFCA determination win-rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, complaints/case system

Key Contact: James Toulson (Salesforce AE, IAG)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. APRA CPS 230 Operational Risk & Third-Party Controls

APRA CPS 230 requires regulated insurers to identify critical operations, set tolerance levels, and maintain evidenced controls over third-party service providers — a heavy lift for a group with NRMA, CGU, NZI, AMI, and WFI running on shared platforms. Compliance Center runs the CPS 230 control library as a live, monitored workflow with escalation and evidence pipeline. Metric: control coverage of critical operations and mean-time-to-evidence for APRA reviews.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Vendor management, GRC platform

Key Contact: James Toulson (Salesforce AE, IAG)

As Agentforce lands in claims and contact-centre workflows, APRA and the Financial Accountability Regime (FAR) expect every autonomous decision to be traceable, tested, and controllable.
