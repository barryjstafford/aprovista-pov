Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. OSFI E-13 Regulatory Compliance Management

As a Canadian D-SIB, NBC operates under OSFI Guideline E-13 (Regulatory Compliance Management), requiring documented control effectiveness across every material regulatory obligation. Compliance Center codifies each control as a monitored workflow — ownership, evidence, exceptions, and attestations — so control health can be shown at any moment rather than reconstructed during an OSFI review. Metric moved: audit-ready controls covered and mean-time-to-evidence per obligation.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: GRC platform, Financial Services Cloud

Key Contact: Bill Bonney (Chief Compliance Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. FINTRAC AML Alert Adjudication & SAR Filing

FINTRAC obligations require adjudicated evidence on every AML alert, and NBC's post-CWB footprint now spans two AML platforms and multiple case-management stacks. Compliance Center gives a single controlled workflow for alert triage, escalation, adverse-media review, and SAR filing with codified SLAs and segregation-of-duties. Cuts false-positive rework and produces regulator-ready evidence on demand. Metric moved: alert-clearing SLA hit-rate and time-to-SAR.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: AML monitoring platform, case management

Key Contact: Bill Bonney (Chief Compliance Officer)

The FCAC Financial Consumer Protection Framework holds federally regulated banks to strict complaint-handling timelines and evidence requirements. Compliance Center runs the intake → investigation → escalation → response process as a controlled workflow — visible SLAs, aging, and auditor-ready evidence per case — and integrates with the Agentic Omni Gateway service redesign.
