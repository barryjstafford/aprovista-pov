Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Order-to-Cash Across 330+ Distribution Centers

Sysco pushes tens of millions of case-lines per week through 330+ distribution facilities on SAP + Salesforce, and cut-off misses, short-ships, and credit rework are the biggest drag on service level and DSO. AFPI mines the SAP OTC and Sales/Service Cloud event log to expose where orders stall between capture, credit hold, allocation, pick, and invoice — and where reps override policy. Metric: order-to-cash cycle time and short-ship rate (each 1% of short-ship on ~$81B of GMV is roughly $800M at risk).

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, Salesforce Sales Cloud

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. FSMA 204 Food Traceability — Lot-Level Chain of Custody

FDA FSMA Section 204 mandates end-to-end lot-level traceability for high-risk foods, with 24-hour recall response and Critical Tracking Event evidence. Compliance Center runs receipt, transformation, shipping, and recall workflows as controlled processes with codified evidence across the WMS, TMS, and SAP receipt logs. Metric: recall response time and % of shipments with complete Key Data Element capture.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: WMS, SAP
