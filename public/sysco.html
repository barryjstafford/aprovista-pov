Topline POV: Apromore can help Sysco Corporation by mining its Order-to-Cash and Source-to-Pay event logs across 330+ distribution centers and 500K+ SKUs — cutting short-ship rate and supplier-onboarding cycle time while giving the in-flight Agentforce for MFG and AF4Service rollouts a documented target-state process before they lock in.

Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Order-to-Cash for the 90-truck / day distribution centers

Sysco processes tens of millions of case-lines per week across 330+ distribution facilities; cut-off misses, short-ships, and credit rework are the single biggest drag on service level and DSO. AFPI mines the SAP + Salesforce Rev Cloud + WMS event log to expose where orders stall between capture, allocation, pick, and invoice — and where reps override credit holds. It moves order-to-cash cycle time and short-ship rate (each 1% of short-ship is roughly $600M of at-risk GMV).

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, Salesforce Rev Cloud

Key Contact: Ron Phillips (EVP & Chief Supply Chain Officer)

2. Contact center deflection tied to the AI Service + Voice build

Two active opps ($1.25M AF4Service/CC + $418K Voice) target 916 agents handling delivery, credit, and product-substitution calls. AFPI mines case + IVR + delivery-exception event logs to find the top rework loops, so Agentforce agents are pointed at the calls that actually deflect — not the ones with the highest volume but lowest containment. Metric: cost-per-contact and first-contact resolution on the top 20 intent clusters.

3. Source-to-Pay and supplier onboarding — the Informatica Product 360 wedge

The active INFA Product 360 opp ($750K) signals a live MDM gap on 500K+ SKUs from 5,000+ suppliers. AFPI mines the P2P event log (PO to invoice to payment) plus supplier-master maintenance events to expose duplicate SKUs, price-file exceptions, and the manual PIM handoffs that break the Product 360 ROI case. It moves supplier-onboarding cycle time (weeks → days) and invoice-match rate.
