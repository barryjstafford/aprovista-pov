Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Order-to-Cash for the 90-truck / day distribution centers. Sysco processes tens of millions of case-lines per week across 330+ distribution facilities; cut-off misses, short-ships, and credit rework are the single biggest drag on service level and DSO. AFPI mines the SAP + Salesforce Rev Cloud + WMS event log to expose where orders stall between capture, allocation, pick, and invoice — and where reps override credit holds. It moves order-to-cash cycle time and short-ship rate (each 1% of short-ship is roughly $600M of at-risk GMV). Engage Ron Phillips, EVP & Chief Supply Chain Officer.

2. Contact center deflection tied to the AI Service + Voice build. Two active opps ($1.25M AF4Service/CC + $418K Voice) target 916 agents handling delivery, credit, and product-substitution calls. AFPI mines case + IVR + delivery-exception event logs to find the top rework loops, so Agentforce agents are pointed at the calls that actually deflect — not the ones with the highest volume but lowest containment. Metric: cost-per-contact and first-contact resolution on the top 20 intent clusters. Engage Marie Robinson, EVP & Chief Supply Chain Officer / customer care sponsor.

3. Source-to-Pay and supplier onboarding — the Informatica Product 360 wedge. The active INFA Product 360 opp ($750K) signals a live MDM gap on 500K+ SKUs from 5,000+ suppliers. AFPI mines the P2P event log (PO to invoice to payment) plus supplier-master maintenance events to expose duplicate SKUs, price-file exceptions, and the manual PIM handoffs that break the Product 360 ROI case. It moves supplier-onboarding cycle time (weeks → days) and invoice-match rate. Engage Victoria Gutierrez, EVP & Chief Merchandising Officer.

4. Quote-to-cash for the Rev Cloud + CLM expansion. Two Rev Cloud opps ($1M upgrade + $450K CLM expansion) sit at early stage; Sysco's national-account and local-market pricing has hundreds of contract templates and side-letter carve-outs. AFPI mines the CPQ + CLM event log to show cycle time from quote request to signed contract by segment, and where deal-desk approvals loop. It moves quote cycle time and margin leakage on non-standard pricing. Engage Greg Bertrand, EVP US Foodservice Operations.

5. Manufacturing / plant-ops execution — the Agentforce for MFG 8500 opp. The $1.07M Agentforce for Manufacturing opp is at Stage 07 Pending — a real budget, real user count, and a soft business case. AFPI mines MES + maintenance + quality event logs across Sysco's own-brand plants (Buckhead Meats, FreshPoint, European Imports) to prove throughput uplift, unplanned-downtime reduction, and OTIF gain before Agentforce agents go live. Metric: OEE and unplanned-downtime hours per line. Engage Tom Peck, EVP & Chief Information & Digital Officer.
