Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Post-Acquisition Delivery Harmonization

CyberCX has rolled up 24 Australian and NZ cybersecurity firms since 2019 (Phriendly Phishing, Decipher Works, CQR, CloudTen, Basis Networks and more), each with its own PSA, ticketing, and delivery methodology. The same "penetration test" or "IR retainer" is scoped, delivered, and invoiced differently across the acquired brands — hidden variance eats margin and blocks the Asia expansion plan. Apromore mines the Sales Cloud → delivery-system event stream to reveal the true happy path versus brand-specific detours, giving the executive team an evidence base to standardize service catalogs and gross margin. Metric moved: engagement cycle time and gross-margin variance across acquired brands.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Sales Cloud, PSA / delivery ticketing

Key Contact: John Paitaridis (Group CEO)

2. Quote-to-Cash & Statement-of-Work Cycle

Custom-scoped consulting engagements move through opportunity → SOW drafting → legal review → PO → resource allocation → invoicing, with multiple approval loops and client back-and-forth. Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

CyberCX runs the largest Australian-owned SOC, and every alert cycles a tier-1 analyst through SIEM, SOAR, threat-intel portals, ticketing, and email while the SLA clock runs. Task mining the SOC analyst desktop captures how much time is lost to app-switching, copy/paste enrichment, and repeated lookups that Agentforce for Service could automate — and directly repositions the "lackluster" Agentforce deployment as a task-mining-led business case. Metric moved: mean-time-to-triage per alert and analyst-touch minutes per incident.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SIEM / SOAR consoles, ITSM ticketing

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. IRAP / ISM / Essential Eight Evidence Automation

CyberCX both assesses clients against and operates its own controls under the ACSC Information Security Manual, Essential Eight, and IRAP. Compliance evidence today is largely manual — screenshots, ticket exports, sampled logs — and re-collected for every re-assessment cycle. Compliance Center continuously conforms live event logs against declared control models, produces audit-ready evidence packs, and flags drift the moment it happens. Metric moved: audit-prep hours per IRAP re-assessment and control-drift detection lag.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: ITSM / GRC platform, Salesforce Service Cloud

Key Contact: Katherine Mansted (Executive Director, Cyber Intelligence)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. ISO 27001 & SOC 2 Continuous Controls

ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II require continuous evidence that controls operated effectively across the audit window, not a point-in-time snapshot. Compliance Center runs each control as a monitored workflow with codified thresholds, sign-offs, and always-on evidence — the exact shape needed as CyberCX layers Agentforce and Data Cloud into client-touching services. Metric moved: control exceptions closed within SLA and time-to-evidence per audit request.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: GRC platform, Salesforce Shield / Event Monitoring

Key Contact: Alastair MacGibbon (Chief Strategy Officer)
