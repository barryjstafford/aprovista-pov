Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Partworks Issue Planning & Print Forecast Accuracy

De Agostini’s core collectibles business ships weekly issues of long-tail series (models, cookbooks, kids’ encyclopedias) into edicola and direct-subscription channels across Italy, France, UK and Iberia. Print-run over-forecasts inflate pulp write-offs on the tail; under-forecasts kill sell-through on the head. AFPI mines the plan-to-print-to-distribute event log to expose where forecasting overrides, contributor delays, and edicola allocation rules diverge from the target flow, and quantifies actual variance-to-plan by series and market. Metric moved: pulp write-off percentage and sell-through rate per issue.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Editorial Planning & Print Management (TBD), Salesforce Sales Cloud

Key Contact: Stefano Bordignoni (Direttore Commerciale)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Subscription Order-to-Fulfilment & Churn Recovery

Direct home-delivery subscriptions are the highest-margin channel but carry a heavy operational tail: missed deliveries, address changes mid-series, payment failures and skipped-issue disputes each trigger service events that today flow through Service Cloud plus a fulfilment 3PL. AFPI reconstructs the true subscriber journey against the target renewal path, isolates the exception branches driving involuntary churn, and hands two or three variants to Agentforce for automated recovery. Metric moved: subscription completion rate and involuntary churn on active series.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Fulfilment / 3PL WMS (TBD)

Key Contact: Tiziana Scafetta (IT CRM, Sale & Distribution Manager)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Edicola Returns & Reverse-Logistics Reconciliation

Unsold newsstand copies flow back through a distributor-managed reverse-logistics chain and must be counted, credited and destroyed under Italian consumer law. Reconciliation lags and credit-note disputes with distributors are a chronic drag on cash and on the finance close. AFPI mines the returns event log (dispatch, receipt, count, credit note, destruction) to expose distributor-specific bottlenecks and multi-hand-off loops, and gives finance and commercial a shared conformance view. Metric moved: returns-cycle days and credit-note dispute rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Distributor Returns Portal (TBD), Finance / Credit-Note ledger (TBD)

Key Contact: Rudi Ghirelli (IT Demand / Supply Chain Manager)

Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. Subscriber Service Agent Desktop Swivel-Chair

Editore’s subscriber-care agents jump between Service Cloud, the fulfilment 3PL portal, a payments UI and email to close a single subscriber ticket — delivery escalation, missed-issue re-ship, address change mid-series. Task mining captures each agent’s on-screen actions to isolate the after-call-work steps and tabs eating handle time, and pinpoints the exact clicks Agentforce can replace. Feeds a direct Agentforce for Service business case tied to Service Cloud. Metric moved: average handle time and after-call-work minutes per ticket.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud console, Fulfilment 3PL portal

Key Contact: Tiziana Scafetta (IT CRM, Sale & Distribution Manager)

2. Editorial Planning Coordinator Desktop

Series planners coordinate weekly issue release across an editorial planning tool, spreadsheets, and email to contributors, printers and distributors — then re-key the same commitments into Sales Cloud and the print-run system. Task mining records what a planner actually does end-to-end, showing where copy-paste and email chases replace real integration. This becomes both a straight automation case and a data feed into the Process Mining partworks analysis. Metric moved: planner touch-time per issue and re-work hours per release window.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Editorial Planning tool (TBD), Salesforce Sales Cloud

Paper, ink, print and collectible-component invoices arrive from dozens of suppliers on tight editorial calendars. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. GDPR Data-Subject-Access-Request Handling

Editore’s direct-subscription business holds subscriber personal data across Service Cloud, fulfilment, marketing and legacy CRM — GDPR obliges Editore to answer access, portability and erasure requests within one month with full audit evidence. Compliance Center runs the DSAR workflow as a controlled process with SLAs and codified sign-offs, and produces continuous evidence for the DPO and the Garante if asked. Metric moved: DSAR cycle time and audit-ready response rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Marketing / Subscriber data store

Key Contact: Davide Ardizio (Chief Information Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Italian Consumer / Subscription-Law Contract Compliance

Italian consumer law (Codice del Consumo) and the EU consumer-rights directive dictate how subscription confirmations, right-of-withdrawal, price changes and cancellation flows must be presented and evidenced. Compliance Center codifies those flows on Service Cloud and the fulfilment stack as controlled processes with sign-offs and version history, so every subscriber interaction can be replayed for a regulator or class-action defence. Metric moved: consumer-law exception rate and mean-time-to-evidence a subscriber interaction.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Subscription billing platform (TBD)

Key Contact: Davide Ardizio (Chief Information Officer)

3. Editorial Rights, Royalties & Contributor Payments Attestation

Every partworks issue and DeA Scuola title carries a stack of author, illustrator and photo-agency rights that must be tracked, exercised inside contractual windows, and royalty-settled on time — a chronic finance-close risk when reconciled by spreadsheet. Compliance Center wraps the rights & royalties workflow as a controlled process with segregation-of-duties sign-offs and audit-ready evidence per title. Metric moved: royalty-settlement cycle time and out-of-scope rights incidents per year.
