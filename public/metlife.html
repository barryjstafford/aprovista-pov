Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Group Benefits Claims Adjudication (GB360)

MetLife's Group Benefits segment (dental, disability, absence management, voluntary) is being rebuilt on GB360 — the active $4.5M GB360 EXTENSION opp is the wedge. AFPI mines the claims event log — intake → triage → medical review → adjudication → payment — to expose where cases stall (missing docs, provider callbacks, adjuster rework) versus where they can be routed straight to an Agentforce claims agent. Two-week pilot: one Ops analyst finds the top three broken variants and hands them to Agentforce Operations. Metric moved: claims cycle time and % straight-through processed.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Insurance Claims Management (Salesforce), Vlocity Insurance

Key Contact: Ramy Tadros (President, U.S. Business)

2. Life & Annuities Underwriting Cycle Time

Individual life and annuity underwriting is a multi-desk process (application → medical evidence → APS retrieval → risk classification → policy issuance) where every day of delay puts placement at risk to a competitor. AFPI overlays the actual underwriting event log to separate "waiting on external evidence" from "waiting on internal rework" — a distinction MetLife's own underwriters can rarely quantify today. Lands directly against the active $565K MS-MetLife CTO engagement as a Discovery-phase Signature Success deliverable. Metric moved: app-to-issue cycle time and NIGO (not-in-good-order) rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Insurance Policy Administration (Salesforce), Vlocity Insurance
