Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Consumer Onboarding & KYC/AML Cycle Time

Chase Consumer & Community Banking onboards millions of retail and small-business customers a year through a fragmented KYC/AML workflow that spans branch, digital, and back-office review queues. AFPI mines the actual account-opening event log to expose rework loops, manual escalations, and stalled cases that quietly push regulatory SLAs. The play plugs directly into the existing Agentforce Flex Credit footprint so Chase pays for outcomes, not implementation; the metric it moves is median onboarding cycle time and first-touch-resolution rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Agentforce

2. Commercial & Investment Bank Deal Lifecycle

The end-to-end path from pitch → mandate → syndication → booking spans dozens of internal systems and every desk runs its own variant. AFPI reconstructs the real process from CRM, deal-management, and settlement events to show where mandates leak, where legal review re-queues, and where booking breaks. Value framing against TPL: the process-mining tier reveals the top three revenue-leak paths inside a quarter, well under a single lost mandate. Metric: mandate-to-book cycle time and revenue leakage per stage.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud / CRM, Deal-management platform (TBD)

3. Service Case Deflection for the $100M+ Service Cloud Estate

JPMC is a Service Cloud RED account with active escalations and sensitive routing. AFPI mines the case-handling event log — swivel-chair between agents, hold-and-resume, escalation ping-pong — and hands the highest-frequency broken flows straight to Agentforce for automation. This is the fastest Compliance-Center wedge: the same process log surfaces reg-mandated complaint SLAs. Metric: cost-to-serve per case and % cases fully agent-resolved.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Agentforce

4. Wholesale Payments & Wire Exception Handling

JPM Payments processes ~$10T/day; every wire that lands in exception review is a manual cost and a customer-experience risk. AFPI mines the exception queue to distinguish repeatable failure patterns (data quality, sanctions hit, cutoff timing) from true one-offs, then routes the repeatable ones to Agentforce Operations. This is the clearest 2-4 week time-to-value story vs. Celonis — no PQL specialist, one no-code analyst inside Payments Ops can pilot. Metric: exception-clear rate per FTE and STP percentage.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Payments platform (TBD), Agentforce
