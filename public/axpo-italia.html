Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Bit2win CPQ Replacement — Baseline Quote-to-Contract Before You Rebuild

Axpo Italia is actively replacing Bit2win CPQ ($270k opp in stage 02) — the quote-to-contract flow for Flexy Mix, Smart Portfolio, and Corporate PPA products spans MuleSoft, Bit2win, and Sales Cloud, with heavy pricing-desk touchpoints for structured energy products. Rebuilding on a new CPQ without first mining the as-is flow risks re-implementing the same rework loops (pricing exceptions, contract redlines, credit checks). AFPI mines the current event log to expose which product variants generate the most cycle-time drag and hand-offs so the new CPQ is designed against real friction. Metric moved: quote-to-signed cycle time, pricing-exception rate, deal-desk touches per quote.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Bit2win CPQ, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Emanuele Mammoliti (Head of ICT)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Pulsee Customer Onboarding & Switching (Fornitura) — Digital-Native Retail

Pulsee is Axpo Italia's digital-only residential retail brand, replatformed on Heroku + Data Cloud + MuleSoft for scale. Every new-customer sign-up triggers a regulated fornitura-switching flow across the distributor (E-Distribuzione / SNAM), SAP billing, and Marketing Cloud — a single stuck switch drops the customer into a black hole and shows up as a Service Cloud case. AFPI mines the end-to-end onboarding event log to quantify switch-completion rate, drop-off variants, and case-generating patterns, feeding the Agentforce 1 upgrade with the real conformance map. Metric moved: switch completion rate, days-to-first-invoice, abandoned-onboarding rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Heroku, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Salvatore Pinto (CEO Business Unit Consumer / Pulsee)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. MuleSoft-Mediated Integration Flows — Defending the $601k Renewal With a Shared Baseline

Axpo has an in-flight $601k MuleSoft renewal (2027 close) plus a live MuleSoft-Flex-Gateway-on-Heroku architecture referenced publicly at the May-2024 Milan Meetup (Integration Architects Schenone & Scaiano). Every critical flow — billing, CRM sync, Pulsee onboarding, Smartbox telemetry — passes through MuleSoft, meaning MuleSoft holds the richest event stream in the estate. AFPI mines those API/orchestration logs to X-ray which flows are load-bearing, which are stale, and where duplicated flows can be retired — hardening the business case for the renewal and expansion. Metric moved: retired-flow count, mean flow latency, integration-related incident volume.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft, Heroku

Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. Service Agent Desktop — De-Risking the Agentforce 1 Upgrade

The $234k Agentforce 1 upgrade lands Autonomous Agents on top of an Einstein 1 Service Cloud that already serves both Axpo B2B (energy portfolios, PPA contract changes) and Pulsee B2C (billing disputes, switching support). Task mining captures each service agent's on-screen actions — which tabs they flip through, which SAP screens they re-key from, where they wait — producing the "what should the agent take" whitelist that turns the upgrade from a feature buy into a measured deflection program. Metric moved: agent-deflectable case %, average handle time, after-call-work minutes per contact.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud console, SAP

Key Contact: Salvatore Pinto (CEO Business Unit Consumer / Pulsee)

The "Axpo - Field Service for Smartbox" opp ($90k, stage 02) rolls Field Service out for Axpo's Smartbox smart-metering and energy-management devices installed at customer premises. 3. Deal Desk / Pricing Analyst Task Mining — Structured-Energy Quote Turnaround

Structured energy quotes (Flexy Mix, Corporate PPA) run through a small pricing desk that swivels between Excel pricing models, Bit2win, SAP tariff tables, and email approvals — the Bit2win replacement will not fix desk-side friction unless that friction is visible. Task mining captures the desk's screen-level activity to reveal how much time is spent re-keying vs. actually pricing, and where the same exception is being re-solved case-by-case. Feeds Agentforce Operations candidates and the target-state design for the new CPQ. Metric moved: quote turnaround time, pricing-analyst touch-time per quote, repeat-exception rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Bit2win CPQ, Excel

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ARERA-Regulated Switching & Billing Controls

ARERA rules mandate strict timelines and consumer protections around retail switching, dual-invoicing prevention, and complaint handling — each violation is fineable and materially impacts Pulsee's digital-native brand promise. Compliance Center codifies the switching, complaint-response, and billing-correction workflows as controlled processes with segregation of duties, evidence capture, and SLA monitoring. Metric moved: ARERA SLA hit-rate, dual-invoicing incidents, complaint-response cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, Service Cloud
