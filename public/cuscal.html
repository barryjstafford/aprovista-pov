Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Payments Exception & Dispute Resolution

Cuscal processes card, NPP, direct-entry, and BPAY volume for 100+ ADIs and fintechs, and every failed authorization, chargeback, or NPP mis-direct becomes a dispute case that gets manually triaged. Apromore mines the end-to-end exception flow across the switch and case-management system to expose rework loops, SLA breaches against scheme networks, and the fallout categories driving the biggest FTE cost. This directly de-risks the operational-resilience story Cuscal owes both regulators and its ADI clients. Metric moved: dispute cycle time and cost-per-case.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Payments switch, case management

2. Client (ADI & Fintech) Onboarding & KYC/AML

Onboarding a new ADI, fintech, or corporate biller into the Cuscal switch is a multi-week workflow spanning compliance, technical integration, and scheme certification. Apromore mines each case to expose where files stall in KYC/AML review, how often re-work is triggered by incomplete evidence, and which segments (crypto exchanges under AUSTRAC pressure, non-bank lenders) drive the longest tails. 3. Card & Scheme Fee Reconciliation

Monthly interchange, scheme-fee, and client-billing reconciliation across Visa, Mastercard, eftpos, and NPP is a grind — mismatches trigger investigations that eat analyst time and, unresolved, become revenue leakage.
