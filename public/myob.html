Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Subscription Trial-to-Paid Conversion & Onboarding

MYOB runs a product-led-growth funnel across MYOB Business, AccountRight, and MYOB Advanced trials into paid subscriptions. Every dropped step is direct ARR leakage, and marketing spend on trial capture is not paying back until users cross the activation threshold. Apromore mines the actual trial event log from Data Cloud + product telemetry, reveals where prospects stall (billing setup, bank feed connect, first invoice), and hands MCA + Agentforce a ranked list of interventions with expected lift. Metric moved: paid-conversion rate on trials, and time-to-first-value in days.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Data Cloud, MYOB product telemetry

2. Support-to-Cash (Case → Retention / Save)

The open $1.4M renewal covers 576 Full CRM + 325 Call Center + 153 Platform seats — the Service Cloud footprint is MYOB's core member-support engine, and case backlogs directly drive downgrade and churn decisions at renewal. Apromore mines the case-to-resolution-to-renewal pathway across Service Cloud, reveals which case patterns (billing disputes, feature gaps, migration friction) statistically precede churn, and feeds the save motion an Agentforce-ready playbook. 3. Quote-to-Cash for the Enterprise / Mid-Market Motion

MYOB Enterprise Solutions and MYOB Advanced target mid-market and larger SMEs — a much slower, quote-heavy motion than the self-serve SMB base, and one where SPM (open opp) is being introduced to align seller incentives. Apromore mines the quote-approve-invoice-collect flow across Sales Cloud + billing, exposes the rework loops (discount approvals, bespoke terms, credit checks) that stretch DSO, and gives the CFO a defensible number to take to the KKR board. Metric moved: DSO, quote-cycle-time, and win-rate on approved-discount deals.
