Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Subscription Renewal & Retention (Support-to-Save)

The open $1.4M renewal covers 576 Full CRM + 325 Call Center + 153 Platform seats — Service Cloud is MYOB's core member-support engine and case backlogs directly drive downgrade and churn at renewal. Apromore mines the case-to-resolution-to-renewal pathway across Service Cloud and Sales Cloud, exposes which case patterns (billing disputes, feature gaps, migration friction) statistically precede churn, and feeds the save motion an Agentforce-ready playbook. Metric: net revenue retention and CSAT-weighted renewal rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Salesforce Sales Cloud

Key Contact: Serena Thomas (Head of Operations — Enterprise)

2. Trial-to-Paid Conversion & Onboarding

MYOB runs a product-led-growth funnel across MYOB Business, AccountRight, and MYOB Advanced trials into paid subscriptions. Every dropped step is direct ARR leakage, and marketing spend on trial capture does not pay back until users cross the activation threshold. Apromore mines the trial event log from Data Cloud + product telemetry, reveals where prospects stall (billing setup, bank feed connect, first invoice), and hands MCA + Agentforce a ranked list of interventions with expected lift. Metric: paid-conversion rate and time-to-first-value in days.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Data Cloud, MYOB product telemetry

Key Contact: Alana Depetro (Product Director)

3. Enterprise / Mid-Market Quote-to-Cash

MYOB Enterprise Solutions and MYOB Advanced target mid-market and larger SMEs — a much slower, quote-heavy motion than the self-serve SMB base, and one where SPM is being introduced to align seller incentives. Apromore mines the quote-approve-invoice-collect flow across Sales Cloud and billing, exposes the rework loops (discount approvals, bespoke terms, credit checks) that stretch DSO, and gives the CFO a defensible number to take to the KKR board. Metric: DSO, quote-cycle-time, and win-rate on approved-discount deals.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Sales Cloud, MYOB Advanced (billing/AR)

1. Call Center Agent Handle-Time & Wrap

MYOB's 325-seat Service Cloud call center handles high-volume member support with measurable AHT and wrap variability. Task mining captures each agent's on-screen actions to isolate the after-call-work steps and the tabs that eat wrap time, then pairs directly with the open $250k AF Contact Centre / SCV opp. Feeds Agentforce a ranked backlog of steps to automate instead of guessing at agent surveys. Metric: AHT and after-call-work minutes per contact type.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases
