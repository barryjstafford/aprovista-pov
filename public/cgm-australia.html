Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. GP Practice Onboarding & Migration to Helix Cloud

CGM AU is actively migrating thousands of general practices off on-premise MedicalDirector Clinical/PracSoft to the cloud-native Helix platform. Every migration is a multi-week choreography of data extraction, patient-record reconciliation, PBS/Medicare re-registration, and clinician training — heavy on manual hand-offs. Apromore can mine the onboarding funnel end-to-end to expose which steps stall practices, which sales-region cohorts convert fastest, and where support engineers become the bottleneck. Metric: median days-to-live per practice and % of practices that abandon mid-migration.
