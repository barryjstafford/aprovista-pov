Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. GP Practice Onboarding & Migration to Helix Cloud

CGM AU is actively migrating thousands of general practices off on-premise MedicalDirector Clinical/PracSoft to the cloud-native Helix platform. Every migration is a multi-week choreography of data extraction, patient-record reconciliation, PBS/Medicare re-registration, and clinician training — heavy on manual hand-offs. Apromore mines the onboarding funnel end-to-end to expose which steps stall practices, which sales-region cohorts convert fastest, and where support engineers become the bottleneck. Metric: median days-to-live per practice and % of practices that abandon mid-migration.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, Helix onboarding platform

Key Contact: Chief Operating Officer (COO, CGM Australia)

2. Customer Support Ticket Resolution & Reopen Rate

MedicalDirector supports ~23,000+ GP users and thousands of specialists — tier-1/2/3 ticket volume around scripting, PBS updates, and clinical workflow bugs is high. The active Org Extension opp is precisely to grow Service Cloud in this footprint. Apromore mines the ticket lifecycle to expose which categories reopen most, which agents drive fastest closure, and where triage adds days without value. Metric: first-contact resolution rate and % tickets reopened within 30 days.

3. Post-Carve-Out Order-to-Cash Harmonization

CGM acquired MedicalDirector from Telstra Health in 2022 and inherited a partially-integrated quote-to-cash stack: legacy billing, disparate CRM instances, and manual reconciliation with the CGM SE global ledger in SAP. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ADHA My Health Record & RACGP Conformance Release Controls

Every MedicalDirector / PracSoft release must pass ADHA My Health Record certification, RACGP conformance, and PBS/Medicare rules changes on a fixed calendar — and each certification carries clinical-safety and audit exposure. Compliance Center runs the certification lifecycle as a controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and always-on evidence. Metric: cycle time from ADHA submission to certification and audit-ready evidence coverage per release.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Jira / Azure DevOps, Confluence

Key Contact: VP Regulatory & Compliance (VP Regulatory, CGM AU)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Australian Privacy Principles & Health-Data Access Controls

APP 11 (security of personal information) plus the OAIC Notifiable Data Breach scheme require CGM AU to demonstrate provable controls on who can access patient data inside Helix, PracSoft, and the back-office CRM. Compliance Center codifies role-provisioning, break-glass, and access-review workflows and produces continuous evidence for the OAIC. Metric: access-review completion rate and mean-time-to-evidence for a data-access audit.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Helix identity/IAM, Sales Cloud audit trail

Key Contact: CISO / Head of Privacy (CISO, CGM AU)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Model-Risk / Clinical AI Governance

CGM AU is embedding AI features into Helix (script decision-support, coding assistance, patient summaries). Australian regulators and the RACGP are actively drafting clinical-AI guidance, and each model needs a lifecycle: validation, staged rollout, monitoring, and retirement. Compliance Center runs that lifecycle as a controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties and audit-ready evidence — the same shape Agentforce deployments will need. Metric: models under formal governance and time-to-evidence per audit request.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Model registry / MLOps, Helix AI feature telemetry
