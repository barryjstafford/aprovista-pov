Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Retail Auto Loan & Lease Origination-to-Funding

Applications flow from dealer DMS (DealerTrack / RouteOne) through underwriting, decisioning, funding, and title/lien perfection. TMCC is investing in Agentforce for Loan Origination & Precision Ops precisely because manual touches, rework, and stipulation loops are stretching decision-to-fund times. Mining the end-to-end trace across the dealer portal, underwriting engine, and Financial Services Cloud will expose the exact stips-rework loops and dealer variability driving days-to-fund. Metric moved: median application-to-fund cycle time, first-decision auto-approval rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial Services Cloud, core loan origination platform

2. Call-Center Servicing & Case Resolution (Auto Cloud Migration)

The active $3M “Call Center Auto Cloud Migration and Improvements” opp signals a service-transformation program in flight. 3. Paid-in-Full / Payoff & Title Release

The open “TFS – PIF Case Process Migration” opp shows Toyota is actively re-platforming the payoff and title-release flow — a top CFPB complaint category across captive lenders and a direct NPS driver at loan-end. Mining the trace across servicing, cashiering, title release, and state DMV interfaces exposes the specific states, product types, and case reasons that stall title release beyond regulatory windows. Metric moved: percent of payoffs closed inside 10 business days, DMV rework rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, core loan servicing platform

Key Contact: Chris Ballinger (SVP, Business Innovation & Servicing)

4. Dealer Wholesale & Floorplan Lifecycle

TMCC finances Toyota and Lexus dealer inventory through wholesale floorplan, dealer real-estate, and capital loans. Mining the request-to-fund, curtailment, and payoff cycles across the dealer portal, floorplan servicing, and MuleSoft integrations exposes dealer-level variability, exception rework, and delinquency escalation patterns. Directly feeds risk-tier pricing and dealer scorecards. Metric moved: floorplan curtailment on-time rate, exception cases per dealer per month.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Dealer portal, floorplan servicing platform

Key Contact: Adam Stam (VP, Dealer Services)

5. Collections & Delinquency-to-Recovery

Captive-finance economics live and die by collections efficiency — every day of DPD delay compounds credit loss. Mining the trace from first missed payment through outbound contact strategy, promise-to-pay, deferral, repossession, and recovery reveals which action sequences actually pull accounts back to current versus those that only delay charge-off. Pairs naturally with Agentforce for outbound and Data Cloud for likelihood scoring. Metric moved: cure rate at 30/60/90 DPD, cost per recovered dollar.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Collections platform, Service Cloud
