Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Retail Auto Loan & Lease Origination-to-Funding

Applications flow from dealer DMS (DealerTrack / RouteOne) through underwriting, decisioning, funding, and title/lien perfection at TMCC. Agentforce for Loan Origination & Precision Ops is being scoped precisely because manual touches, stipulation loops, and dealer variability stretch decision-to-fund times. Mining the end-to-end trace across the dealer portal, underwriting engine, and Financial Services Cloud exposes exactly which stips-rework loops and which dealers drive days-to-fund. Metric moved: median application-to-fund cycle time and first-decision auto-approval rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Financial Services Cloud, core loan origination platform

2. Paid-in-Full / Payoff & Title Release

The open “TFS – PIF Case Process Migration” opp shows TMCC is actively re-platforming payoff and title-release — a top CFPB complaint category across captive lenders and a direct NPS driver at loan-end. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

The CFPB has been active on auto-loan servicing — payoff processing, title release, add-on refunds, and complaint handling are hot enforcement themes. Compliance Center runs each of these servicing sub-processes as a controlled workflow with codified SLAs, segregation-of-duties, and continuous audit evidence tied to CFPB expectations. Directly aligned to the PIF Case Process Migration in-flight. Metric moved: SLA adherence on payoffs and complaints, and time-to-audit-evidence per enforcement theme.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, core loan servicing platform

2. SOX & Reg Z/B Origination Controls

TMCC inherits SOX from Toyota Motor parent and carries Reg Z (Truth-in-Lending) and Reg B (ECOA) obligations directly on every loan and lease. Compliance Center codifies the origination controls — disclosure accuracy, adverse-action timing, pricing exception approval — as continuous, evidence-producing workflows rather than periodic sample-testing. Especially important as Agentforce enters decisioning. Metric moved: controls with continuous evidence, adverse-action-timing adherence, and audit findings per cycle.
