Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Supply-Chain Replenishment & Automated-DC Throughput (Ocado / Witron Ramp)

Coles is spending well over A$1B on two Ocado customer-fulfilment centres and Witron ambient DCs. Ramp-up is the highest-risk period — bottlenecks between forecast, replenishment order, DC pick, and store receipt destroy on-shelf availability. Apromore mines SAP S/4HANA replenishment events and Manhattan WMS pick/pack events to expose where the automated flow diverges from design and why fill-rate stalls. Metric moved: on-shelf availability (OSA) and DC labour cost per case.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Food Safety & Product-Recall Controls (FSANZ)

Grocery retailers live under Food Standards Australia New Zealand (FSANZ), state health regulators, and Coles' own private-label QA regime. Product recalls today mix email, spreadsheets, and SAP holds — slow, noisy, and hard to audit. Compliance Center codifies recall notification, store-level withdrawal, supplier root-cause, and customer refund into one controlled workflow with continuous evidence. Metric moved: time-to-full-withdrawal, recall coverage rate, and audit-finding count.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP S/4HANA (materials + holds), Coles QA / recall system
