Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Supply-Chain Replenishment & DC Throughput (Ocado/Witron Ramp)

Coles is spending A$1B+ on two automated customer fulfilment centres (Ocado) plus Witron ambient DCs. Ramp-up is the highest-risk period — bottlenecks between forecast, replenishment order, DC pick, and store receipt destroy availability. Apromore mines SAP replenishment events and Manhattan WMS pick/pack events to expose where the automated flow diverges from the designed process and why fill-rate stalls. Metric moved: on-shelf availability (OSA), DC labour cost per case.
