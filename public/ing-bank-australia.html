Complaints Manager Megan Green's team has a strict KPI to resolve 50% of complaints within two days but lacks deep reporting for root-cause analysis and workforce management tooling [Slack]. This is the "low-hanging fruit" COO Tijn Lodsteijn asked for — measurable within 3-4 weeks and directly attributable to the cost-reduction mandate.

2. Mortgage Origination — Credit Decision Cycle Time

ING enhanced mortgage credit decisioning in 2025 with ML-based scorecards to enable faster, more digitised applications, and Australia was a principal driver of the group's €28.5B residential mortgage growth [Web]. Mining origination end-to-end will expose where broker submissions stall between "radar/neo" backends and Salesforce, quantify manual-touch rework, and validate the ML-scorecard uplift. Directly moves days-to-decision and pull-through rate on the highest-revenue product line.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce (FSC/Platform), core banking backends (radar, neo)
