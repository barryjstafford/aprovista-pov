Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Complaints Management — Root-Cause & 2-Day Resolution KPI

Complaints Manager Megan Green's team has a strict KPI to resolve 50% of complaints within two days but lacks root-cause reporting and workforce-management tooling [Slack]. Apromore reconstructs the full complaint lifecycle from Service Cloud + CC 2.0 telephony logs, exposes variants that blow past 48 hours, and pinpoints the handoffs where SLA breaks. This is the "low-hanging fruit" COO Tijn Lodsteijn asked for — measurable within 3-4 weeks and directly attributable to the 5% cost-reduction mandate. Metric: complaint cycle time and 48-hour SLA hit-rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, CC 2.0 telephony

2. Mortgage Origination — Credit Decision Cycle Time

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. APRA CPS 230 Operational Resilience — Continuous Conformance

APRA CPS 230 mandates end-to-end operational-resilience controls with evidence of critical-process performance, tolerance breaches, and third-party dependencies — and the regime is live for the majors [Docs]. Compliance Center produces on-demand audit trails and conformance dashboards for the four critical processes (payments, lending, deposits, incident response), replacing annual manual attestation with continuous monitoring. Ties directly to the existing Security Center opp already in the pipeline. Metric: critical-process coverage and mean-time-to-evidence for APRA queries.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Platform, Fraud Management System

Key Contact: Berkay Özlüdil (COO)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. AUSTRAC AML / KYC Alert Adjudication

AUSTRAC and cross-border AML reporting require adjudicated evidence on every alert — and Australian banks are under sustained enforcement pressure post-Westpac and post-CBA. Compliance Center runs alert triage, escalation, and SMR filing as a single controlled workflow with codified SLAs and full evidence trails. Integrates with the Fraud Management stack and gives the CRO a defensible position on AUSTRAC readiness. Metric: alert-clearing SLA hit-rate and time-to-SMR.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Fraud Management System, core banking

ASIC RG 271 requires banks to record, classify, and report complaints on a mandatory 30-day SLA with granular breach and remediation evidence.
