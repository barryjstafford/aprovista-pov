Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Middle-Market Direct Lending — Deal Origination to Funding Cycle Time

Ares Capital Corporation and the Ares Credit Group underwrite thousands of middle-market loans annually across BDC, private credit, and SDL vehicles. The loop from term sheet through credit committee, legal docs, KYC/AML, closing, and initial fund draw touches Salesforce, credit-risk models, doc-management, and treasury systems — classic swivel-chair with rework in credit memos and stalled legal reviews. AFPI overlays the deal-pipeline event log to show where a $30M unitranche stalls vs. a $200M syndicated deal. Metric: median days-to-close cut 20–30% and origination-officer capacity freed.
