1. Post-Merger MergeCo Order-to-Cash Harmonization

Alinta is absorbing EnergyAustralia customer contracts ("MergeCo" — three active opps totalling ~$2.6M). Two distinct O2C processes now run in parallel across duplicated CIS, billing, and CRM stacks. Without objective mapping, harmonization decisions are being made from tribal knowledge — driving rework, mis-billing risk, and integration debt. Mining both O2C event logs side-by-side surfaces the variant graph, exposes cheapest-path harmonization, and produces the fact base Alinta needs before its C360 target-state locks in. Metric moved: integration timeline (target 24 → 12 months, per Petrobras proof point) and mis-bill exception rate.

2. Retail Move-In / Move-Out & Customer Onboarding

Move-in/move-out is the highest-volume retail process at Alinta and the single biggest churn and complaint driver in AU energy retail. The Retail Transformation ("Alinta RT Get Started" — $5M) explicitly targets this journey, but pain is being discovered manually in workshops. Mining the actual MuleSoft + Sales/Service Cloud event stream reveals where handoffs stall (identity checks, meter data delays, credit checks), where agents re-work orchestrations, and where the "happy path" masks 15+ real variants. Feeds Agentforce Operations with the exact orchestration gaps to auto-resolve. Metric moved: onboarding cycle time and first-call resolution.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, MuleSoft

3. Meter-to-Cash Billing Exception Handling

Retail energy carries an exception rate an order of magnitude above SaaS — estimated meter reads, market settlement mismatches, and dispute-driven adjustments all create manual rework in Alinta's billing operations. Mining the Oracle EPM + Sales/Service billing exception queues quantifies which exception classes drive the most agent time and financial leakage, and which are prime candidates for Agentforce agent-assist. Marcus Krznaric's Customer Service Operations team and Kathryn Griffiths' Finance team already own the KPI. Metric moved: exception backlog age and cost-to-serve per bill.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Oracle EPM, Salesforce Service Cloud

5. Vulnerable Customer & Hardship Case Handling

AER's Better Bills / hardship framework is a compliance hotspot for every AU energy retailer, and Todd McDonald (Functional Operations Manager, Vulnerable Customers) explicitly owns this process at Alinta. Mining the case + payment-plan event log proves compliance by construction, identifies where customers drop out of the hardship funnel, and highlights the plans most likely to succeed vs. fail.
