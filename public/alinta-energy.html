Mining both O2C event logs side-by-side surfaces the variant graph and produces the objective basis the C360 target-state needs before design lock. Metric moved: integration timeline (target 24 → 12 months) and mis-bill exception rate.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Vulnerable Customer & Hardship Case Handling (AER Better Bills)

The AER Better Bills / hardship framework is the single biggest compliance hotspot for every AU energy retailer. The hardship journey spans intake, assessment, payment plans, and reactivation — with mandatory disclosures at each step. Compliance Center runs the process as a controlled workflow with codified SLAs, sign-offs, and audit-ready evidence for every case. Metric moved: hardship completion rate, regulatory audit exceptions, disconnection-avoidance rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, CIS/billing
