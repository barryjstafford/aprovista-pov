Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Wholesale & D2C Order-to-Cash across 32 Licensed Brands

Safilo runs 32 licensed brands (Boss, Tommy Hilfiger, Jimmy Choo, Levi's, Victoria Beckham) plus house brands through wholly-owned subsidiaries in 40 countries and distribution partners in another 30. Each license carries distinct royalty triggers, minimum guarantees, and channel-exclusivity rules that today produce manual credit blocks, delivery renegotiations, and revenue-recognition rework at every close. Mining the full O2C event log across ERP, B2B Commerce, and Service Cloud exposes the rework hotspots by brand and geography, and hands the CFO office a business-analyst-controlled cockpit. Metric: DSO reduction of 3-5 days and OTIF lift of 4-6 points.
