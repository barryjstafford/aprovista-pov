Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Omnichannel Order-to-Cash Across Eight Brands

Oniverse operates 8 brands (Calzedonia, Intimissimi, Tezenis, Falconeri, Signorvino, Atelier Emé, Intimissimi Uomo, plus Cantiere del Pardo) across e-commerce and 5,732 physical stores. Each brand runs its own O2C variant on shared Commerce Cloud + Order Management, creating hidden rework loops and inconsistent SLAs at scale. AFPI can mine every order event across brands and countries, quantify variance versus the "one Oniverse" target flow, and feed exception routing into Agentforce Operations. Metric moved: order-to-cash cycle days, first-pass yield, and manual-touch rate per order.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Commerce Cloud, Salesforce Order Management

Key Contact: Head of Digital Transformation, Oniverse (Digital / IT leadership)

2. Returns & Reverse-Logistics Optimization

Specialty apparel — hosiery, lingerie, cashmere — carries the highest return rates in retail, and Oniverse absorbs the cost across 55 countries with mixed carrier and store-return paths. Today, exception handling is a black box: refund-delay complaints hit Service Cloud with no upstream root-cause. AFPI can mine the full return event stream from OMS, WMS, and carrier events, isolate the top drivers of refund delay, and give Ops a variant-level view before scaling automation. Metric moved: refund cycle time, return-related contact rate, and shrink from process leakage.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Order Management, Salesforce Service Cloud

Key Contact: Head of Customer Operations, Oniverse (Customer Ops / Service)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Store Replenishment & Allocation

With 5,732 stores across 8 brands and highly seasonal SKUs (swimwear, hosiery, cashmere), replenishment cycle time directly drives full-price sell-through. Today the process spans an ERP (likely SAP), OMS, and store systems — with allocation decisions made outside any mined baseline. AFPI can mine the replenishment event log, expose stockout-to-store-request loops, and quantify the working-capital cost of slow allocations. Metric moved: stockout rate, full-price sell-through, and days-of-cover variance per brand.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Order Management, SAP (or Oracle) ERP

Key Contact: Supply Chain Director, Oniverse (Supply Chain / Logistics)

4. Marketing-to-Cash Campaign Attribution & Rework

Oniverse spends heavily on Marketing Cloud ExactTarget ($2.1M renewal) plus SMS/WhatsApp/adaptive-website add-ons across brands, but campaign-to-order conversion mixes journeys, offers, and store fulfilment paths that no report can fully unpick. AFPI can mine the end-to-end Journey → Commerce → OMS event chain, surface where offers stall or duplicate, and let Marketing operate on a single mined truth instead of vendor dashboards. Metric moved: campaign ROI variance, duplicate-touch rate, and journey completion cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Salesforce Commerce Cloud
