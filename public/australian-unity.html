Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Aged Care & Home Care Package Onboarding

The onboarding journey from initial enquiry through ACAT assessment, Home Care Package assignment, funding activation, and first service delivery involves dozens of hand-offs across intake teams, clinical assessors, and government portals — with statutory clocks under the Aged Care Act 2024. AFPI mines the actual event log across CRM, clinical, and Services Australia touchpoints to expose where the median 45–60 day journey is being blown out by rework and unassigned queues. The metric it moves is time-to-first-service and unallocated-package leakage, both directly tied to funded revenue and regulator attestation.

2. Private Health Insurance Claims Adjudication

Health insurance claims flow through eligibility checks, provider validation, gap-cover rules, and manual clinical review — with APRA and Private Health Insurance Ombudsman scrutiny on turnaround times. Straight-through-processing rates in the mid-70s hide the real story: a long tail of claims cycling through rework loops that consume clinical assessor capacity. AFPI compares the "as-designed" claims process against the true event log to surface the two or three loop patterns that account for most of the manual effort, moving STP rate and claims-cost-per-policy.
