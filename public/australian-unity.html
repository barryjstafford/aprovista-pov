Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Aged Care & Home Care Package Onboarding. The onboarding journey from initial enquiry through ACAT assessment, Home Care Package assignment, funding activation, and first service delivery involves dozens of hand-offs across intake teams, clinical assessors, and government portals — with statutory clocks under the Aged Care Act 2024. AFPI mines the actual event log across CRM, clinical, and Services Australia touchpoints to expose where the median 45–60 day journey is being blown out by rework and unassigned queues. The metric it moves is time-to-first-service and unallocated-package leakage, both directly tied to funded revenue and regulator attestation. Engage the Chief Operating Officer, Home Health as the operational champion.

2. Private Health Insurance Claims Adjudication. Health insurance claims flow through eligibility checks, provider validation, gap-cover rules, and manual clinical review — with APRA and Private Health Insurance Ombudsman scrutiny on turnaround times. Straight-through-processing rates in the mid-70s hide the real story: a long tail of claims cycling through rework loops that consume clinical assessor capacity. AFPI compares the "as-designed" claims process against the true event log to surface the two or three loop patterns that account for most of the manual effort, moving STP rate and claims-cost-per-policy. Engage the COO, Health Insurance.

3. Wealth & Advice Client-Onboarding and KYC Refresh. Post Royal Commission, wealth onboarding at Australian Unity carries a heavy KYC / DDO / Best-Interest-Duty documentation burden across advice, platform, and trustee entities. Every remediation cycle from AUSTRAC or ASIC starts with "show us your process" — and the actual process only lives in operator memory. AFPI produces the regulator-defensible process map with variant analysis and compliance-center controls attached, cutting onboarding cycle time and audit prep hours. Engage the Group Executive, Wealth & Capital Markets.

4. Marketing Cloud Journey Reconciliation Against Service Outcomes. With a A$699k Marketing Cloud renewal in flight (ExactTarget, Platform, plus prior Datorama), the natural co-sell wedge is proving that outbound journeys convert to actual serviced outcomes — enrolments, retained members, funded packages — not just click-throughs. AFPI ingests Marketing Cloud send events alongside downstream CRM and operational events to expose where campaign responders drop out of the servicing funnel. This moves conversion-to-serviced-outcome and gives the CMO renewal-cycle ammunition. Engage the Chief Marketing Officer.

5. Retirement Village Resident Lifecycle & Contract Administration. Australian Unity's retirement villages (a distinct Group Services entity in Org62) run a long-tail contract process — from initial deposit through DMF calculation, exit refunds, and estate settlement — that is state-legislation-specific and litigation-prone. Contract handoffs between village managers, legal, finance, and family typically span 60–120 days per exit, with cash and reputational exposure at each step. AFPI maps the full contract lifecycle event log and surfaces which villages, contract vintages, or state jurisdictions drive the worst exit-cycle outliers, moving DMF release time and settlement disputes. Engage the GM, Retirement Communities.
