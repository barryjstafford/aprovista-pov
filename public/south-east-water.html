Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Billing-to-Cash on HiAffinity + Salesforce

Mine the billing-to-cash flow from meter read through bill generation in HiAffinity into Salesforce Service Cloud dispute and adjustment cases. The pain is well-documented: at the VicWater SIC Peter O'Donoghue and peer CIOs named "billing complexities" as a shared, unsolved industry problem, and the Phase 2 review is explicitly scoped around HiAffinity data flows. AFPI surfaces where adjustments, re-bills, and exceptions loop back to agents, then feeds Agentforce with the rules to auto-resolve high-frequency variants. Target metric: 15-25% reduction in billing-driven service contacts and a measurable cut in days-sales-outstanding.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: HiAffinity, Salesforce Service Cloud

Key Contact: Peter O'Donoghue (CIO)

2. Debt Recovery & Hardship Management

Debt recovery is one of the five named Agentforce priority use cases at SEW and touches the new Marketing Cloud instance for arrears communications. Mining the recovery journey — from first-missed-payment through hardship program enrolment, payment plans, and disconnection avoidance — pinpoints where cases stall, where hardship customers get re-contacted, and where automated Marketing Cloud journeys can safely replace agent touchpoints. The metric that moves is recovery-per-agent-hour and the reduction in avoidable disconnection escalations, both regulator-sensitive numbers under the ESC Customer Service Code.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud

3. Case Management & Omni-Channel Routing Efficiency

Case management is explicitly on the Agentforce priority list and Omni-Channel routing is already deployed across voice, message, and email. Directly supports the business case for turning on the Einstein features already implemented but awaiting governance approval. Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. Contact-Centre Agent Desktop Swivel-Chair

SEW's contact-centre agents work across Service Cloud, HiAffinity billing, and the Amazon Connect softphone to handle a single billing or account enquiry. Task mining the agent desktop captures every screen switch, copy/paste, and lookup between the CRM record and HiAffinity's legacy UI — the exact touchpoints where average handle time leaks and where Agentforce assistive actions should be deployed first. Feeds a defensible business case for the AF Voice / IVR containment expansion already queued. Metric: agent handle time and after-call-work minutes per contact type.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Data-Breach Remediation & Data-Matching Assurance

Phase 2 of the current engagement is a broad review of processes and systems related to historical data breaches, spanning Salesforce, HiAffinity, and ancillary systems for data matching. Compliance Center codifies how customer data is supposed to flow across integrations, then continuously evidences where PII touchpoints deviate from documented policy — the exact evidence base the regulator will ask for on the next visit. AproScore's process-mining approach lets one business analyst produce audit-grade evidence that would otherwise require months of SQL-plus-PQL specialist work. Metric: audit evidence delivered in weeks rather than quarters.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce, HiAffinity

2. ESC Customer Service Code & Hardship Compliance

The Essential Services Commission's Customer Service Code sets prescriptive obligations on Victorian water retailers for hardship handling, disconnection avoidance, and complaint resolution timelines — every one of which SEW must evidence to the regulator. Compliance Center runs the hardship-to-disconnection process as a controlled workflow with codified SLAs, sign-offs, and always-on audit trails, replacing the ad-hoc reporting that peer utilities have been fined for. Directly de-risks the debt-recovery Agentforce play by giving the regulator a documented control layer over automated decisions. Metric: ESC SLA hit-rate and time-to-evidence per complaint.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, HiAffinity

Key Contact: Head of Customer Advocacy (TBD — field verify)

3. Australian Privacy Act & Notifiable Data Breach Controls

Following the historical data breach and the Phase 2 review, SEW must demonstrate active compliance with the Australian Privacy Act (APPs) and the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme — on every system that touches customer PII. Compliance Center runs the assess-contain-notify workflow as a codified process with segregation-of-duties, evidence, and 30-day OAIC clock tracking, so any future incident is handled by a repeatable audited path rather than a bespoke response. Also serves as the governance layer that unblocks the Einstein AI features currently held pending compliance approval. Metric: incident-to-notification cycle time and audit findings per privacy assessment.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce, HiAffinity
