Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Billing-to-Cash on HiAffinity + Salesforce

Mine the billing-to-cash flow from meter read through bill generation in HiAffinity into Salesforce Service Cloud dispute and adjustment cases. The pain is well-documented: at the VicWater SIC Peter O'Donoghue and peer CIOs named "billing complexities" as a shared, unsolved industry problem, and the Phase 2 review is explicitly scoped around HiAffinity data flows. AFPI surfaces where adjustments, re-bills, and exceptions loop back to agents, then feeds Agentforce with the rules to auto-resolve high-frequency variants. Target metric: 15-25% reduction in billing-driven service contacts and a measurable cut in days-sales-outstanding.

2. Debt Recovery & Hardship Management

Debt recovery is one of the five named Agentforce priority use cases at SEW and touches the new Marketing Cloud instance for arrears communications. Mining the recovery journey — from first-missed-payment through hardship program enrolment, payment plans, and disconnection avoidance — pinpoints where cases stall, where hardship customers get re-contacted, and where automated Marketing Cloud journeys can safely replace agent touchpoints. The metric that moves is recovery-per-agent-hour and the reduction in avoidable disconnection escalations, both regulator-sensitive numbers.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud

3. Contact-Centre IVR to Agent Handoff (AF Voice)

SEW's pilot Service Cloud Voice + Amazon Connect solution already handles ~30% of Billing & Account calls, with a live AF Voice / IVR expansion queued as a priority Agentforce use case. Process-mining the IVR-to-agent path exposes where self-service intent is being mis-classified, where callers escalate needlessly, and which containment opportunities Agentforce Voice should own next. AFPI provides the evidence layer that converts the pilot into a full production rollout with a defensible business case. Metric: containment rate lifted from ~30% toward 55-65% on billing enquiries.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud Voice, Amazon Connect

4. Case Management & Omni-Channel Routing Efficiency

Case management is explicitly on the Agentforce priority list and Omni-Channel routing is already deployed across voice, message, and email. Directly supports the business case for turning on the Einstein features already implemented but awaiting governance approval.
