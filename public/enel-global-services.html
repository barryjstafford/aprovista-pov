Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Group-wide Procure-to-Pay Harmonization

Enel Global Procurement runs P2P for the entire Enel Group across dozens of operating companies and countries. Local variants, exception approvals, and rework loops inflate cycle time and hide maverick spend. Mining SAP + Ariba event logs exposes the true "as-is" per country, quantifies rework, and targets the top 3 automation wedges for Agentforce Operations. Metric moved: invoice cycle time (typical 20-35% reduction) and touchless-invoice rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP ERP, Ariba

Key Contact: Fabio Sarcinelli (Head of Services, SHSEQ Digital Factory)

2. Meter-to-Cash for e-distribuzione & Enel Italia

Meter-to-cash is Enel's most valuable — and most complex — customer process, spanning smart-meter data ingestion, billing, dispute handling, and collections. Regulatory windows (ARERA) and free-market switching add exception paths that eat margin. Apromore mines the end-to-end path across SAP IS-U and Salesforce Service Cloud to expose where disputes and re-bills originate, and where Agentforce can auto-resolve. Metric moved: days-sales-outstanding and first-bill-right rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP IS-U, Salesforce Service Cloud

Key Contact: Marco Codispoti (Head of Market Digital Factory Italy)

Enel is a reference benchmark for utility field-technician operations, and the $495k Geocall Replacement opp signals a live Field Service transformation on Salesforce.
