1. Group-wide Procure-to-Pay Harmonization

Enel Global Procurement runs P2P for the entire Enel Group across dozens of operating companies and countries. Local variants, exception approvals, and rework loops inflate cycle time and hide maverick spend. Mining SAP + Ariba event logs exposes the true "as-is" per country, quantifies rework, and targets the top 3 automation wedges for Agentforce Operations. 2. Meter-to-Cash for e-distribuzione & Enel Italia

Meter-to-cash is Enel's most valuable — and most complex — customer process, spanning smart-meter data ingestion, billing, dispute handling, and collections. Regulatory windows (ARERA) and free-market switching add exception paths that eat margin. Apromore mines the end-to-end path across SAP IS-U and Salesforce Service Cloud to expose where disputes and re-bills originate, and where Agentforce can auto-resolve. Metric moved: days-sales-outstanding and first-bill-right rate.

Mining Salesforce Field Service + workforce-management logs quantifies technician idle time and permit-loop rework, feeding Agentforce Operations for dispatch optimization.
