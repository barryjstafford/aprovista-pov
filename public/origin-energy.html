1. Meter-to-Cash (M2C) Cycle-Time & Exception Mining

Origin's retail M2C spans meter reads, billing, dispute handling, and collections across SAP and Marketing Cloud journeys. Public AER data shows Australian retailers absorb material bill-correction and complaint volume that erodes margin and NPS. Apromore mines the true process from SAP billing logs + Data Cloud events to expose rebill loops, exception-handling bottlenecks, and journey drop-offs — then hands the highest-value automations directly to Agentforce Operations. 4. Complaints & Hardship Handling — AER Conformance

Australian energy retailers operate under strict AER Retail Law obligations for hardship, disconnection-of-last-resort, and complaint SLAs. Apromore's conformance-checking engine compares Origin's designed process to the actual event trail from Service Cloud + case data — instantly showing where hardship cases skip mandatory checkpoints or breach timing rules. This is Compliance-Center wedge territory: it protects Origin against AER enforcement action while feeding Agentforce Service agents cleaner playbooks. Metric moved: AER breach exposure, hardship-case cycle time, complaint recurrence.
