Apromore mines the true process from SAP billing logs plus Data Cloud events to expose rebill loops, exception-handling bottlenecks, and where cases spin between billing and care. It hands the highest-value automations directly to the Agentforce Operations agents Origin is already piloting. Metric moved: days-to-bill, first-time-right billing %, cost-to-serve per account.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. AER Retail Law — Hardship & Complaint Conformance

Australian energy retailers operate under strict AER Retail Law obligations for hardship, disconnection-of-last-resort, and complaint SLAs, with real enforcement risk. Compliance Center compares Origin's designed process to the actual event trail from Service Cloud + case data — instantly showing where hardship cases skip mandatory checkpoints or breach timing rules. It protects Origin against AER action while feeding Agentforce Service agents cleaner playbooks. Metric moved: AER breach exposure, hardship-case cycle time, complaint recurrence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Data Cloud

Key Contact: Head of Customer Care & Compliance

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. AEMO Wholesale Market Compliance & Settlement Controls

AEMO's National Electricity Rules and Gas Rules impose bidding, dispatch conformance, and settlement obligations that carry material civil penalties when breached. Compliance Center codifies the bid-to-settle process with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and continuous conformance checks across the trading and settlement estate. It gives risk and audit a live view rather than a quarterly attestation. Metric moved: rule-breach exposure, control-coverage %, mean-time-to-evidence per audit request.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Trading platform, GL / settlement

Key Contact: Head of Wholesale & Trading Risk

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. NGER & Climate Disclosure Reporting Controls

The National Greenhouse and Energy Reporting (NGER) scheme plus Australia's mandatory climate-disclosure regime require Origin to produce auditable Scope 1/2/3 numbers on tight timelines. Compliance Center runs the emissions-data close as a controlled workflow — source-system pulls, reviewer sign-offs, restatement handling — and captures continuous evidence for the Clean Energy Regulator and external assurance. Especially valuable given Origin Zero's transition narrative is now investor-material. Metric moved: reporting cycle time, restatements per period, audit findings.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Emissions data platform, GL / consolidation
