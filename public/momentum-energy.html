Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Contact-Centre Case Resolution & Agent Handoff

Retail energy contact centres carry unpredictable variance in first-contact resolution and repeat-caller loops driven by billing disputes, connections/disconnections and hardship enquiries. Momentum has publicly committed to a Salesforce Agentic Contact Center rollout with 8-10 agent-led use cases plus voice — mining Service Cloud + voice event logs establishes the today-state cycle time and rework rate so agent deployments can be A/B evaluated against baseline. Metric moved: average handle time, first-contact resolution, agent deflection rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Ruchin Gupta (Salesforce Account Executive on Momentum) — for onward introduction to Momentum contact-centre leadership

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Meter-to-Cash / Billing Exception Handling

Australian energy retailers routinely see 10-20% of monthly bills flow through an exception-handling process (estimate reads, NMI disputes, market-message failures). This process spans the billing engine, market gateway, and back-office adjustments — a classic multi-system mining target where hidden rework is the single biggest lever on cost-to-serve. AFPI can quantify the exception funnel and pinpoint the two or three market-message failure types that drive most of the manual work. Metric moved: exception rate, days-to-bill, back-office FTE per 100k customers.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Billing platform (SAP IS-U or Gentrack — TBD), MuleSoft

Key Contact: Bharath Kadiam (Salesforce Solution Engineering — Momentum)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Customer Onboarding & Move-In / Move-Out

Momentum serves 200,000+ homes and businesses across VIC, NSW, SA and QLD, meaning move-in / move-out and switch-in flows are a high-volume, high-churn-risk journey involving market messages (NEM12/NEM13), identity checks, CDR consent, and welcome comms. Mining this end-to-end from the customer web portal through MuleSoft into billing exposes drop-off points, duplicate-record creation and hand-off delays. Metric moved: switch completion rate, days-to-active, cost per acquisition.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MyAccount portal / MuleSoft, Salesforce Sales & Service Cloud

Key Contact: Alicia Gunn (Salesforce — Momentum account team)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
4. Solar / Battery / VPP Connection Workflow

Momentum markets solar, battery, EV and Virtual Power Plant / Virtual Solar Sharing products — each requiring a distributor pre-approval, metering reconfiguration, and post-install commissioning that regularly stall for weeks. Mining these connect-and-activate flows reveals which distributor jurisdictions and installer partners cause the longest lead times, and quantifies revenue-at-risk from stalled activations. Metric moved: days-from-signup-to-first-export, activation completion rate, distributor-hold rework loops.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service / Sales Cloud, Distributor market-message gateway

Key Contact: Sean Adrichem (Salesforce — Momentum engagement)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
5. Hardship, Payment Plans & Complaints (AER / EWOV compliance)

Under AER retail-energy rules and state ombudsman scrutiny, hardship enrolment, payment-plan management and complaint handling must follow prescribed sequences within regulated timeframes. Mining these regulated processes proves compliance (or exposes the specific step where SLAs slip), and does so with the same event data already flowing through Service Cloud — making it a natural anchor use case that pairs the Compliance Center wedge with contact-centre modernisation. Metric moved: EWOV/AER complaint count, days-to-resolution, hardship enrolment SLA breaches.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Billing platform (TBD)
