Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Contact-Centre Case Resolution & Agent Handoff

Retail energy contact centres carry unpredictable variance in first-contact resolution and repeat-caller loops driven by billing disputes, connections/disconnections and hardship enquiries. Momentum has a $293k Contact Centre opp in negotiation plus a $277k AFCC Pilot + Expansion — mining Service Cloud + voice event logs establishes the today-state cycle time and rework rate so agent deployments can be A/B evaluated against baseline. Metric moved: average handle time, first-contact resolution, agent deflection rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Vy Kim (Momentum Energy — Agentforce Melbourne fireside speaker on energy retail transformation)

2. Meter-to-Cash / Billing Exception Handling

Australian energy retailers routinely see 10-20% of monthly bills flow through an exception-handling process (estimate reads, NMI disputes, market-message failures). The open Momentum Billing RFP ($280k) confirms billing is on the table — a classic multi-system mining target where hidden rework drives cost-to-serve. AFPI can quantify the exception funnel and pinpoint the two or three market-message failure types that account for most of the manual work. Metric moved: exception rate, days-to-bill, back-office FTE per 100k customers.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Billing platform, MuleSoft

3. Customer Onboarding & Move-In / Move-Out

Momentum serves 200,000+ homes and businesses across VIC, NSW, SA, TAS and QLD, meaning move-in / move-out and switch-in flows are a high-volume, high-churn-risk journey involving market messages (NEM12/NEM13), identity checks, CDR consent, and welcome comms. Mining this end-to-end from the customer web portal through MuleSoft into billing exposes drop-off points, duplicate-record creation and hand-off delays. Metric moved: switch completion rate, days-to-active, cost per acquisition.

Task mining captures each agent's on-screen actions to isolate the specific tabs and steps that consume wrap minutes, feeding the AFCC business case directly. Metric moved: average wrap time, after-call-work minutes per contact, tabs-per-case.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. AER Hardship & Payment Plan Compliance

Under the AER retail-energy rules, hardship enrolment, payment-plan management and disconnection warnings must follow prescribed sequences within regulated timeframes. Compliance Center codifies that process as a controlled workflow with codified SLAs and evidence, and produces always-on audit artefacts for the AER and state ombudsmen. Metric moved: hardship enrolment SLA hit-rate, AER complaint count, disconnection audit findings.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, billing platform

Key Contact: Head of Regulatory Compliance (TBD — field verify)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. CDR Consumer Data Right Consent Lifecycle

Energy CDR obligations require Momentum to record, refresh and revoke customer data-sharing consent within strict SLAs, with an auditable trail on every consent event. Compliance Center runs the consent lifecycle as a controlled workflow across MyAccount, MuleSoft and Service Cloud, producing evidence for the ACCC and OAIC on demand. Metric moved: consent audit-ready coverage, expired-consent leakage rate, consent breach count.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: MuleSoft, Salesforce Service Cloud

3. AEMO Market-Message SLA Controls (NEM12/NEM13)

Every meter read, standing data change, and switch-in exchanged with AEMO and network operators via NEM12/NEM13 market messages carries regulated response SLAs; misses drive fines and downstream billing exceptions. Compliance Center pins the market-message exchange to a controlled workflow with codified SLAs, escalations, and evidence for AEMO audits. Metric moved: market-message SLA hit-rate, unresolved failure count, audit findings per compliance cycle.
