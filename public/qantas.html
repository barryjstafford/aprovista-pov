Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Safety & Risk Case-Management Cycle Time

Every safety report, hazard, and incident at Qantas Group must be triaged, investigated, and closed under CASA and IOSA audit windows. Today those cases flow through multiple hand-offs across flight ops, engineering, and cabin safety with limited end-to-end visibility. AFPI can mine the full case lifecycle to expose reopens, aged queues, and investigator bottlenecks, then feed Agentforce nudges that route and escalate the right cases faster. Target metric: median case cycle time and % reopened after closure — already funded via the live $200k Apromore Safety & Risk opp.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, MuleSoft

2. On-Time Departure & Turnaround Sequence

Turnaround is a choreography of fuelling, catering, cleaning, boarding, and dispatch — dozens of dependent steps that ripple into delay codes when any actor slips.
