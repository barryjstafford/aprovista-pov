Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Post-Mediobanca Merger Process Harmonization

With the February 2026 board approval to delist Mediobanca and fully merge it into MPS, the group is entering the largest process-integration event in Italian banking in a decade. Two distinct operating models — a retail-heavy universal bank and a corporate/investment bank — must be reconciled across credit, wealth, and back-office, sitting on top of the $1.5M Merge Support CSG opportunity. AFPI mines current-state event logs on both sides to expose which variants to keep, which to retire, and where handoffs will break under a unified target operating model. Metric moved: post-merger integration cycle time and rework in the harmonization program.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Core Banking (TBD), Salesforce CRM

Key Contact: Enrica Terranova (Chief Transformation / Head of Innovation, AI & App Infrastructure)

2. Loan & Mortgage Origination Cycle Time

Retail lending remains MPS's largest earning asset, and its origination journey spans branch submission, credit decisioning, valuation, and disbursement across legacy Italian core-banking platforms. Post-bail-out, ECB and Bank of Italy continue to scrutinize lending-decision timeliness and audit-trail integrity. AFPI mines the origination process end-to-end to expose rework loops, verification bottlenecks, and cases where broker or branch abandonment triggers volume loss. Metric moved: median time-to-disbursement and pull-through on approved applications.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Loan Origination Platform (TBD), Salesforce CRM

3. Customer Onboarding & Front-End CRM Journey

The $920k Front End CRM opportunity and the New Digital Branch RFP (at Confirming Value With Power stage) tell the same story: MPS is rebuilding the customer-facing service journey while retaining a heavy branch footprint across Tuscany and Italy. Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. Contact Center Agent Handle-Time (900 tickets/day, MIA chatbot)

MPS runs a 120-agent contact center (plus overflow outsourcers) fielding ~900 tickets per day, 40% of which are already deflected by MIA, the Watson-based chatbot. That leaves the remaining 60% split across five business units where agents swivel between Watson, the core-banking front end, Salesforce, and email. Task mining captures each agent's on-screen work to isolate after-call wrap steps and the tabs eating handle time — the exact evidence Contact Center director Domenica Arena has already flagged interest in. Metric moved: average handle time and after-call-work minutes per contact type.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Contact Center desktop, Watson (MIA) chatbot

Key Contact: Domenica Arena (Director, Contact Center Rete & Clientela)

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. DORA Operational Resilience & ITSM Change Evidence

The open MPS-New-ITSM opportunity to swap BMC coincides with the EU Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA), which requires Italian banks to demonstrate evidence-based control of ICT change, incident, and third-party processes. Combined with the Mediobanca integration, IT change velocity is about to spike exactly when auditors are watching most closely. Compliance Center runs incident-to-resolution and change-management processes as controlled workflows and produces continuous evidence regulators can consume — and pre-configures the new ITSM tool against real variants rather than idealized workflows. Metric moved: mean-time-to-restore and DORA audit finding closure time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: BMC / New ITSM, Change Management (TBD)

Key Contact: Paolo Abbo (Head of ICT Compliance)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. AML / KYC Alert Adjudication & Case Handling

Italian banks operate under EBA MRT, ECB SREP, and Banca d'Italia AML supervisory intensity that has only sharpened after the Mediobanca takeover put MPS at the centre of Italian financial regulation. Alert-to-disposition analysts still see high false-positive rates and inconsistent case paths, and the group must now unify two financial-crime operating models. Compliance Center runs alert triage, escalation, and SAR filing as a single controlled workflow with codified SLAs, and exposes the highest-volume variants for Agentforce assist. Metric moved: alert-clearing SLA hit-rate, productive-alert ratio, and time-to-SAR.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Transaction Monitoring (TBD), Case Management (TBD)

Key Contact: Antonio Lombrano (Head of Group Security Strategy, Fraud & BCM)
