Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Post-Mediobanca Merger Process Harmonization

With the February 2026 board approval to delist Mediobanca and fully merge it into MPS, the group is entering the largest process-integration event in Italian banking in a decade. Two distinct operating models — a retail-heavy universal bank and a corporate/investment bank — must be reconciled across credit, wealth, and back-office. AFPI mines the current-state event logs on both sides to expose which variants to keep, which to retire, and where handoffs will break under a unified target operating model. Metric moved: post-merger integration cycle time and rework in the harmonization program.

2. Loan & Mortgage Origination Cycle Time

Retail lending remains MPS's largest earning asset and its origination journey spans branch submission, credit decisioning, valuation, and disbursement across legacy Italian core-banking platforms. Post-bail-out, ECB and Bank of Italy have kept a close eye on lending-decision timeliness and audit trail integrity. AFPI mines the origination process end-to-end to expose rework loops, verification bottlenecks, and cases where broker or branch abandonment triggers volume loss. Metric moved: median time-to-disbursement and pull-through on approved applications.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Loan Origination Platform (TBD), Salesforce CRM

Key Contact: Massimo Di Carluccio (Sales Director - Retail Division)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. AML / KYC Alert Triage & Case Handling

Italian banks operate under EBA MRT, ECB SREP, and Banca d'Italia AML supervisory intensity that has only sharpened after the Generali stake and Mediobanca takeover put MPS at the centre of Italian financial regulation. Alert-to-disposition analysts still see high false-positive rates and inconsistent case paths, and the group must now unify two financial-crime operating models. AFPI mines the alert lifecycle to surface variants driving SLA breaches and to target the highest-volume paths for Agentforce assist and straight-through automation. Metric moved: cost per alert closed and productive-alert ratio.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Transaction Monitoring (TBD), Case Management (TBD)

Key Contact: Antonio Lombrano (Resp. Group Security Strategy, Fraud & BCM)

4. Customer Onboarding & Front-End CRM Journey

The $920k Front End CRM opportunity and the New Digital Branch RFP (at Confirming Value With Power stage) tell the same story: MPS is rebuilding the customer-facing service journey while retaining a heavy branch footprint across Tuscany and Italy. Without a process-mined as-is baseline, the target Salesforce-based journey is designed from PowerPoint rather than event data, which drives rework once the branch and contact-center rollout begins. AFPI mines the current onboarding and servicing paths across the branch, contact center, and digital channels to prioritize which variants to lift onto the new stack first. Metric moved: time-to-first-transaction for new customers and NPS on retained customers.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce CRM, Contact Center (TBD)

Key Contact: Stefano Marra (Head of CRM & Operations Service, Director)

5. IT Service Management & DORA Operational-Resilience Evidence

The open MPS-New-ITSM opportunity to swap BMC coincides with the EU Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA), which requires Italian banks to demonstrate evidence-based control of ICT change, incident, and third-party processes. Combined with the Mediobanca integration, IT change velocity is about to spike exactly when auditors are watching most closely. AFPI mines the incident-to-resolution and change-management processes to give the operational-resilience team a Compliance Center wedge with process evidence regulators can consume directly, and highlights where the new ITSM tool will need to be configured against real variants rather than idealized workflows. Metric moved: mean-time-to-restore and DORA audit finding closure time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: BMC/New ITSM, Change Management (TBD)
