1. Auto Loan Origination — Time-to-Decision & Dealer Funding Cycle

SFS is standing up a new loan-origination platform (SFS Loan Origination Services PH2, $1.0M) and Adaptive Agent for Sales — both create fresh event logs across dealer submission, credit decision, contract booking, and funds disbursement. Dealers churn to competing captives when funding slips past 24 hours, and manual stipulations reviews add rework loops that never show up in KPI dashboards. AFPI mines the origination event stream from MuleSoft-integrated dealer, LOS, and Service Cloud data to expose decisioning bottlenecks, stipulation-driven rework, and dealer variance — targeting a 30-40% reduction in cycle time and higher captive penetration.

2. Collections & Delinquency Workflow Optimization

In a rising-delinquency auto-finance market, collections cure rate is the single biggest lever on net charge-offs — every day earlier a delinquent account is worked converts directly to basis points saved. Current queues, dialer strategies, promise-to-pay follow-ups, and legal-handoff triggers are governed by static rules that no one has time to re-tune against actual behavior. AFPI mines Service Cloud case, dialer, and payment-event logs to reveal which agent paths, contact channels, and treatment sequences drive the fastest cure — feeding Agentforce to route the right contact to the right agent at the right hour. Metric: 3-5 bps improvement in net loss on portfolio scale.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Head of Collections (Stellantis Financial Services)

3. Dealer Onboarding & Floor Plan Servicing

Bringing a Chrysler/Dodge/Jeep/Ram/Fiat dealer live on SFS floor plan and retail programs is a multi-team dance across credit, compliance, legal, and IT provisioning — the Experience Cloud Partner Portal ($529K subscribers on the current SFA renewal, $5.8M) is the front door but the back-office swivel-chair is invisible.
