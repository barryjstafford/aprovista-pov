2. Collections & Delinquency Workflow Optimization. In a rising-delinquency auto-finance market, collections cure rate is the single biggest lever on net charge-offs — every day earlier a delinquent account is worked converts directly to basis points saved. Current queues, dialer strategies, promise-to-pay follow-ups, and legal-handoff triggers are governed by static rules that no one has time to re-tune against actual behavior. AFPI mines Service Cloud case, dialer, and payment-event logs to reveal which agent paths, contact channels, and treatment sequences drive the fastest cure — feeding Agentforce to route the right contact to the right agent at the right hour. Metric: 3-5 bps improvement in net loss on portfolio scale. Engage the Head of Collections at Stellantis Financial Services.

3. Dealer Onboarding & Floor Plan Servicing. Bringing a Chrysler/Dodge/Jeep/Ram/Fiat dealer live on SFS floor plan and retail programs is a multi-team dance across credit, compliance, legal, and IT provisioning — the Experience Cloud Partner Portal ($529K subscribers on the current SFA renewal, $5.8M) is the front door but the back-office swivel-chair is invisible. Every week of onboarding delay is lost paper contracts and lost captive share to Ally, GM Financial, and Ford Credit. AFPI mines Partner Portal, MuleSoft, and Service Cloud event logs to expose which onboarding steps stall, why, and by whom — feeding an Agentforce Operations agent that auto-nudges the blocking party. Metric: 40% faster dealer time-to-first-contract. Engage the Head of Dealer Services at Stellantis Financial Services.

4. Customer Servicing & Contact-Center Deflection. The Service Cloud footprint plus $918K Slack for US and $250K Adaptive Agent for Sales opp signals an active push toward AI-assisted servicing. Payoff quotes, extension requests, insurance and title issues, and end-of-lease inspections dominate call volume — and each is a repeatable process with clear event trails. 5. Regulatory & Compliance Center — CFPB, GLBA, FDCPA Evidence.
