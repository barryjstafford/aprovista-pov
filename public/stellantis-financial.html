1. Auto Loan Origination — Time-to-Decision & Dealer Funding Cycle

SFS is standing up a new loan-origination platform (SFS LOS PH2, $1.0M) plus an Adaptive Agent for Sales — both create fresh event logs across dealer submission, credit decision, stipulations review, contract booking, and funds disbursement. Dealers churn to Ally, GM Financial, and Ford Credit when funding slips past 24 hours, and manual stipulations reviews add rework loops that never show up in KPI dashboards. 2. Collections Cure-Rate & Delinquency Workflow Optimization

In a rising-delinquency auto-finance market, cure rate is the single biggest lever on net charge-offs — every day earlier a delinquent account is worked converts directly to basis points saved. Current queues, dialer strategies, promise-to-pay follow-ups, and legal-handoff triggers are governed by static rules that no one has time to re-tune against actual behavior, and the SFS Collections AF Sales Coach opp acknowledges the gap. AFPI mines Service Cloud case, dialer, and payment-event logs to reveal which agent paths, contact channels, and treatment sequences drive the fastest cure — feeding Agentforce to route the right contact to the right agent at the right hour. Metric: 3-5 bps improvement in net loss at portfolio scale.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, MuleSoft

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

Turns CFPB exams from months of pulls into on-demand dashboards. Metric: 50% reduction in exam-response labor, materially lower regulatory risk exposure.

3. SCRA & Repossession Compliance

The Servicemembers Civil Relief Act plus state repossession rules require verifiable proof, on every affected account, that SFS checked DoD status, capped interest rates, and honored notice periods before any adverse action. Miss one and the fine is enterprise-material. Compliance Center runs the repo/adverse-action workflow as a controlled process with mandatory SCRA checkpoints and produces evidence per account — not per sample. Metric: 100% SCRA-covered adverse actions with defensible per-account evidence, and 50% cut in state-DMV audit response time.
