Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Guest Booking-to-Boarding Journey

Costa books guests across web, mobile, direct-to-consumer contact centers, and the AIDA German branch, then hands each guest through pre-cruise upsell, check-in, and boarding across ~10 ships. Cart abandonment, payment-failure loops, and check-in exceptions each shave points off net promoter score and per-guest revenue. AFPI mines the full booking-to-boarding event log across Sales Cloud, MuleSoft flows, and the reservation platform, exposes drop-off hotspots, and lets a business analyst re-simulate policy changes without a PQL specialist. Metric: booking conversion rate and check-in cycle time per ship.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Mario Zanetti (President, Costa Crociere)

2. Travel-Agency (B2B) Partner Enablement & Flex Credits

Costa's B2B channel — a massive travel-agency network — is the growth engine, and Salesforce is actively positioning Flex Credits and Agentforce for Service to empower it. Today agency partners bounce across booking tools, credit lookups, and back-office support with no shared view of their own case history. AFPI mines the agency-to-Costa case flow, quantifies handoff loops, and feeds Agentforce for Service to auto-route the highest-value agencies. Metric: agency case cycle time and Flex Credits attach rate per partner tier.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, MuleSoft

3. Ship-to-Shore Operational Data Flow & MDM Reconciliation

Costa runs a webMethods-to-MuleSoft agentic-layer migration and an Informatica MDM E2 program in parallel — event data flowing between ships (intermittent MQTT) and shore-side master data. Every reconciliation gap between ship POS, on-board revenue, and shore-side guest records leaks margin and creates PII/GDPR risk. AFPI mines the end-to-end data flow, quantifies reconciliation exceptions, and gives Data Cloud + Informatica a documented target-state process. Metric: MDM data-quality issue count and on-ship revenue leakage recovered.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. EU MRV / IMO Decarbonization Voyage Reporting

Costa's LNG-powered Smeralda and Toscana plus the wider fleet sit at the center of EU MRV emissions reporting, IMO 2030/2050 decarbonization, and EU ETS-Maritime obligations — every voyage is a compliance event. Today the fuel-log-to-emissions-report path spans on-ship telemetry, shore-side energy management, and finance sign-off with manual reconciliation. Compliance Center codifies the voyage-to-report flow with segregation-of-duties and always-on evidence, turning a compliance line item into a durable renewal moat. Metric: reporting-cycle FTE and voyage-level audit findings.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Voyage data platform, shore-side energy management
