Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Family Banker Client-Onboarding & KYC (Retail Wealth)

Banca Mediolanum's differentiator is its ~6,000 Family Bankers advising retail wealth clients across Italy, Germany, Spain, and Ireland — but that model turns onboarding into a many-hop process across advisor app, KYC engine, custody, and MDM. AFPI mines the client-to-first-investment journey to expose where documents get re-requested, where AML/PEP checks bounce back, and where advisors abandon in favor of manual work-arounds. With Informatica MDM already governing the golden customer record, the event log is unusually clean and ready to mine. Metric moved: onboarding cycle time, advisor drop-off rate, first-investment time-to-fund.

2. Cross-Border Operating-Model Harmonization (IT / DE / ES / IE)

Banca Mediolanum runs the same "Family Banker" model across four legal entities — Banca Mediolanum (IT), Bankhaus August Lenz (DE), Banco Mediolanum (Spain), and the Irish life/asset entity — but each stack has grown its own onboarding, complaint, and product-transfer variations. AFPI mines the same golden process across all four subsidiaries to quantify variance, expose which country's flow is best-in-class, and generate a data-backed harmonization roadmap. This directly supports the cost-to-serve and CET1 story to the ECB. Metric moved: process-variance rate across subsidiaries, cost-per-transaction gap, harmonization-milestone slippage.

3. Post-Mediobanca-Divestment Treasury & Reporting Reshape

In June 2025 Banca Mediolanum divested its full 3.5% stake in Mediobanca via accelerated bookbuild, freeing roughly €548M in capital and forcing a reshape of treasury reporting, investment governance, and ECB CET1 disclosures. AFPI mines the month-end close, investment-decision, and regulatory-reporting flows to expose where teams are still doing manual reconciliation post-divestment, and where governance controls have not yet caught up to the new capital base. This is a time-boxed, high-visibility play tied to a real board-level event. Metric moved: month-end close cycle time, reconciliation exception rate, ECB report re-work rate.
