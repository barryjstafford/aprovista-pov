The Feb 2026 Kito Crosby acquisition bolts a $1B+ rigging/hardware business onto CMCO's hoist and motion portfolio, creating two parallel OTC stacks with duplicative branch, pricing, and billing steps across 15+ brands. AFPI mines both legacy event logs to expose which variants win on cycle time and margin, then guides a single target flow — the 2–4 week time-to-value case for merger integration, feeding directly into the active $879k Sales Cloud renewal. Metric moved: quote-to-cash cycle time and DSO.
