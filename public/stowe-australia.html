Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Field Service Dispatch-to-Complete Optimization

Stowe dispatches hundreds of electricians and technicians daily across 12 branches for construction, service, and maintenance work. Every extra hour of travel, callback, or wrong-branch routing bleeds margin off a A$500M revenue base. Mining the dispatch-to-close event log surfaces travel loops, unproductive triage, and jobs that route through the wrong branch — feeding the live $200k Field Service Transformation opp with hard evidence. Metric: first-time-fix rate and technician utilization.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, Service Cloud

2. Electrical Licensing & Certification Currency Audit

Every Stowe electrician, cabler, and supervisor operates under a state-issued license (NSW Fair Trading, ESV, ORS) with hard expiry dates — work performed with a lapsed licence is a regulatory and insurance exposure. Compliance Center runs the licence-renewal and certification-currency process as a controlled workflow with SLA-driven escalations and audit-ready evidence. This ties directly to insurance and pre-qualification for tier-1 principal contractors. Metric: percentage of active workforce with current, verified licences and mean time to renewal.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: HRIS / workforce compliance system (TBD), Salesforce
