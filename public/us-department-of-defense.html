T4 Deprioritize — Why Not Now

Why AFPI Cannot Pursue This Account Today

The US Department of Defense represents the world's largest operational organization with genuine, massive process complexity — $886B in annual spend, 2.87M personnel, and procurement/logistics/maintenance operations spanning every branch of the military. The process mining opportunity is real. The sales path is not, for four structural reasons:

1. No AFPI territory coverage. The AFPI AE map covers commercial North America, EMEA, ANZ, and APAC. There is no Public Sector AE. Any engagement must be routed through Salesforce's Missionforce National Security organization before a commercial AFPI play can be attached. [Org62]

2. Government cloud authorization barrier. DoD operates on US Government Cloud with FedRAMP High and IL4/IL5 classification requirements. AFPI would need a separate FedRAMP authorization to be deployed in this environment. This is a multi-year certification path, not a sales cycle blocker to solve in a quarter. [Web]

3. Federal procurement model. All active Org62 opps for this account are Informatica/INFA data platform deals running through federal contracting vehicles (GWACs, ELAs). The commercial AFPI GTM motion — direct AE engagement with a VP Transformation — does not translate to FAR-compliant federal acquisition. [Org62]

4. No commercial champion identified. CIO Timothy Paul [Org62] is the only senior contact on record, with no VP Operations/Transformation or process excellence owner reachable through commercial channels.

The Signal Worth Monitoring

Missionforce National Security + Agentforce. In June 2026, Salesforce closed a $72M ELA with the US Air Force (a DoD sub-agency) for Missionforce National Security. The Air Force is actively piloting Agentforce to automate complex workflows including aircraft maintenance and deployment orders — exactly the process-mining use case AFPI supports. [Slack/#media-coverage, 2026-06-10]

If AFPI is bundled into or certified alongside the Missionforce National Security product suite, the Air Force Agentforce pilot becomes a direct entry point for process discovery. The CDAO ADVANA data modernization initiative ($280K opp, Stage 03 Validating) also signals that DoD is investing in the data infrastructure that would eventually support event-log-based process mining.

What Would Need to Change

  • AFPI achieves FedRAMP Moderate/High authorization, or is bundled into Missionforce National Security as a certified capability.
  • A Public Sector AFPI AE is designated with a federal contracting vehicle.
  • The Air Force Agentforce pilot (aircraft maintenance / deployment orders) formally requests a process-map to guide agent deployment — creating the first pull signal.

Recommended handoff: Share the Air Force Agentforce pilot signal with the Missionforce National Security team. Flag CDAO ADVANA as a long-term data-readiness indicator. Revisit when FedRAMP authorization is in progress.
