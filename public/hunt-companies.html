Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Work-Order Turn & Repair Cycle Time (Field Service)

Hunt runs 876+ Field Service Plus techs plus a large contractor pool across ~50 military installations, and turn-time on unit make-ready and resident repair is the loudest MHPI KPI. Mining the Work Order → Service Appointment → Dispatch → Complete cycle exposes rework loops, cross-base dispatch waste, and contractor SLA breach patterns. AFPI can quantify hours lost per rework and simulate scheduling policy changes before they hit the field. Metric moved: median unit-turn days and first-time-fix rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, Service Cloud

Key Contact: Todd Zylka (Vice President, Operations)

2. Move-In / Move-Out & Lease Turnover

PCS-driven turnover creates spiky move-in / move-out volume that has to hand off cleanly between community managers, maintenance, contractors, and billing. Mining the lease turnover process end-to-end surfaces bottlenecks between inspection, make-ready, and re-occupancy, and shows where variance across bases is driving vacancy loss. AFPI variant analysis lets Hunt see which portfolio operations directors are running the process cleanly and which aren't. Metric moved: days-vacant per unit and turn cost variance.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Field Service

3. Resident Service Case & Call-Center Deflection

Hunt has 1,585 Call Center seats and 15k+ Customer Community logins fielding resident complaints — a well-documented reputational pain point for privatized military housing. Mining the case-to-resolution flow shows which reason codes drive repeat contacts, where the community-manager escalation path adds days, and where an Agentforce agent could deflect routine tickets. AFPI ties Agentforce ROI to concrete conformance gaps rather than a demo. Metric moved: repeat-contact rate and mean-time-to-resolve.

Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

Community managers at each installation flip between Service Cloud, the Customer Community portal, work-order screens in Field Service, and email/Outlook while triaging resident complaints. Task mining the community-manager desktop reveals how much time is lost to app-switching, re-keying resident details, and repeated policy lookups. That evidence anchors the Agentforce for Service business case and shows exactly which steps an agent can safely take. Metric moved: cases per FTE per day and touch-time per case.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. MHPI / HUD / DoD Housing Inspection & Remediation

MHPI compliance obligations (HUD, DoD, environmental, lead/asbestos, life-safety) require documented preventive-maintenance and inspection cadences per unit and per base, with Congressional oversight scrutinizing the results. Compliance Center codifies the scheduled-inspection → completion → remediation workflow with segregation-of-duties, evidence capture, and early-warning conformance dashboards. Bases running behind cadence surface before the auditor does. Metric moved: on-time inspection compliance and open-remediation age.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Audra Froom (Vice President of Transformation Projects)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Contractor Onboarding & Certification Controls

Third-party contractors touching military housing units must clear background checks, insurance verification, safety training, and DoD-mandated certifications before they are dispatched. Compliance Center runs contractor onboarding as a controlled workflow with mandatory approvals, expirations, and auditable evidence, replacing today's spreadsheet-plus-email pattern. Expired certifications no longer slip through into dispatch. Metric moved: percentage of dispatches to fully-certified contractors and time-to-onboard.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, contractor onboarding portal

Key Contact: Michael Lowry (VP Controller)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Resident Complaint Escalation & Congressional-Inquiry Response

Hunt's 15k+ Customer Community and 1,585-seat call center generate resident complaints that can escalate to base command, DoD leadership, or Congressional inquiries within days. Compliance Center governs the escalation ladder as a controlled process — SLAs at each rung, documented sign-offs, and always-on evidence packages ready for hearing prep. This transforms escalation response from ad-hoc email threads into an auditable, defensible workflow. Metric moved: escalation SLA hit-rate and time-to-evidence-package.
