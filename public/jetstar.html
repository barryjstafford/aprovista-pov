Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Aircraft Turnaround & On-Time Performance

Mine the end-to-end turnaround sequence — block-on, cabin clean, catering, fuel, boarding, block-off — across A320 / 787 fleets and the remaining Group ports. Jetstar's LCC economics depend on 25-minute turns, so every extra minute compounds into missed slots and misconnected pax. AFPI exposes which handling stations, activities, and vendor combinations consistently blow the SLA and quantifies the OTP lift from fixing them. Metric: turnaround minutes and OTP15 %.

2. CASA Safety-Management & Maintenance-Release Controls

CASA CASR Part 42 puts continuing-airworthiness and maintenance-release under formal controlled processes; every defect deferral and Certificate of Release to Service must be evidenced. Compliance Center runs the defect-adjudication-to-release workflow as a controlled process with sign-offs, feeding the CASA audit trail continuously rather than as an annual scramble.
