Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Rare-Disease Patient Access & Onboarding (Chiesi Global Rare Diseases / ex-Amryt)

Since acquiring Amryt Pharma in 2023, Chiesi Global Rare Diseases runs patient-support programmes for ultra-rare conditions (Myalept, Filsuvez, Mycapssa) where time-to-therapy directly changes outcomes and every case touches enrollment, benefits investigation, prior authorisation, specialty-pharmacy dispense, adherence, and adverse-event reporting. AFPI mines the enrollment-to-first-dose event log across Health Cloud, specialty-pharmacy interfaces, and PSP call-centre queues to expose which prior-auth denial patterns, benefits-verification loops, or hub handoffs are stretching cycle time. Feeding that map into the live Chiesi Agentforce pilot lets an agent triage the exact steps proven to slip. Metric moved: median time-to-first-dose, prior-auth turnaround, and PSP abandonment rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Health Cloud (Amryt org), Service Cloud Call Center

Key Contact: Giacomo Chiesi (Head of Chiesi Global Rare Diseases)

2. Regulatory Submission & Response Cycle Time (R&D Regulatory)

Chiesi’s active push to compress regulatory submission and health-authority response handling turns every EMA/FDA question, CTA amendment, labelling update, and IDMP data change into a cascade across Regulatory Affairs, Medical Writing, PV, and Legal. Today those handoffs live in email, Veeva Vault, and shared drives with variants no one has ever mapped. AFPI mines the submission-and-response event log to reveal which question types, sites, or product families cause the most rework, so Agentforce can pre-triage them. Metric moved: mean submission cycle time, response-turnaround SLA compliance, and rework loops per dossier.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Veeva Vault RIM (TBD), Life Sciences Cloud R&D Regulatory workflow

Key Contact: Enrico Piccinini (Head of Global Regulatory Affairs, Chiesi)

3. Tibco to NTT Integration Cutover & Post-Cutover Process Stability

The open $394k Integrations opp and $262k Tibco Replacement Big Bet Wave 2 are replacing Tibco with an NTT-delivered integration platform — the kind of programme that either lands with hidden fallout (silently-dropped events, mis-mapped variants, duplicate cases) or with a defensible baseline the business trusts. AFPI mines the “as-is” event flow across Tibco endpoints today and re-mines the “to-be” flow on NTT after cutover, giving the CIO office an objective before/after conformance and throughput view rather than a red/green dashboard. Metric moved: message-drop rate, end-to-end order/patient/complaint latency, and post-cutover incident volume.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Tibco (in flight), NTT integration platform

Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. HCP Engagement & MSL Response Desktop (30+ Country Affiliates)

Across Chiesi’s 30+ country affiliates, reps and MSLs flip between Life Sciences Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud Call Center for medical-information intake, and email/attachment tools to close a single HCP conversation — so one high-value doctor is fragmented across four screens. Task mining the desktop reveals how much rep time is lost to app-switching, duplicate outreach, and manual routing of low-risk medical-info questions that Agentforce could handle straight-through. Feeds the live Chiesi Agentforce opp with a costed automation backlog. Metric moved: medical-info response SLA, MSL follow-up conversion, and rep-touch minutes per HCP by country.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Life Sciences Cloud console, Service Cloud Call Center

Key Contact: Alessandro Chiesi (Head of Europe Region, Chiesi Farmaceutici)

2. Rare-Disease PSP Case Handler Desktop (Amryt / Global Rare Diseases)

PSP case handlers supporting ultra-rare therapies swivel between Health Cloud, specialty-pharmacy portals, benefits-investigation tools, e-fax/attachments, and email for every enrollment — and repeat that swivel for adherence check-ins and adverse-event capture. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. GxP Complaint Handling & CAPA Controls

Chiesi’s respiratory portfolio (Foster, Trimbow) and rare-disease portfolio generate a steady stream of product complaints and CAPA cases that must be logged, investigated, categorised, and closed within GxP-defined windows — missed windows drive Form 483 exposure and warning-letter risk. Compliance Center runs complaint-to-closure and CAPA-to-effectiveness-check as a controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and always-on audit evidence, and flags the exact investigations breaching cycle-time thresholds. Metric moved: complaint-cycle SLA compliance, CAPA closure-on-time rate, and repeat-defect rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Veeva Vault QMS (TBD), Service Cloud Call Center

Key Contact: Alessandro Chiesi (Head of Europe Region, Chiesi Farmaceutici)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Pharmacovigilance / Adverse-Event Reporting SLA Controls

EMA and FDA pharmacovigilance reporting windows for serious adverse events (7-day for fatal/life-threatening, 15-day for other serious) are absolute and audited — a single missed submission cascades into inspection findings. Compliance Center codifies the AE intake, medical assessment, MedDRA coding, and expedited/periodic submission workflow with enforced SLAs and continuous evidence, so the PV team and QA can prove compliance to inspectors rather than reconstruct it. Metric moved: AE case-processing SLA hit-rate, ICSR submission on-time percentage, and inspection-ready mean-time-to-evidence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Veeva Vault Safety (TBD), Service Cloud Call Center

Key Contact: Enrico Piccinini (Head of Global Regulatory Affairs, Chiesi)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. EU AI Act & GxP Model Lifecycle Controls (Agentforce Governance)

The EU AI Act — and GxP by extension — puts every AI/ML and Agentforce model Chiesi deploys (R&D Regulatory triage, Rare Disease PSP, HCP medical-info) under formal lifecycle controls with documented validation, monitoring, and retirement evidence. Compliance Center codifies the promote/monitor/retire process across the Agentforce and Data Cloud estate and produces continuous evidence for auditors, which becomes critical as Agentforce lands in patient-facing and regulatory-facing flows. Metric moved: audit-ready models covered, mean-time-to-evidence, and validation-cycle time per model release.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Agentforce / Data Cloud, model registry
