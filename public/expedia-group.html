Mining the event log across Salesforce Service Cloud, the supplier API layer, and payments will surface the true rework loops, partner-side bottlenecks, and refund-aging buckets that consume agent time. 2. Partner & Supply Onboarding (Property/Air)

Onboarding a new hotel, alternative-accommodation host, or air partner runs through content ingestion, rate/inventory mapping, contracting, and compliance checks — typically weeks of back-and-forth email plus custom internal tooling. Process mining exposes where properties stall in the funnel (missing content, tax setup, geo tagging) and how long time-to-first-booking really takes vs. the internal SLA. AFPI plus Agentforce can auto-nudge partners at the exact stall points and auto-approve low-risk paths. Metric moved: partner time-to-live and inventory ramp velocity.

3. Traveler Support Contact-Deflection Loop

Millions of calls and chats hit Service Cloud each year with predictable patterns — itinerary changes, refund status, price-drop disputes, loyalty issues — but true root causes hide inside the process, not the transcript. Mining the case journey (self-service → virtual agent → human) shows which policies and system handoffs actually cause repeat contacts. AFPI feeds Agentforce Service Agents the deflection blueprint per case type; wedge is Agentforce Ops co-sell attached to the two live MCA opps. Metric moved: contained rate and cost-to-serve per booking.
