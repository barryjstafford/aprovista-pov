Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Claims Adjudication & Leakage Reduction (Adcubum Syrius)

Helsana processes millions of KVG basic and VVG supplementary claims per year through Adcubum Syrius, with routing, medical review, and payout sub-processes riddled with rework loops and manual exceptions. On CHF 7B in premiums, even a 2-3% leakage line is material. Apromore mines auto-adjudication rates, exception paths, and adjudicator variance so RPA and Agentforce automation are pointed at the steps that actually pay back. Metric moved: auto-adjudication rate, cost-per-claim, provider-payout DSO.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Adcubum Syrius, Microsoft Dynamics 365 CRM

3. Premium Billing, Collections & KVG Art. 64a Dunning

Monthly premium billing across ~2M insured lives produces millions of billing events, chase letters, payment plans, and cantonal-authority hand-offs for unpaid premiums under KVG Article 64a. Manual dunning cadences and rework erode margin and increase bad-debt exposure. Apromore mines the actual dunning path variants, isolates the segments where a payment plan works vs. cantonal referral, and lets an Agentforce agent take conformant next-best action. Metric moved: DSO, bad-debt write-offs, cost-per-dun.

2. nFADP Data-Subject Access & Consent Lifecycle

Under the new Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP, in force since Sept 2023), Swiss health insurers must respond to data-subject access, correction, and erasure requests inside strict SLAs, with proof of controls across every system holding member data. Compliance Center runs the DSAR/consent lifecycle as a controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties, evidence, and lineage across Adcubum + both MSD tenants.
