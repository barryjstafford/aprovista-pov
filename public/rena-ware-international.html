Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Service Center Case-to-Resolution Optimization

The 114-seat Call Center resolves distributor and consumer cases across the U.S. and Latin American markets, and it is the highest-friction workflow in the business. Handle times, escalation loops, and Voice-to-Case handoffs directly feed the in-flight $107k Agentforce for Service opp — but without a baseline, the AI Coworker business case is hand-waving. Mining Service Cloud + Service Cloud Voice event logs surfaces where automations will move the needle before deployment and quantifies expected AHT and FCR lift. Metric moved: average handle time, first-call resolution, escalation rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, Service Cloud Voice

Key Contact: Bob Petersen (Service Center Manager)

2. Cross-Border Order-to-Cash for U.S. + LATAM Markets

Rena Ware de Colombia, Peru, and Mexico execute order-to-cash on regionally distinct payment methods, tax regimes, and delivery windows — a classic multi-country O2C variance problem. Mining the VTEX order stream against Salesforce service events reveals where each country's happy-path deviates and where currency, tax, or logistics rework hits. Delivers the numbers a $50M direct-sales business needs to justify an AFPI subscription on top of the Agentforce co-sell. Metric moved: order-to-cash cycle time by country, exception rate, days-sales-outstanding.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: VTEX, Salesforce

3. Distributor Onboarding & Time-to-First-Order

Rena-Ware runs an 80+ year direct-sales model where new distributors must be recruited, trained, credentialled, and shipped their starter kits across multiple countries. Every day between application and first commissioned order is lost revenue and cohort attrition. Mining the SFDC lead / partner community lifecycle plus the VTEX first-order event exposes drop-off between application, training completion, and first sale — ideal Agentforce Coworker automation candidates. Metric moved: distributor time-to-first-order, 30/60/90-day cohort retention.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce (Partner Community, Sales Cloud), VTEX

2. Distributor Commission & Payout Reconciliation Desktop

Direct-sales commissions are the beating heart of the business — late, disputed, or reworked payouts drive distributor churn faster than anything else. The commission workflow bridges VTEX orders, Salesforce partner data, and a back-office finance system, so today it lives in spreadsheets that a small team hand-adjusts. Task mining the sales-admin desktop shows exactly where reversals, holds, and manual overrides concentrate and which ones are safe to automate. Metric moved: commission dispute rate, days-to-payout, manual rework hours per cycle.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales-admin spreadsheets, Salesforce partner UI

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. Multi-Country Sales Tax & VAT Compliance

Selling into the U.S., Colombia, Peru, and Mexico means running four distinct tax regimes on every VTEX order — U.S. state sales tax, Colombian IVA, Peruvian IGV, and Mexican IVA/CFDI e-invoicing. Compliance Center codifies the tax-calc, invoice-issuance, and evidence-retention controls per country and produces continuous audit trails when SAT, DIAN, or SUNAT ask. Metric moved: e-invoicing exception rate, mean-time-to-audit-evidence per country.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: VTEX order/invoice engine, tax-calc service

Key Contact: Jeff Roberts (VP Operations)

2. Distributor Data Privacy (CCPA + LATAM Privacy Regimes)

The direct-sales model captures personal data on thousands of distributors and end consumers across the U.S. (CCPA/CPRA), Colombia (Ley 1581), Mexico (LFPDPPP), and Peru (Ley 29733). Compliance Center runs consent capture, subject-rights fulfillment, and retention-schedule controls as one auditable workflow across Salesforce and VTEX rather than in country-specific spreadsheets. Metric moved: DSAR response cycle time, consent-record coverage, retention-purge exception count.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce (Partner Community, Service Cloud), VTEX
