Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Capex Investment & Engineering-Design Cycle

Capexforce — jointly built with Salesforce over 4 waves — coordinates Italgas's full capital investment lifecycle (Investments, Engineering Design, Permitting, Tendering & Contracting, Execution) [Slack]. After three years in production, mining these flows exposes where approvals stall, which permit types blow duration SLAs, and where document rework cascades across the design→tender→execute hand-off. Directly moves capital deployment velocity — the KPI Marco Barra Caracciolo built the deal on. Metric: median approval-to-execution cycle time and rework loops per Capex project.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Capexforce, SAP

Key Contact: Gianmarco Pulga (Head of Digital Factory at Italgas)

2. Priority Service Requests (Methane Leak Response)

Italgas Reti runs a highly-regulated priority service request process — dispatch for suspected methane leaks — with strict response-time SLAs enforced by ARERA. This is the org so mission-critical they refused Hyperforce migration until Equinix SEC could be preserved [Slack]. Mining end-to-end (call intake → work order → field arrival → containment → close) shows which regions and shifts miss regulatory clocks and why. Metric: % of priority interventions inside the mandated response window.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, SAP field-ops

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. ARERA Priority-Intervention SLA Controls

ARERA (Italy's energy regulator) mandates strict response-time SLAs on methane-leak priority interventions with financial penalties for misses. Compliance Center codifies the intake→dispatch→field-close workflow as a controlled process with continuous evidence, segregation-of-duties, and always-on audit trails for the regulator. Removes the current spreadsheet-and-attestation model for regulatory reporting. Metric: audit-ready evidence packets produced per quarter, penalty exposure avoided.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, SAP field-ops
