Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. First Notice of Loss to Claim Closure — Personal & Small Commercial Auto

The FNOL-to-closure journey moves across the digital intake channel, adjuster triage, coverage verification, damage estimation, vendor dispatch, subrogation, and payout — with state-DOI unfair claims practice clocks running the entire time. Straight-through-processing rates in P&C claims typically hide a long tail of files that cycle through reassignment and information-request loops that quietly consume the majority of adjuster capacity. AFPI mines the actual event log across the claims platform, the AI triage layer, and the payment system to expose the two or three loop patterns that drive rework, moving cycle time, LAE (loss adjustment expense) ratio, and DOI complaint volume.

2. Small Commercial Underwriting & Quote-to-Bind

The Hartford's small commercial franchise runs on speed — the winning quote is often the first one back to the independent agent — and quote-to-bind cycle time is the single biggest lever on hit-ratio. Behind the scenes the actual workflow ricochets between the agent portal, ordered third-party data (ISO, MVR, prior-carrier), the rating engine, and referrals to underwriters when a rule fires. AFPI produces the true process graph and exposes which referral rules, data-vendor latencies, or agency segments are structurally slowing the quote — the metric moved is bind rate at the target service-level, and premium per underwriter FTE.

3. Group Benefits Absence & Disability Case Management

The Hartford is a top-three US carrier in group disability, absence management, and paid family leave — a business where the "process" is a case that lives for weeks or months across intake, medical review, employer coordination, and periodic re-adjudication. State PFML mandates keep multiplying and each one has its own eligibility clock and appeal SLA. AFPI ingests the case-management event log and surfaces where cases stall against statutory clocks and which case segments generate disproportionate rework, moving benefit-decision cycle time, appeals-overturn rate, and PFML compliance exceptions.

4. Policy Servicing & Endorsement Processing (Commercial Middle Market)

Once a commercial policy binds, the real cost sits in mid-term endorsements — added vehicles, changed limits, new locations, certificate requests — that flow across the agent portal, service centers, and underwriting for anything material. Endorsement backlogs and re-work drive agent NPS almost as much as claims do, and are invisible in the policy admin system's canned dashboards. AFPI maps the full endorsement lifecycle event log, isolates the endorsement types and agency segments that consistently generate the worst cycle-time outliers, and quantifies the servicing-cost-to-premium ratio by segment.

5. SOX & Model Risk Compliance — Reserving and Actuarial Process Attestation

As an SEC-registered insurer, The Hartford operates under SOX Section 404 for financial reporting and NAIC Model Audit Rule / Model Risk Management for actuarial models — and every year the internal-audit and external-auditor teams reconstruct the actual process by which reserves, DAC, and reinsurance recoverables are calculated and reviewed.
