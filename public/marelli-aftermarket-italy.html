The e-Marelli B2B portal is Marelli Aftermarket's primary channel to independent workshops, distributors and the Checkstar network across 60+ countries — and there are two active opps upgrading it ($178k B2B upgrade + $85k Phase 2). 3. Warranty & Returns — Claims Cycle Across Distributors

Aftermarket parts (Magneti Marelli branded starters, alternators, lighting, suspension, brakes) carry warranty exposure that flows back through the distributor network as returns and credit-notes. Claim cycles that drag hurt margin twice: reserve carry cost, and distributor NPS. AFPI mines the warranty-claim event log across the call center, sales cloud case objects and SAP credit-note flow to expose why some claims sit 45+ days, which distributors are outliers, and where the AI-driven Copilot in Apromore can flag anomalies live. Metric moved: warranty cycle time, reserve accuracy, distributor satisfaction on claims.
