Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. SME Loan Origination & Credit-Decisioning Migration (nCino → FSC)

Nearly all originated loans (ex-treasury and mortgage) currently route through nCino/Salesforce, with an active $1M opp to remove nCino and re-platform on FSC. Mining the current origination process before, during, and after cutover exposes where relationship bankers actually get stuck — householding, pricing exceptions, credit committee handoffs — and gives Judo an evidence-based blueprint for the target state on FSC. Without this, the migration risks paving cow paths. Metric moved: time-to-yes on commercial loans (target 30-50% reduction), plus a 20%+ lift in first-time-right applications.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: nCino, Salesforce FSC

Key Contact: Chris Bayliss (Deputy CEO & Chief Financial Officer)

2. KYC / AML Onboarding for SME Customers & UBOs

AUSTRAC has intensified AML/CTF program reviews across Australian ADIs, and SME onboarding — with layered corporate structures and Ultimate Beneficial Owner checks — is where onboarding stalls most often. Mining the end-to-end onboarding process across FSC, identity-verification vendors, and manual analyst review reveals the concrete handoffs where cases sit idle, get re-worked, or escalate. Feeds Compliance Center directly. Metric moved: onboarding cycle time and analyst rework rate on high-risk SME cases.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce FSC, KYC/AML platform

3. Credit Approval & Exception Handling (Relationship-Banker Model)

Judo's differentiator is the relationship-banker credit model — human judgment layered onto data — but that same judgment creates non-standard paths, delegated-authority breaches, and pricing exceptions that are invisible to leadership. Mining the credit-approval workflow (including the custom household object built to work around nCino's native model) shows which exception paths are systemic vs. one-off, and where authority limits get bypassed. Becomes the control fabric for Agentforce-assisted credit ops. Metric moved: exception-rate on delegated authority and average days-in-committee.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce FSC (custom household object), nCino

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. APRA CPS 230 Operational Risk & Critical-Operations Controls

APRA CPS 230 (effective July 2025) forces every ADI to identify, control, and continuously test its "critical operations" — loan origination, payments, deposits — with mapped tolerances and third-party dependency evidence. Compliance Center runs those controls as live, auditable processes with automated evidence capture and exception-flow triggers. This is exactly the shape APRA wants under CPS 230 examinations. Metric moved: mean-time-to-evidence per critical operation and audit findings per review.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce FSC, core banking / payments

Key Contact: Yien Hong (Chief Risk Officer)
