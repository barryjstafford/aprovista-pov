1. SME Loan Origination & Credit-Decisioning Migration (nCino → FSC)

100% of Judo's originated loans (ex-treasury and mortgage) currently route through nCino/Salesforce, with an active $1M opp to remove nCino and re-platform on FSC. Mining the current origination process before, during, and after cutover exposes where relationship bankers actually get stuck — householding, pricing exceptions, credit committee handoffs — and gives Judo an evidence-based blueprint for the target-state on FSC. Without this, the migration risks paving cow paths. Metric moved: time-to-yes on commercial loans (target 30–50% reduction), plus a 20%+ lift in first-time-right applications.

2. KYC / AML Onboarding for SME Customers & UBOs

AUSTRAC has intensified AML/CTF program reviews across Australian ADIs, and SME onboarding — with layered corporate structures and Ultimate Beneficial Owner checks — is where onboarding stalls most often at Judo. Mining the end-to-end onboarding process across FSC, identity-verification vendors, and manual analyst review reveals the concrete handoffs where cases sit idle, get re-worked, or escalate. AFPI's Compliance Center wedge turns that into an auditable "prove-how-a-decision-was-made" pack for AUSTRAC. Metric moved: onboarding cycle time and analyst rework rate.

3. Dispute & Complaint Handling under RG 271

ASIC's RG 271 imposes hard 30-day resolution windows on internal dispute resolution, and boards are personally accountable for evidence of adherence. Mining Judo's complaint intake, triage, and resolution flow across FSC Service Cloud and manual back-office queues surfaces exactly which case types blow past the deadline and why. The output is a live control that regulators can inspect, not a spreadsheet reconciled after the fact. Metric moved: % of complaints resolved within RG 271 windows and reduction in remediation payouts.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce FSC Service Cloud, complaints case log

4. Term-Deposit Renewal & Retention Journey

Judo's funding book leans heavily on retail term deposits, and every basis point of rollover-attrition directly hits net-interest margin. Mining the renewal journey — from maturity notice, through digital renewal channel, to the manual save-desk in the contact centre — reveals where depositors drop off and which touchpoints actually save the deposit. AFPI feeds the process-signals into Agentforce, which can then act on at-risk depositors in real time. Metric moved: TD retention rate (target +200–400 bps) and cost-per-retained-dollar.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce FSC, deposit core system

5. Credit Approval & Exception Handling (Relationship-Banker Model)

Judo's differentiator is the relationship-banker credit model — human judgment layered onto data — but that same judgment creates non-standard paths, delegated-authority breaches, and pricing exceptions that are invisible to leadership. Mining the credit-approval workflow (including the custom household object built to work around nCino's native model) shows which exception paths are systemic vs. one-off, and where authority limits are being bypassed. This becomes the control fabric for Agentforce-assisted credit ops. Metric moved: exception-rate on delegated authority and average days-in-committee.
