Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Cross-Entity Order-to-Cash Across 6 Legal Entities

Sycomp bills the same customer through US, India, Singapore, HK, Ireland, or Brazil depending on ship-from location, and the Salesforce opp-to-invoice-to-cash chain runs through Oracle GL and back into 530 Sales Cloud seats. This creates entity-selection variance, deal-desk exceptions, and DSO drag that no dashboard exposes. Apromore mines the actual quote-to-order-to-cash path across all six entities and pinpoints where invoice cycle time and revenue leakage happen. Metric moved: quote-to-order cycle time and DSO days.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Sales Cloud, Oracle ERP

2. Onboarding Agent SOP Discovery (Priority-1 Pre-Work)
