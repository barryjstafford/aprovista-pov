Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. SAP S/4HANA Work & Asset Management (WAM) Cutover Assurance

The $3M WAM program is the highest-stakes ERP transformation CenterPoint has run in a decade — work order creation, dispatch, meter-to-cash, and asset lifecycle all move at once. Without event-log-based process discovery, cutover teams rely on interview-based TO-BE maps that miss 30-40% of real variants (emergency work orders, mutual-aid crews, storm exceptions). AFPI mines the actual SAP + legacy CIS event logs to expose every variant before go-live, cutting stabilization cost by 15-25% and moving hypercare from 12 to 6 weeks. Metric moved: hypercare defect volume and time-to-stable.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP S/4HANA, Legacy CIS

Key Contact: Jason Ryan (CIO)

2. Storm Restoration & Systemwide Resiliency Orchestration

After Hurricane Beryl, the Texas PUC and the Governor's office put CenterPoint's outage-restoration process under a microscope — average restoration time, crew dispatch, damage-assessment cycle, and stakeholder comms all became reportable metrics. AFPI mines the end-to-end restoration process across OMS, GIS, and WFM to expose bottlenecks (crew staging delays, damage-assessment rework, mutual-aid onboarding friction) and quantify what a 10% faster restoration is worth in avoided customer-hours-lost. Directly feeds the $975k Resiliency Planning RFP. Metric moved: SAIDI/SAIFI and mutual-aid mobilization time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: OMS, GIS

Key Contact: Darin Carroll (SVP, Electric Business)

3. Meter-to-Cash & Customer Move-In/Move-Out

With 7M+ metered customers across gas and electric, move-in/move-out, billing exceptions, and payment plan workflows are high-volume, high-friction, and touch Salesforce/CIS/AMI.
