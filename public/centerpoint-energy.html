Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. SAP S/4HANA Work & Asset Management (WAM) Cutover Assurance. The $3M WAM program is the highest-stakes ERP transformation CenterPoint has run in a decade — work order creation, dispatch, meter-to-cash, and asset lifecycle all move. Without event-log-based process discovery, cutover teams rely on interview-based TO-BE maps that miss 30-40% of real variants (emergency work orders, mutual-aid crews, storm exceptions). AFPI mines the actual SAP + legacy CIS event logs to expose every variant before go-live, cutting stabilization cost by 15-25% and moving hypercare from 12 to 6 weeks. Engage Jason Ryan (CIO) as executive sponsor.

2. Systemwide Resiliency & Storm Response Orchestration. After Hurricane Beryl, Texas PUC and the Governor's office put CenterPoint's outage-restoration process under a microscope — average restoration time, crew dispatch, damage-assessment cycle, and stakeholder comms all became reportable metrics. AFPI mines the end-to-end restoration process across OMS, GIS, WFM, and CIS to expose bottlenecks (crew staging delays, damage-assessment rework, mutual-aid onboarding friction) and quantify what a 10% faster restoration is worth in avoided customer-hours-lost. Metric moved: SAIDI/SAIFI, mutual-aid mobilization time. Engage Darin Carroll (SVP, Electric Business).

3. Regrello Workflow — Process Baseline Before You Automate. Two active Regrello opps ($1.5M + $700k) signal CenterPoint is standardizing cross-functional workflows — but automating a broken process just makes it break faster. AFPI runs discovery FIRST on the target workflows (permitting, vendor onboarding, capital-project intake) to map the actual paths, exception loops, and handoff friction. Regrello then automates the validated happy path, and AFPI continuously monitors conformance. Metric moved: cycle time, first-pass yield, rework rate. Champion: Amy Sedgwick (VP, Business Transformation) or the digital-transformation lead.

4. Regulatory Rate-Case & Compliance Evidence Automation. CenterPoint files continuously with PUC Texas, MPUC (Minnesota), and Indiana URC — every rate case, storm-cost recovery filing, and prudency review demands defensible evidence of operational efficiency. Compliance teams spend thousands of hours pulling logs and building narratives. AFPI's Compliance Center wedge produces continuously-updated, audit-grade process evidence for every regulated workflow (outage response, capital deployment, safety inspections), collapsing rate-case prep from months to weeks. Metric moved: rate-case cycle time, prudency approval rate. Engage Monica Karuturi (EVP & General Counsel).

5. Meter-to-Cash & Customer Move-In/Out Cycle. With 7M+ metered customers across gas and electric, move-in/move-out, billing exceptions, and payment plan workflows are high-volume, high-friction, and touch Salesforce/CIS/AMI. AFPI mines the meter-to-cash event log to quantify billing rework, days-sales-outstanding, and self-service leakage. Directly feeds the Agentic Analytics Big Bet ($576k opp) — AFPI supplies the process ground truth, Agentforce automates the top variants. Metric moved: DSO, cost-to-serve per customer, self-service containment. Champion: Kenneth Coleman (SVP, Customer Transformation) or the customer-ops lead.
