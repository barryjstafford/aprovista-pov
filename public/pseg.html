2. Appliance Services × Gas Distribution — Duplicate Work-Order Elimination

SVP Appliance Services Brian Clark confirmed ACES and MWIMS "do not communicate effectively today," producing duplicate work orders, manual handoffs, and customer-experience gaps at every cross-functional boundary. Process mining across both platforms exposes exactly where the same job is being logged twice, which handoffs are manual, and where first-time-fix is failing. This turns anecdote into an evidence-backed target list for the Agentforce Operations vision (AI agents, virtual diagnostics, video-assisted troubleshooting). Metric moved: reduced truck rolls, first-time-fix uplift, cycle-time from customer request to closed work order.

It hardens the $180k Agentforce for Service business case with hard baseline numbers. Metric moved: average handle time, after-call-work minutes, first-contact resolution.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. NERC CIP — Continuous Access & Change Evidence for Critical Infrastructure

PSEG Nuclear and PSEG Power operate assets under NERC CIP with mandatory logging, access reviews, and configuration-change control — today largely evidenced through periodic spreadsheet-driven attestations. Compliance Center runs access provisioning, config change, and privileged-user reviews as a controlled workflow and produces continuous, auditor-ready evidence. Cuts prep time for each NERC CIP audit cycle and reduces the risk of self-reports. Metric moved: audit-ready control coverage, mean-time-to-evidence, and self-reported findings.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Active Directory / IAM logs, ServiceNow change management

Key Contact: Head of NERC CIP Compliance (PSEG Power)
