Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Complaint Handling & Remediation Conformance (ASIC RG 271)

Under ASIC RG 271 CBA must acknowledge complaints in 24 hours and resolve in 30 days — with an internal dispute-resolution audit trail. Today the process spans multiple contact channels, Pega decisioning, and manual case-handler triage, and breaches drive both AFCA escalations and reputational cost. Apromore mines the case lifecycle across Marketing Cloud → Pega → core banking to expose where SLA breaches originate (queue routing, missing evidence, credit-decision handoffs) and quantify rework. Metric moved: reduce >30-day breach rate and cut AFCA escalation volume.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Pega, Salesforce Marketing Cloud

2. Financial-Hardship Assistance End-to-End Flow

Post-COVID and through the 2024-26 cost-of-living cycle, hardship-assistance volumes at CBA have stayed structurally elevated and every step is regulator-watched. The process today mixes digital self-service, call-centre triage, credit assessment, and repayment restructuring — with multiple hand-offs where customers drop out or miss deadlines. Apromore mines the actual hardship journey to find bottleneck touchpoints, no-response drop-offs, and manual credit re-work, feeding Agentforce Operations agents the right high-value automation targets. Metric moved: reduce hardship time-to-decision and increase successful arrangement completion.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Pega, CommSee

3. Mortgage Origination & Approval Cycle Time

Home lending is CBA's largest earnings engine and broker-channel time-to-yes is a competitive weapon — a day of cycle-time is worth material market share against Macquarie and NAB. The origination flow spans broker portals, credit decisioning, valuations, verification-of-income and documentation with heavy conditional-approval rework. Apromore mines each application's actual event log to find rework loops (repeated doc requests, credit re-runs), broker-versus-branch variance, and where AI agents can automate verification. Metric moved: reduce median time-to-unconditional-approval and lift straight-through-processing rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: CommSee, Pega

4. AUSTRAC AML/CTF Transaction-Monitoring Alert Triage

CBA's 2018 AUSTRAC settlement (AUD $700M) permanently reshaped financial-crime scrutiny — every alert-triage decision is now audited. Analysts today juggle SAS/Pega alert queues, manual SME escalation, and lengthy suspicious-matter-report drafting. Apromore mines the alert-to-SMR lifecycle to find where analysts loop, where dispositions vary unfairly between teams, and where AI-assisted triage cuts total-review-time without weakening controls. Metric moved: reduce mean alert-review time and improve conformance to the internal FinCrime playbook.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAS, Pega
