Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Complaint Handling & Remediation Conformance (ASIC RG 271)

Under ASIC RG 271, CBA must acknowledge complaints in 24 hours and resolve in 30 days with a defensible internal-dispute-resolution trail. Today the flow spans Marketing Cloud comms, Pega decisioning, Service Cloud cases, and manual handler triage — and breaches drive AFCA escalations and reputational cost. Apromore mines the case lifecycle across Service Cloud, Pega, and CommSee to expose where SLA breaches originate (routing, missing evidence, credit-decision handoffs) and quantify rework. Metric: reduce >30-day breach rate and cut AFCA escalation volume.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Pega

Key Contact: Angus Sullivan (Group Executive, Retail Banking Services)

2. Financial-Hardship Assistance End-to-End

Through the 2024-26 cost-of-living cycle, hardship-assistance volumes at CBA have stayed structurally elevated and every step is regulator-watched. The flow mixes digital self-service, contact-centre triage, credit assessment, and repayment restructuring with multiple hand-offs where customers drop out or miss deadlines. Apromore mines the actual hardship journey to find bottleneck touchpoints, no-response drop-offs, and manual credit re-work — then hands Agentforce Operations a ranked automation target list. Metric: reduce hardship time-to-decision and lift arrangement completion rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Pega, CommSee

Key Contact: Rodrigo Castillo (EGM, Everyday Banking & Digital)

3. Mortgage Origination & Approval Cycle Time

Home lending is CBA's largest earnings engine and broker-channel time-to-yes is a competitive weapon — a day of cycle-time is material market share against Macquarie and NAB. The origination flow spans broker portals, credit decisioning, valuations, verification-of-income, and documentation with heavy conditional-approval rework. Apromore mines each application's actual event log to find rework loops (repeated doc requests, credit re-runs), broker-versus-branch variance, and where AI agents can automate verification. Metric: reduce median time-to-unconditional-approval and lift straight-through-processing rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: CommSee, Pega

Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. Contact-Centre Agent Desktop Swivel-Chair

CBA's retail contact centres run agents across Service Cloud, CommSee, Pega, and multiple product-specific screens for a single call — a well-known driver of AHT and post-call wrap-time variance. Task mining captures each agent's on-screen actions to isolate the after-call-work steps and the tabs that eat wrap time. Findings feed directly into the "AI factory" backlog and pair with Agentforce for Service. Metric: reduce AHT and after-call-work minutes per contact type.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud console, CommSee

2. Financial-Crime Analyst Alert-Triage Desktop

Post-2018 AUSTRAC settlement, CBA's financial-crime analysts adjudicate high alert volumes across SAS, Pega, and internal knowledge tools — today largely a manual, screen-flipping activity. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. APRA CPS 230 Operational-Resilience Controls

APRA CPS 230 requires CBA to identify critical operations, set tolerance levels, and continuously evidence that controls are working across material service providers. Compliance Center codifies the CPS 230 process map (identify → test → attest → report) and produces always-on evidence rather than point-in-time attestation. It plugs directly into the process-mining use cases above so a breach in complaint or hardship cycle time becomes a live CPS 230 signal. Metric: mean-time-to-evidence per critical operation and audit findings per business unit.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Pega

Key Contact: Nigel Williams (Group Chief Risk Officer)
