2. Hardship & NCCP Compliance Cycle

ASIC's tightened hardship-response rules under NCCP require documented, timely handling of every customer hardship request — with regulator visibility into how variations were assessed. Manual audits sample thin; process mining gives every case a compliant trace. AFPI reconstructs the full hardship journey from intake through variation and re-contact, flagging cases that miss statutory clocks. Metric moved: hardship SLA breach rate, target <2%.

3. Consumer Lending Origination-to-Fund
