Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Service Activation & Installation Cycle Compression (RSP-facing)

NBN Co orchestrates activation for every retail service provider (Telstra, Optus, TPG, Aussie Broadband, etc.) across FTTP, FTTN, HFC, and Fixed Wireless — a hand-off-heavy process where each rework loop directly hits SLAs, RSP satisfaction, and TIO complaint volume. Mining Order → Design → Field-Job → Activation → Confirmation across Service Cloud and the OSS/BSS event stream will expose where truck-rolls repeat, where "appointment missed" and "premises not ready" traces diverge from happy-path, and which RSP+technology combinations burn the most cycle time. AFPI can baseline this in weeks and give the COO the evidence pack the Agentforce activation-agent business case needs. Target metric: cut mean activation cycle by 20% and lift right-first-time by 8 points.

2. Fault-to-Resolve & Assurance (Trouble-Ticket Flow)

Fault handling on the wholesale network is the single most visible NBN process — every unresolved incident ends up in TIO complaint data and government dashboards.
