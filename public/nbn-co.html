Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Service Activation & Installation Cycle Compression (RSP-facing)

NBN Co orchestrates activation for every retail service provider — Telstra, Optus, TPG, Aussie Broadband — across FTTP, FTTN, HFC, and Fixed Wireless. It is a hand-off-heavy process where every rework loop hits SLAs, RSP satisfaction, and TIO complaint volume. Mining Order → Design → Field-Job → Activation → Confirmation across Service Cloud and the OSS/BSS event stream exposes where truck-rolls repeat and which RSP + technology combinations burn the most cycle time. Target metric: cut mean activation cycle by 20% and lift right-first-time by 8 points.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Data Cloud

Key Contact: Ellie Sweeney (Chief Executive Officer)

2. Fault-to-Resolve & Assurance (Trouble-Ticket Flow)

Fault handling on the wholesale network is the single most visible NBN process — every unresolved incident ends up in TIO complaint data and government dashboards. Mining the trouble-ticket lifecycle (RSP-raised ticket → NBN triage → Tier 2 → field dispatch → close) across Service Cloud and Data Cloud surfaces reroute loops between Tier-1/Tier-2, misclassified "customer premises" vs "network fault" traces, and where SLA breaches actually originate. AFPI hands Assurance leadership a diagnostic no BI dashboard can — the actual process graph with variants ranked by cost. Target metric: cut MTTR by 25% and drop repeat-fault rate by 30%.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, Data Cloud

3. Wholesale Order-to-Bill (RSP Partner Portal)

NBN's B2B revenue model is entirely wholesale — every dollar comes through an RSP order placed via the Partner Portal. Mining Order-to-Bill across Experience Cloud → Sales Cloud → invoicing exposes where RSP orders stall on validation, credit checks, or CVC/AVC configuration errors, and where downstream billing exceptions cluster. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. TIO Complaint Root-Cause & Regulatory Reporting

As a federal-government-owned entity under the Telecommunications Consumer Protections Code and the TIO ombudsman regime, every escalated complaint is a political liability. Compliance Center codifies the complaint-handling flow (RSP escalation → NBN Level 1 → Level 2 → TIO referral → resolution) as a controlled workflow with segregation-of-duties, sign-offs, and always-on audit evidence. Every mined variant becomes an audit-ready control. Metric: cut TIO-referred complaints by 20% and shorten resolution cycle by 15%.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud, complaints case log

Key Contact: Chief Legal & Regulatory Officer (Compliance)

2. Security of Critical Infrastructure Act (SoCI) Controls

NBN Co is a declared Systems of National Significance asset under the Security of Critical Infrastructure Act, obliged to demonstrate risk-management, incident-response, and access-control processes to the Cyber and Infrastructure Security Centre (CISC). Compliance Center runs the risk-management program (identify → assess → mitigate → report) with codified controls, sign-offs, and evidence that answers CISC in one click rather than a spreadsheet drill. Metric: audit-ready control coverage and mean-time-to-evidence-package.
