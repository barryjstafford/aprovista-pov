Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Claims Adjudication & Payment Cycle Time

Medibank processes millions of hospital and ancillary claims annually across Medibank- and ahm-branded members. Manual exceptions, provider-side data-quality defects, and downstream reconciliation drive rework loops that stretch payment cycle time and depress member NPS. AFPI would mine the claim-to-payment event log end-to-end, isolate the top three rework variants, and route them to Agentforce for auto-triage. Metric moved: median claims cycle time (target 20-30% reduction) and straight-through-processing rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Core claims platform, Service Cloud

Key Contact: Andrew Matthews (COO)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Provider Contracting & Onboarding

New hospital and allied-health provider onboarding is a multi-stakeholder process — contract, credentialing, fee schedule, systems handshake — today spread across email, contract management, and provider portals. Bottlenecks delay time-to-first-claim and create data-quality debt that resurfaces in adjudication. Mining the process exposes which handoffs stall, which provider segments fail credentialing checks, and where SLA breaches concentrate. AFPI plus Agentforce can compress onboarding from weeks to days and pre-empt downstream claims defects.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, contract lifecycle system

Key Contact: Milosh Milisavljevic (Chief Provider Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Amplar Health Referral-to-Discharge Care Flow

Amplar Health (Medibank's in-house care arm) runs hospital-in-the-home, telehealth, and virtual care on an AWS-hosted CRM. Referral-to-care-plan-to-discharge is a multi-clinician, multi-system journey where duplicate work and delayed discharges directly hit unit economics and readmission risk. Mining the care-episode event log surfaces where clinicians re-key, where handoffs stall, and which pathways drive readmissions. Metric moved: episodes per clinician per week and 30-day readmission rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Amplar CRM (AWS), clinical EMR

Key Contact: Andrew Wilson (Amplar Health lead)

Top 3 Task Mining Use Cases

1. Call-Center Agent Desktop & After-Call Work

Medibank's Service Cloud Call Center handles claims queries, product changes, and retention calls, with measurable AHT and wrap variability. Task mining captures each agent's on-screen actions to isolate after-call-work steps and the tabs that eat wrap time — e.g., swivelling into the core policy system to update a benefit. Pairs directly with Agentforce for Service on the existing Service Cloud footprint. Metric moved: AHT and after-call-work minutes per contact type.

Complex or exception claims land with human assessors who flip between the claims platform, provider portals, member records, and email to complete a case. Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. APRA CPS 234 — Access & Privacy Controls Assurance

Since the 2022 data breach, Medibank has been under APRA CPS 234, OAIC, and class-action-driven scrutiny of its identity, access, and privacy controls. Today's evidence is largely control-attestation, not process-behaviour. Compliance Center mines the actual access-request-to-fulfilment and privacy-request event flows against the documented control model, exposing where variants diverge from policy and where controls execute but aren't logged. Metric moved: audit-ready controls covered and mean-time-to-evidence for regulator asks.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: IAM / SSO, Event Monitoring on Salesforce

Key Contact: Chief Information Security Officer (TBD — field verify)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Privacy Act Reform — DSAR & Data-Handling Workflow

Australian Privacy Act reform tightens data-subject rights (access, correction, deletion) and shortens response windows. Medibank's DSAR handling today spans Service Cloud, core policy, and Amplar systems — hard to prove a fully compliant flow. Compliance Center runs DSAR intake through fulfilment as a controlled workflow with SoD, sign-offs, and immutable evidence. Metric moved: DSAR response SLA hit-rate and audit-defensible completeness of each request.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Service Cloud (Privacy queue), Field Audit Trail

Key Contact: Chief Privacy Officer / DPO (TBD — field verify)

3. Regulator Reporting & Board Attestation Evidence

APRA, the Private Health Insurance Ombudsman, and the Board itself expect timely, evidenced attestation on control effectiveness — especially for the post-breach remediation programme. Compliance Center codifies each attestation as a controlled process with source-of-truth evidence pulled from the mined event logs, so the same run produces both regulator-grade and Board-grade reports. Metric moved: attestation cycle time and open control-gap count between quarterly reviews.
