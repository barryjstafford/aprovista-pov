Topline POV: Apromore can help Medibank Private Limited by mining its claims-to-payment, provider onboarding, and member service processes to cut cycle time, expose the residual control gaps flagged after the 2022 cyber incident, and lift the ROI of its existing Service Cloud and Marketing Cloud footprint — all without adding a second specialist analyst layer.

Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Claims Adjudication & Payment Cycle Time

Medibank processes millions of hospital and ancillary claims annually across Medibank- and ahm-branded members. Manual exceptions, provider-side data quality, and downstream reconciliation drive rework loops that stretch payment cycle time and depress member NPS. AFPI would mine the claim-to-payment event log end-to-end, isolate the top 3 rework variants, and route them to Agentforce for auto-triage. Target metric: reduce median claims cycle time by 20–30% and lift straight-through-processing rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Core claims platform, Service Cloud

Key Contact: Andrew Matthews (COO)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
2. Provider Contracting & Onboarding

New hospital and allied-health provider onboarding is a multi-stakeholder process (contract, credentialing, fee schedule, systems handshake) that today lives across email, contract-management, and provider portals. Bottlenecks delay time-to-first-claim and create data-quality debt that shows up later in adjudication. Mining the process reveals which handoffs stall, which provider segments fail credentialing checks, and where SLA breaches concentrate. AFPI + Agentforce can compress onboarding from weeks to days and pre-empt downstream claims defects.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud, contract lifecycle system

Key Contact: Milosh Milisavljevic (Chief Provider Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
3. Member Retention & Churn-Save Journey

Australian private health insurers face persistent lapse and downgrade risk, especially at annual premium changes. Medibank's Marketing Cloud + Datorama stack already orchestrates the outbound journey, but the actual inbound retention conversation lives in Service Cloud call-center flows and self-service. Mining the full save-attempt event log — from at-risk trigger through save offer through outcome — surfaces which agent scripts, offers, and handoffs actually retain vs. accelerate churn. Metric moved: retention lift at renewal and lower cost-per-save.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud

Key Contact: Milosh Milisavljevic (Chief Customer Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
4. Post-Breach Access & Privacy Controls Assurance

Since the 2022 data breach, Medibank has been under APRA CPS 234, OAIC, and class-action-driven scrutiny of its identity, access, and privacy controls. Compliance evidence today is largely control-attestation, not process-behaviour. AFPI can mine the actual access-request-to-fulfilment and privacy-request event flows against the documented control model, exposing where variants diverge from policy and where controls execute but aren't logged. This turns a Board-level narrative into audit-grade evidence and shortens the next APRA review.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: IAM, ServiceNow (if deployed)

Key Contact: Kylie Bishop (Chief People & Sustainability Officer)

Deep Dive (coming soon)
5. Amplar Health — Care-Delivery Case & Referral Flow

Amplar Health (Medibank's in-house care arm) runs hospital-in-the-home, telehealth, and virtual care, with the AWS-hosted Amplar CRM already flagged in the active renewal. Referral-to-care-plan-to-discharge is a multi-clinician, multi-system journey where duplicate work and delayed discharges directly hit unit economics. Mining the care-episode event log surfaces where clinicians re-key, where handoffs stall, and which care pathways drive readmission risk. Metric moved: episodes per clinician per week and readmission rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Amplar CRM (AWS), clinical EMR
