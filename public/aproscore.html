AproScore

AFPI Propensity-to-Buy Rubric — 100 points, 8 categories

Every AproVista Point-of-View brief includes an AproScore: a 0-100 grade of an account's propensity to buy Agentforce Process Intelligence (AFPI). The score is composed of eight positive categories summing to 100 points, minus any triggered red-flag deductions.

Each category is scored from evidence gathered across Org62, Slack, the web, and the AproVista knowledge library — roughly 93 discrete signals in total. Every point is anchored to a source tag ([Slack], [Web], [Docs]; Org62 is the default authoritative source and is not tagged on the rendered POV).

Positive categories (100 points)

CategoryWhat it measuresMax
Buyer & Champion Fit Are target buyer titles present (COO, CFO, VP Ops, Transformation lead, Chief Data / AI Officer, etc.), and is a sponsor reachable and engaged? 20
Industry / Vertical Priority Fit against Apromore's priority verticals — Financial Services, Insurance, Utilities, Manufacturing, Life Sciences, Public Sector, Retail / CPG. Priority verticals score full; adjacent verticals score partial. 15
Process-Pain Intensity Explicit signals of bottlenecks, rework, cycle-time misses, backlogs, exception handling, or ERP-migration friction — in filings, Slack, news, or job posts. 15
Systems & Event-Log Readiness Presence of mineable systems (SAP, Oracle, ServiceNow, Salesforce) with structured event logs available or achievable. 12
Salesforce Footprint & Relationship Existing Salesforce install base, active Org62 opportunities, and strength of the account team relationship. 12
Transformation & Agentic-AI Momentum Active ERP migration, recent M&A, Agentforce initiatives, or a formal Automation / AI Center of Excellence. 12
Compliance & Risk Pressure Regulatory pressure that drives process visibility spend — SOX, KYC / AML, NERC CIP, HIPAA, GDPR, DORA, etc. 8
Commercial Timing & Deal Viability Budget ownership confirmed, deal size ≥ $200k ACV, and the timing window is open (not frozen or deferred). 6

How AproVista uses the AproScore

T1 and T2 accounts get a full "Pursue" POV — top process-intelligence use cases, champion / economic-buyer mapping, and the recommended sales motion (standalone / co-sell with Agentforce Operations / integrated with Salesforce). T3 and T4 accounts get a nurture / disqualify analysis explaining why not now, what would need to change, and any long-term signal worth monitoring.
