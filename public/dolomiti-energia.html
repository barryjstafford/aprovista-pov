Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Meter-to-Cash Billing Accuracy & Regulatory Defense

Multi-utility billing across electricity, gas, water and district heating is the highest-risk process at Dolomiti — AGCM sanctioned the company in 2023 for improper commercial practices in billing [Slack]. AFPI mines the meter-read → bill → dispute → adjustment path to expose where holds, manual re-issues, and complaint loops originate, giving the CIDO an auditable evidence base and a lever to compress dispute resolution. Directly de-risks any repeat regulator scrutiny. Metric moved: bill-rework rate and dispute cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP IS-U, Salesforce Service Cloud
