Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Meter-to-Cash Billing Accuracy & Regulatory Defense

Multi-utility billing across electricity, gas, water and district heating is the highest-risk process at Dolomiti — AGCM fined the company in 2023 for improper commercial practices in billing [Slack]. AFPI mines the meter-read → bill → dispute → adjustment path to expose where holds, manual re-issues and complaints originate, giving the CIDO an auditable evidence base and a lever to compress dispute resolution. Metric moved: bill-rework rate and dispute cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP IS-U, Salesforce Service Cloud

3. Field-Service Dispatch & Outage Response

Hydro Dolomiti Energia operates hydro assets across the Alpine grid where outage-to-restore and planned-maintenance cycle time drive both SAIDI/SAIFI and margin. AFPI mines dispatch → technician-en-route → repair → close-out to surface schedule breakage, travel waste and rework visits — building the operating case for extending Agentforce into field service. Metric moved: mean-time-to-repair and truck-roll efficiency.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Field Service, SAP PM

Key Contact: Alberto Pinter (Head of Technical & Admin Development, Hydro Dolomiti)

4. Customer Onboarding & Switch-In / Switch-Out

Italian free-market energy switching is heavily choreographed and time-boxed by ARERA rules — a single stalled step turns into a regulatory breach. AFPI mines the switch-in / switch-out variant tree to expose which supplier interactions, KYC steps, and internal handoffs cause missed SLAs, and to prove where Marketing Cloud journeys and Agentforce can safely automate. Metric moved: on-time-switch % and onboarding cycle time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Sales/Service Cloud, SAP IS-U

Key Contact: Arianna Pontalti (Operations Director)

5. ESG & Sustainability Reporting Assurance

Dolomiti is a signature "green" utility (majority hydro) with a dedicated Group ESG lead — CSRD-era reporting demands auditable, source-of-record evidence of how emissions, water and grid data actually flow from operational systems into disclosure. AFPI's Compliance Center wedge mines the reporting close cycle to surface manual bridges, late data, and re-open loops that put assurance at risk.
