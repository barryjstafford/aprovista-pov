Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Meter-to-Cash Across A2A Energia (Electricity + Gas Retail)

A2A Energia serves ~3M retail customers across electricity and gas with a MuleSoft-orchestrated flow that spans SAP IS-U, MC ExactTarget, and the Sales/Service Cloud call center. Billing exceptions, switching (fornitura) delays, and payment-plan rework are chronic — every unresolved exception drives an inbound call and a credit-note. AFPI mines the end-to-end meter-to-cash event log to quantify DSO, first-bill-right rate, and self-service leakage, feeding the Agentforce-for-Sales rollout with the real variant map. Metric moved: DSO, cost-to-serve per customer, first-call resolution.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP IS-U, MuleSoft

2. ITSM Corporate Program — Baseline Before You Automate

The $1.5M ITSM Corporate opp signals A2A is consolidating IT service management across the group — but automating a fractured incident/problem/change process just makes the friction faster. AFPI runs discovery FIRST on the ServiceNow event logs (or the target platform) to expose ticket-bounce patterns, SLA breach root causes, and cross-BU handoff delays across A2A Ambiente, Unareti, Ciclo Idrico and A2A Energia. That baseline becomes the design input for the ITSM consolidation and the KPI dashboard for hypercare. Metric moved: MTTR, first-time-fix, ticket-reopen rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: ServiceNow, SAP

3. Waste-Collection & Route Optimization (A2A Ambiente)

A2A Ambiente is the group's environmental services arm — municipal waste collection, treatment plants, and district-heating grid maintenance across Lombardy. Route deviations, missed pickups, and unplanned truck downtime drive concession-fee exposure with municipalities (Milan, Brescia, Bergamo). AFPI mines the fleet management and work-order event logs to expose route-conformance gaps, dispatch rework and asset-availability patterns — directly protecting the municipal SLAs that anchor A2A's largest concessions. Metric moved: route conformance, missed-pickup rate, cost per tonne collected.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, Fleet/Work-Order System

Key Contact: Fulvio Roncari (CEO, A2A Ambiente)

4. Grid Connections & Distribution Work Orders (Unareti)

Unareti is A2A's electricity/gas distribution DSO covering ~1M customers in Milan and Brescia — every new connection, upgrade, and unplanned outage is an ARERA-regulated event with strict SLA windows and financial penalties for miss. AFPI mines the connection-request-to-energization process across SAP and the field-service platform to expose which variants blow the ARERA SLA and why (documentation loops, contractor availability, permit delays). Same discovery underpins the storm/outage restoration process for reg reporting. Metric moved: ARERA-compliant connection %, average connection cycle time, outage restoration time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Giuseppe Mastropieri (CEO, Unareti)

5. CSRD & Net Zero Cloud — Sustainability Data Lineage as Process Evidence

A2A has an active Net Zero Cloud opp and is subject to full EU CSRD reporting starting FY26 — Scope 1/2/3 emissions, waste-diversion rates, water-loss metrics all require audit-grade lineage of how numbers were produced. AFPI's Compliance Center wedge produces continuously-updated process evidence for the emissions-data-collection workflow across the five business units, collapsing the annual CSRD assurance cycle from months to weeks and de-risking the external auditor sign-off. Directly complements Net Zero Cloud rather than competing with it. Metric moved: CSRD assurance cycle time, restatement rate, auditor query resolution time.
