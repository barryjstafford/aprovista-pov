Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. A2A Energia Meter-to-Cash (Electricity + Gas Retail)

A2A Energia serves ~3M retail customers across electricity and gas via a MuleSoft-orchestrated flow spanning SAP IS-U, MC ExactTarget, and the Sales/Service Cloud call center. Billing exceptions, switching (fornitura) delays, and payment-plan rework are chronic — every unresolved exception drives an inbound call and a credit-note. AFPI mines the end-to-end meter-to-cash event log to quantify DSO, first-bill-right rate, and self-service leakage, feeding the Agentforce-for-Sales rollout with the real variant map. Metric moved: DSO, cost-to-serve per customer, first-call resolution.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP IS-U, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Luca Moroni (CEO, A2A Energia)

2. Unareti Grid Connections & Distribution Work Orders

Unareti is A2A's electricity/gas DSO covering ~1M customers in Milan and Brescia — every connection, upgrade, and unplanned outage is an ARERA-regulated event with strict SLA windows and financial penalties for miss. AFPI mines the connection-request-to-energization process across SAP and the field-service platform to expose which variants blow the ARERA SLA and why (documentation loops, contractor availability, permit delays). The same discovery underpins outage-restoration reporting to the regulator. Metric moved: ARERA-compliant connection %, average connection cycle time, outage restoration time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, MuleSoft

Key Contact: Giuseppe Mastropieri (CEO, Unareti)

3. A2A Ambiente Waste-Collection & Route Conformance

A2A Ambiente runs municipal waste collection, treatment plants, and district-heating grid maintenance across Lombardy. Route deviations, missed pickups, and unplanned truck downtime drive concession-fee exposure with Milan, Brescia, and Bergamo. AFPI mines fleet-management and work-order event logs to expose route-conformance gaps, dispatch rework, and asset-availability patterns — directly protecting the municipal SLAs that anchor A2A's largest concessions. Metric moved: route conformance, missed-pickup rate, cost per tonne collected.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: SAP, Fleet / Work-Order Platform

Key Contact: Fulvio Roncari (CEO, A2A Ambiente)

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. CSRD Sustainability Reporting & Net Zero Cloud Lineage

A2A is subject to full EU CSRD reporting with Scope 1/2/3 emissions, waste-diversion, and water-loss metrics all requiring audit-grade lineage of how numbers were produced. Compliance Center produces continuously-updated process evidence for the emissions-data-collection workflow across the five business units, collapsing the annual CSRD assurance cycle from months to weeks and de-risking external-auditor sign-off. Complements the open Net Zero Cloud footprint rather than competing with it. Metric moved: CSRD assurance cycle time, restatement rate, auditor-query resolution time.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Net Zero Cloud, SAP

Key Contact: Andrea Salvini (Chief Sustainability Officer)

2. ARERA Regulatory Controls (Unareti + Ciclo Idrico)

ARERA (the Italian energy and water regulator) mandates specific SLAs, tariff-application accuracy, and quality-of-service metrics for both distribution (Unareti) and water (A2A Ciclo Idrico), with financial penalties and public reporting. Compliance Center codifies each ARERA control as a monitored process, produces continuous evidence for the annual regulatory data submission, and flags variants that risk penalty exposure before the submission window closes. Metric moved: ARERA control coverage, evidence collection cycle time, penalty exposure at close.
