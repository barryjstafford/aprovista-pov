1. Mopar Parts Order-to-Cash & Dealer Backorder Reduction

2. Warranty Claims Cycle-Time & Leakage Recovery

Warranty claim processing at Mopar involves dealer submission, engineering adjudication, supplier chargeback, and payment — a highly variant workflow with material $ leakage. Mining the claim event log surfaces reject-and-resubmit loops, aged claims > 45 days, and supplier recovery gaps. AFPI conformance checking against the standard claim policy targets recovery on aged inventory. Metric moved: claim adjudication cycle time + supplier chargeback recovery $.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Salesforce Service Cloud, SAP (warranty module)

Key Contact: Director, Warranty Operations (Mopar / FCA US)
