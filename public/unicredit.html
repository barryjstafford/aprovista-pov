Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Commercial Banking Loan Origination Cycle-Time Reduction

UniCredit's commercial lending journey spans credit-scoring, KYC, collateral evaluation and disbursement across multiple country cores and shared services. Manual re-work and country-specific variants blow out cycle time and stretch SME customer patience. Apromore mines the origination event log across the German + Italian instances to expose rework loops, hand-off delays and non-conforming variants — feeding a single "golden path" that AF Ops can then automate. Metric moved: origination cycle time (days) and first-time-right rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Core banking / lending platform, Salesforce SFA
