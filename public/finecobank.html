Top 5 Process Intelligence Use Cases to be Mined

1. Digital Customer Onboarding & KYC/AML Straight-Through Rate

Fineco acquires customers exclusively online, so every drop-off in ID verification, PEP screening, or MiFID suitability directly hits net new asset (NNA) growth. Today the funnel is instrumented in analytics but not process-mined end-to-end — the bank sees conversion rates but not the actual variants that stall (manual re-KYC, document rejection loops, appropriateness re-tests). AFPI mines the full onboarding event log to surface the top 3 variants blocking straight-through processing, then drives conformance against the ECB/Banca d'Italia target path. Metric: onboarding-to-first-trade cycle time and STP rate.

2. Financial Advisor (PFA) Lifecycle & Portale Consulenti Adoption

Fineco's ~3,000 personal financial advisors are the growth engine, and the active $1.25M "CRM Consulenti Finanziari" + $400k "Portale Consulenti Core" builds are re-platforming how they recruit, onboard, coach, and retain them. 3. Trading Order Exception & Post-Trade Settlement Flow

As one of Europe's largest direct brokers, Fineco routes millions of retail equity, ETF, CFD, and derivative orders daily. Every rejected order, failed settlement, or manual break-fix erodes margin and triggers client-service tickets. Mining the order-management-to-settlement event log exposes the exception patterns (venue rejects, corporate-action mismatches, FX cutoff timing) that consume Operations bandwidth. AFPI simulates the top interventions and quantifies the STP-rate lift before any change is coded. Metric: trade break rate and mean-time-to-resolve settlement exceptions.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Order Management System, Settlement / Custody platform

5. Compliance-Center Wedge: MiFID Suitability & ECB SREP Evidence

ECB direct supervision (Significant Institution status since 2021) plus MiFID II suitability, appropriateness, and PSD2 SCA rules force Fineco to prove — with evidence, not slides — that every advised trade and every payment auth traversed the compliant path. Today that evidence is stitched together from log dumps for each SREP or internal-audit cycle. Apromore's Compliance Center turns this into a continuous conformance dashboard against the declared reference model, with red-flag alerts on drift and full replay for auditors. This is the classic "pay for the audit, get the ops savings free" wedge. Metric: audit prep hours and rate of first-time-right suitability checks.
