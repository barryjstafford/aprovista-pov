Top 3 Process Mining Use Cases

1. Digital Customer Onboarding & KYC/AML Straight-Through Rate

Fineco acquires clients exclusively online, so every drop-off in ID verification, PEP screening, or MiFID suitability directly hits net new asset (NNA) growth. Today the funnel is instrumented in analytics but not process-mined end-to-end — the bank sees conversion rates but not the actual variants that stall (manual re-KYC, document rejection loops, appropriateness re-tests). AFPI mines the full onboarding event log to surface the top variants blocking straight-through processing, then drives conformance against the ECB / Banca d'Italia target path. Metric: onboarding-to-first-trade cycle time and STP rate.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud / CRM Consulenti, KYC platform

Key Contact: Fabio Milanesi (GM & Head of IT, Operations)

2. Financial Advisor (PFA) Lifecycle & Portale Consulenti Adoption

Fineco's ~3,000 personal financial advisors are the growth engine, and the active $1.25M "CRM Consulenti Finanziari" + $400k "Portale Consulenti Core" + $375k Portale PEM builds are re-platforming how the bank recruits, onboards, coaches, and retains them. Process mining the advisor recruitment funnel, portal adoption events, and book-transfer workflow reveals which regional Area Managers are stuck in exception handling and where the new portal actually saves time versus the old one. This turns the Sales Cloud rebuild from a rip-and-replace into a measurable value story. Metric: advisor time-to-productivity and portal DAU per feature.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Sales Cloud / CRM Consulenti, Portale Consulenti

3. Trading Order Exception & Post-Trade Settlement Flow

As one of Europe's largest direct brokers, Fineco routes millions of retail equity, ETF, CFD, and derivative orders daily. Every rejected order, failed settlement, or manual break-fix erodes margin and triggers client-service tickets. Mining the order-management-to-settlement event log exposes the exception patterns (venue rejects, corporate-action mismatches, FX cutoff timing) that consume Operations bandwidth, and AFPI simulates the top interventions before any change is coded. Metric: trade break rate and mean-time-to-resolve settlement exceptions.

Top 3 Compliance Center Use Cases

1. MiFID II Suitability & Appropriateness Continuous Conformance

MiFID II requires Fineco to prove that every advised trade traversed a compliant suitability + appropriateness path, and today that evidence is stitched together from log dumps at each audit or internal-review cycle. Compliance Center codifies the reference model and continuously conforms live executions against it, red-flagging drift the day it happens instead of at the next review. This is the classic "pay for the audit, get the ops savings free" wedge. Metric: rate of first-time-right suitability checks and audit-prep hours per cycle.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Trading platform, CRM Consulenti

Key Contact: Fabio Milanesi (GM & Head of IT, Operations)

2. ECB SREP & AML/KYC Evidence-on-Demand

ECB direct supervision (Significant Institution status since 2021) plus Banca d'Italia AML expectations force Fineco to demonstrate — with evidence, not slides — that KYC refresh, PEP screening, and periodic re-review actually happened on every in-scope client. Compliance Center turns that into a continuous conformance dashboard against the declared reference model, with full replay for JST examiners and drift alerts before findings escalate. Metric: KYC / periodic-review SLA hit-rate and mean-time-to-evidence.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: KYC / AML platform, case management

3. PSD2 SCA & GDPR Consent Traceability

PSD2 Strong Customer Authentication and GDPR consent management both demand step-level traceability: which auth factor fired, which exemption was used, which consent was in force at the moment of processing. Today Fineco can produce this from logs on demand, but not continuously and not against a declared model. Compliance Center defines the SCA + consent reference model and runs live auth / consent events against it, so anomalies (silent exemption drift, expired-consent processing) are caught in-flight. Metric: SCA exemption-abuse rate and unresolved consent gaps per month.

Key System(s) to be Process Mined: Payment / auth platform, consent management
